## Grooming — the shaved head (his signature look)

- **Shaved head, by choice, now a brand element (self-narrated, 2018-04-07).** 🗣️ Neil says he
  began **losing his hair around age 18**, used **Rogaine and Propecia for ~6–7 years**, then
  stopped after a **doctor-uncle warned of a Propecia cancer risk** and his parents pushed him to
  quit. He abandoned a **~$3,000/month glued-on hair system** and **shaved his head permanently** —
  framing it as the **low-maintenance** choice that also became a recognizable part of his personal
  brand. ([[2018-04-07-yt-zoqhUhDyE9w]])
