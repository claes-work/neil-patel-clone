# How to Launch a Product | (Neil Patel's Insider Marketing Secrets)

_Q&A Thursday; **Adam Lodolce** (viewership.com) hosts/asks (question from viewer **Deepak**),
Neil answers, using Adam's **Viewership Academy** as the worked example. Only Neil-attributed
material is persona data._

## Summary
Neil's end-to-end product-launch funnel. **Stack**: **ClickFunnels** on the front end to sell
(optimize the funnel) and **Kajabi** on the back end to deliver a course and drip content over
time. For a higher-ticket product (~**$500**) short video/text won't convert — run a
**webinar** (he uses **WebinarJam**). **Funnel flow**: a quick ClickFunnels intro video (fine
to shoot on iPhone) → register for the webinar → a **45–60 min PowerPoint**, where the **first
30 min give away real tactical education for free**, then pitch a **faster/simpler "hacks"
path** (results in ~3 months vs 7–8), tease (don't reveal) the advanced strategies, and make a
**genuine special offer** (be honest about what you'd really charge for 1:1) → checkout on
ClickFunnels → Kajabi login/delivery. **Evergreen** via **EverWebinar** (converts less than
live but still well). **Traffic**: **Facebook ads convert better than YouTube ads** for this;
he name-drops media buyer **Jason Hornung** (~**$10K/month flat** fee, no referral incentive
for Neil). **Affiliates/JV**: give bloggers **30–50% per sale** and run **joint webinars** —
he cites **ConvertKit** (public revenue **>$10M/yr**, mostly affiliate/launch-driven via
partners like **Pat Flynn / Smart Passive Income**). Wrap with a **7-day educational email
sequence**. **Cart-abandonment fix**: ~**60–70%** who reach checkout don't buy — hit them with
a **YouTube + Facebook remarketing** video that runs the **opposite** pitch (emotional front →
logical remarketing, or vice versa) and send them back to checkout; Neil says this becomes the
**highest-converting campaign**.

## Key claims
As of 2018-03-08, Neil states (some names are caption-uncertain — see note):
- **Launch stack**: **ClickFunnels** front end (sell/optimize) + **Kajabi** back end (deliver
  course, drip content). Kajabi can technically do both, but he prefers this split.
- **Higher-ticket (~$500) products need a webinar**, not a short video/text — he uses
  **WebinarJam**.
- **Webinar structure**: ClickFunnels intro video (iPhone is fine) → register → **45–60 min**
  PowerPoint; **first ~30 min = free tactical education**, then pitch a faster/simpler path
  ("results in 3 months vs 7–8"), tease advanced strategies without revealing secrets, then a
  **genuine special offer** (don't inflate the "normally I'd charge five figures" claim).
- **Evergreen** with **EverWebinar** (an add-on to WebinarJam): converts **less than live but
  still well**.
- **Facebook ads convert better than YouTube ads** for launches. Media buyer **Jason Hornung**
  charges a **flat ~$10K/month**; Neil says he has **no referral incentive** — Hornung just
  produced better results than most (past clients cited include Neil, Tim Sykes, Frank Kern,
  Mike Dillard, Jermaine Griggs).
- **Affiliate/JV launches**: offer bloggers **30–50% of each sale** and run **joint webinars**.
  **ConvertKit** does **>$10M/yr** (public figures), mostly from affiliates/launches via
  partners like **Pat Flynn (Smart Passive Income)**.
- **Support with a 7-day email sequence** continually educating and showing what buyers get.
- **Cart-abandonment recovery**: **~60–70%** of people who reach checkout don't buy — remarket
  them on **YouTube + Facebook** with a video using the **opposite** pitch style (if the front
  end was emotional, go logical, and vice versa) and send them back to checkout; this becomes
  the **highest-converting campaign**.
