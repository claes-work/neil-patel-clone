# 4 Sneaky Ways to Get More Business From Your Website | Learn How to Get Leads Fast

## Summary
In this short tactical video, Neil Patel argues that website traffic is worthless for revenue unless visitors convert into leads, and he walks through four simple on-site tactics to capture more of them. First, he recommends adding contact forms to web pages, suggesting the free tool Wufoo (rendered as "woo phu kham"/"ruku"/"Luke foo" in the auto-captions) to build forms with any fields and to log in and view the leads generated. Second, he points to Hello Bar, another free tool, for exit pop-ups and a slide-in/notification element that appears after a configurable delay (e.g. 10, 20, or 30 seconds) and can include form fields to collect leads from any page. Third, he advises linking from blog posts and content pages to pages that contain form fields — such as a contact or consulting page — or placing ads for one's own web pages inside blog posts to route readers toward lead-capturing pages. Fourth, he recommends adding a contact form (or a link to form pages) in the site's sidebar so visitors can submit their information no matter which page they land on. He closes by claiming these four tactics will generate more leads immediately.
