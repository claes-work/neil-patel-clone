# Watch This If Your Paid Ads Aren't Generating Any Sales

## Summary

Neil diagnoses **paid ads that spend money but generate zero sales**, stressing that the
problem is often not the ads themselves. Six fixes: (1) **fix the landing page** — use Crazy
Egg heatmaps to see where people click and get stuck, then tweak (many ad problems are
landing-page problems); (2) **fix targeting** — copy matters less if you target the wrong
people, so use **remarketing** (higher conversion) and **lookalike audiences** on Google/
Facebook to reach people like your existing buyers; (3) **test copy in both ads and landing
pages** with **message match** (if someone searches "MacBook Air," that keyword should appear
on the landing page for a cohesive message); (4) **survey your audience** (SurveyMonkey) to
learn why non-buyers don't buy — analytics and Crazy Egg don't tell you everything; (5) try
**dayparting** — in sectors like B2B where customers buy 8am–5pm, don't waste budget showing
ads at 7pm; (6) **target the right regions** (a Beverly Hills plastic surgeon shouldn't
advertise in Florida).
