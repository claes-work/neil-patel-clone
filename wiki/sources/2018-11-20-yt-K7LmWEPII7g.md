## Summary

A rapid landing-page-optimization talk built around **7 conversion hacks** (plus "basic" fixes:
strip nav/footer options to one CTA, CTAs in every section incl. inside blog content, larger buy
buttons, currency/locale-correct fields, mobile readability):

1. **Engage visitors first (quiz them).** Personalized quizzes before the opt-in beat cold lead
   forms — a nutrition quiz **+108%**, a Hello Bar "enter your URL first" flow **+52.11%** signups.
   Rationale (his "will you marry me?" analogy): people say no to strangers; sunk-cost engagement
   makes them complete the form. Tool: **LeadQuizzes** (~<$100/mo).
2. **Don't use a plain exit pop-up — use a GIF background** showing what they get (email-collection
   rate jumped). **Mobile sliders** ("how rich do you want to be?" dollar-sign slider) drove ~16% of
   leads; apply the slider idea to wrinkle cream, debt, etc.
3. **Use GeoIP in copy.** Location-matched copy (dating-site "1.6M singles in Bangkok"; "millionaire
   trader in Las Vegas"; Expedia auto-populating your city) lifted sales **~12.49%–27.57%** across
   5 pages.
4. **Add a checkout bump.** Upsells are easier than new sales; keep the bump **≤50% of the main
   price** (he likes ~$30 on a $100 product), one-sentence benefit, speed/automation angle.
5. **Make it *harder* to buy (qualification friction).** Instant Checkmate's roadblocks/waits, and
   quiz-gating skincare ("do you qualify?") — working for it raises conversions. Tested on 5 sites:
   one **-34.63%**, four up to **+58.86%**.
6. **Copy tweaks have the biggest impact — headline first.** ~**8/10 read the headline, only 2/10
   read the rest.** Rules: numbers + negative/curiosity words, headline matches content, **odd
   numbers** beat even, **six-word** headlines, avoid multi-meaning words, powers/adjectives,
   conversational "you/I," keep it clear/dark/readable, **AMP** for mobile speed, answer objections
   (tool: Qualaroo → "why should we pay you over competitors?" **+14%**).
7. **Ask customers/visitors for a favor.** A "**I need a favor, can you help?**" email offering a
   free sample in exchange for a share drove referral shares → **+6.41%** sales (traffic barely
   moves at his volume, but referred visitors convert well).

**On-stage Q&A (Neil):** near-term landing-page gains come from **VR** and **data-driven
personalization** (GeoIP), not "AI" yet, and note **EU privacy law changes** coming in ~3-4 months;
his highest-converting affiliate trick is **retargeting a video to button-clickers for 7 days**;
best landing-page tools **LeadPages / ClickFunnels** (custom = hire Upwork devs); **push
notifications** beat Messenger for drip; **focus on LTV, not turn-and-burn.** On **SaaS**: ~**4-5%**
free-to-paid (8-9% if great), but SaaS wins long-term because **recurring revenue sells for 6-10x
yearly revenue at breakeven** (vs a few-x profit for other businesses) — PE buyers lever bank debt
to acquire. His **neilpatel.com non-paid email list ~550k active, 30%+ open rate** (high because
"I don't sell to my list"). For **$2k+ offers**, **webinars + retargeting** beat content marketing.
