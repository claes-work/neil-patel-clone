# My Instagram Strategy at 0 Followers VS. 290,000 Followers

## Summary
A first-person case study of growing his own Instagram from near-zero to 290,000
followers in roughly two years (starting 2019), self-attributed almost entirely to one
tactic: live-interview partnerships with other creators/influencers, rather than
content-type or posting-volume optimization per se. Describes an early testing phase
(video, carousels, images, personal photos, stories, cross-promoted YouTube/Facebook/
LinkedIn content, blog promotion) before identifying that partnerships outperformed
everything else and doubling down on them exclusively. Recommends: (1) do live
interviews with peers regardless of follower count (works from 500 to 50,000+
followers), aiming for roughly weekly cadence; (2) as your audience grows, seek to be
interviewed as a guest on larger regional accounts in adjacent niches
(entrepreneurship, design, fashion); (3) at higher follower counts, ask interview
guests to reciprocate with a story post about the interview. Gives a personal
outreach-conversion rate: roughly 4 out of every 50 people he approaches say yes (an
~8% hit rate), framing it as a numbers game requiring volume outreach (~100/week).

## Key claims
- Self-attributes the majority of his own Instagram growth (near-zero to 290,000 over
  ~2 years starting 2019) specifically to live-interview partnerships with other
  creators, more than content type, quality, or posting frequency (self-reported, dated
  2022-02-24) — social-media. Concrete, quantified first-party growth-tactic claim.
- Personal outreach conversion rate: approximately 4 out of every 50 people he
  personally hits up for interview partnerships say yes (~8%), and he recommends
  volume outreach of ~100 people/week to compensate for a lower hit rate than his own
  (self-reported, dated 2022-02-24) — social-media, agency-entrepreneurship.
- Explicit growth-tactic sequencing by follower-count stage: at low follower counts, do
  live interviews with similarly-sized accounts weekly; once past roughly 15,000-20,000
  followers, pivot to seeking placement as a guest on larger accounts and asking those
  hosts for reciprocal story shoutouts (self-reported framework, dated 2022-02-24) —
  social-media.
- Positions this tactic as differentiated specifically because "very few people are
  doing" it, contrasted with generic advice ("just post," "do videos," "do reels") that
  he frames as saturated/ineffective on its own (opinion, dated 2022-02-24) —
  social-media.
