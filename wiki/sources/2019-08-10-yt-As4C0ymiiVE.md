# One Simple Hack To Leverage Social Media When You Have no Money or Followers

## Summary

Neil's thesis: you don't need money or followers to win at social media — you get **other
people who already have a following to promote for you**. The one hack is **content first**,
then borrowed distribution. Paid version: go to **BuzzSumo** ($99/mo), enter a keyword, see
the most-shared articles, then apply **Brian Dean's Skyscraper Technique** — make your
version not 1–2x but **5–10x more detailed** so nobody wants to copy it — optionally dressing
it up with Fiverr designers or free **Canva** graphics. Then use BuzzSumo's "view shares" to
list everyone who shared the original piece, find their emails, and message them a short
pitch ("I have a similar one with 25 more tips — feel free to share") that ends with a
reciprocity **P.S.** offering to share their work back. Free version: swap BuzzSumo for
**Ubersuggest's content-ideas report** (unlimited results, shows social-share counts and
who linked), then paste the article URL into **search.twitter.com** to find the sharers and
run the same outreach. Neil says he still uses this for clients and on every new site he
builds.
