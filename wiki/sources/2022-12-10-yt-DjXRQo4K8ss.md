# How My Webinars Generate 1038 Leads A Month

## Summary
Self-reports NP Digital generates over 1,000 leads per month from webinars and lays
out four strategies to reproduce this. (1) Attract high-quality leads from the start —
cites Cloud Income that 2-5% of webinar attendees typically purchase, rising to 5-20%
with proper targeting; recommends building a buyer persona and a crystal-clear
call-to-action before promoting. (2) Co-host webinars with complementary (non-
competing) partner brands — names BigCommerce and WP Engine as past co-hosts — to
double reach and split production work. (3) Time the promotional cadence precisely: an
email a few weeks out (not a month — "they'll just forget"), a follow-up three days
before, same-day, plus SMS reminders a day before and 15 minutes before for opted-in
phone numbers, and a no-show follow-up offering the recording. (4) Distribute the live
webinar across social platforms (via a tool like Restream) while gating premium/
customized content behind a landing-page opt-in to still capture leads from viewers who
watch for free. Cites 58% of B2B marketers using webinars in content marketing (source
unnamed) and a projected $800M webinar market by 2023 (cites "letter.ly" — likely a
caption mis-transcription of a market-research source name). Closes with an NP Digital
plug.
