# 10 Extremely Useful Tools You Should Be Using In Your Marketing Right Now

## Summary
A tool roundup pitched at solo marketers/consultants who lack a full team,
framed against his own early-career experience of doing everything manually —
strategy, content, keyword research, and even logging into client sites via FTP
to hand-edit code, "back then a lot of people didn't even have CMSs." Groups
tools into four categories: content research/writing (Frase.io, SEOscout.com,
Clearscope.io, Usetopic.com, Marketmuse.com — AI-assisted outlines, headings, and
even AI-drafted paragraphs, which he says save "around half the time" though
"not perfect"; teases an upcoming free Ubersuggest AI-writing feature and asks
viewers to comment interest); paid-ad management (Madgicx for cross-platform ad
optimization across Facebook/Instagram/YouTube/Google; the Facebook Ad Library for
competitor-ad transparency); creative tools (Canva templates; Pexels free stock
imagery; Hotpot.ai for AI-assisted image editing/restoration); and SEO
(Ubersuggest, positioned as the free, no-manual-keyword-research option).
