# A Simple Way to Boost Your Amazon SEO

## Summary
Tactical walkthrough of Amazon SEO via the "A10" search algorithm, framed as ranking
on revenue-per-click via two main factors: sales velocity and keyword mastery. Cites
Amazon's marketplace as having 2.5 million sellers (as cited, dated 2023-02-11 stated
but published 2023-02-13). Six-step checklist: (1) keyword research via Amazon's own
search box autosuggest plus Ubersuggest and AnswerThePublic (which pull from Amazon
and other sources) and the Ubersuggest Chrome extension for on-Amazon search-volume
overlays; (2) title optimization — match the physical packaging label, 60–80
characters, no all-caps, use numerals, avoid subjective terms like "bestseller"
(explains packaging mismatch causes bad reviews/returns that tank rankings); (3)
description optimization — brand, size, material, color, packaging, quantity; (4) key
product features — up to five bullet points, 1,000 characters total across all five,
use only what's necessary; (5) image optimization — show product in use and multiple
angles, at least one image required, six images + one video ideal, white background
filling ≥85% of frame, 500x500 or 1000x1000px, nutrition labels for food products; (6)
search-terms field — generic words, no punctuation, stay under 250 bytes, points to
Amazon's own search-terms best-practices guide. Notes reviews and price also matter
but "everyone" is already optimizing those, framing the six steps above as the
differentiator most companies miss. Plugs NP Digital's marketplace-listing team
(Amazon, Walmart, Target).
