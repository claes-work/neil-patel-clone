# Mastermind Dinners: LeadGen That Actually Works in 2024

## Summary
A ~15-minute AOA coaching-call preview hosted by Eric Siu with several agency-owner attendees.
Topics: handling summer slowdowns by focusing on controllable inputs; landing large/Enterprise
clients via low-cost relationship-building "mastermind dinners" at conferences (Brendan's tactic:
~$200/head round-table dinners for ~25-35 travel-industry marketers, no direct pitch, positioning
the host as "a connector" rather than a vendor — cited ROI math: a $5,000/month client at 55%
margin over 18 months yields $50-60K, easily justifying a few hundred dollars of dinner spend);
building a long-term content/authority play via guest-interview podcasts (Eric's own
11-12-year-old "Leveling Up" podcast, which historically opened doors with CMOs); a question from
Enrique on how to promote a large roofing-client case study (Eric's answer: organic LinkedIn/
Twitter distribution over paid promotion, based on his agency's experience that paid amplification
of case studies "never worked"); and a question from an attendee in a technical/engineering niche
about how to scale a team that needs deep domain fluency (Eric's answer, citing Neil's agency's
VP of sales and his own head of sales — both are hired from within the discipline itself, i.e.
practitioners-turned-salespeople build trust faster than generalist sales hires).
