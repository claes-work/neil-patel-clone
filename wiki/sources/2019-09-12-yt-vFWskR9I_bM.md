# The Easiest Way to Get Started With Affiliate Marketing (Step-by-Step)

## Summary

Neil lays out a step-by-step affiliate-marketing start. (1) **Define your traffic source**
by what you're good at — SEO, paid ads, or JVs/partnerships — because it dictates approach:
SEO and JVs require picking one niche and focusing; paid ads can work any niche if payouts
are high enough to buy traffic. (2) **Define your target audience/niche by what you love,
not what pays most** — you burn out on money-only niches, and you create better content and
make more when you already know the space. (3) **Find products via existing affiliate
networks** (JVZoo, CJ Affiliate, OfferVault, Click2Sell, CommissionSoup, ShareASale,
FlexOffers) — their top offers are the proven earners; avoid the unpopular ones (affiliates
won't promote low-earners). (4) **Build a funnel** matched to your source: SEO → content →
email (Hello Bar) → lead magnet → webinar → email follow-up; paid ads → value-first landing
page with testimonials → product page, plus exit email capture and follow-up; JVs → co-host
webinars with influencers. (5) **Increase lifetime value** — keep serving the list (help
~90% of the time, sell ~10%), and add upsells/downsells (force the merchant to have them,
or fulfill their product and add your own).

## Key claims

- As of 2019-09-12, Neil said **pick your affiliate approach by your existing skill** — SEO
  and JVs demand a single focused niche; **paid ads can run any niche** if payouts justify
  buying traffic.
- As of 2019-09-12, Neil said **choose a niche you love, not the highest-paying one**,
  because burnout kills money-only niches and expertise increases revenue.
- As of 2019-09-12, Neil said to **source offers from affiliate networks** (JVZoo, CJ
  Affiliate, OfferVault, ShareASale, FlexOffers, etc.) and promote their **popular** offers,
  since popularity signals affiliates are earning.
- As of 2019-09-12, Neil's **funnel by source**: SEO → email → webinar → follow-up; paid
  ads → value landing page + testimonials → product page + exit capture; JVs → co-hosted
  webinars.
- As of 2019-09-12, Neil said to **maximize lifetime value** by serving the list mostly
  (help ~90%, sell ~10%) and adding **upsells/downsells** after the initial affiliate sale.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Most people are like, 'I want to be in the niche that pays me the most money.' You don't do well as affiliate marketer in that. Why? Because after awhile you give up, you hate what you're doing."

> "If you do paid ads, you can do any niche out there as long as the payouts are high enough because you got to buy that traffic."

> "If you just sell to them every single week, eventually they're going to drop off your list. But if you help them out majority of the time and then sell to them one-tenth of the time, they're much more likely to convert."
