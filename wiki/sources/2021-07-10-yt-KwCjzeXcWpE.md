# A New Way To Build Links (And Spend Less Time Finding Opportunities)

## Summary
Solo screen-share tutorial demoing Ubersuggest's **Backlink Opportunity report**: enter
your own domain plus several competitors' domains, and the tool surfaces every site that
links to at least one competitor but not to you — the premise being that sites already
linking to multiple players in your space are far more likely to link to you too than
cold-outreach targets are. Demoed live against neilpatel.com vs. moz.com, Search Engine
Land, and Search Engine Journal for the keyword "SEO." Adds a refinement: run the report
at the specific-article level (not just domain level) to find sites linking to a
competitor's article similar to one of yours, then pitch a differentiator (demoed pitch:
"AI in SEO is a really booming topic, and none of them really cover that"). Pairs the
report with **Hunter.io** (free tier, 25 searches/month) to find contact emails for
outreach targets. States a target benchmark: "if you're emailing out 100 people... you
should aim for 15 plus links."

## Key claims
- Recommends Ubersuggest's Backlink Opportunity report (own domain + competitor domains)
  to find sites already linking to competitors but not to you, on the logic that such
  sites are far likelier outreach converts than cold targets (paraphrase, 2021-07-10) —
  seo.
- Refines the tactic to the article level: find sites linking to a specific competitor
  article similar to one of yours, and pitch a concrete differentiator in the outreach
  email rather than a generic ask (paraphrase, 2021-07-10) — seo.
- States a link-building outreach benchmark: out of 100 highly targeted, customized
  outreach emails, aim for 15+ resulting links (self-reported target, 2021-07-10) — seo.
- Uses Hunter.io's free tier (25 searches/month) to source outreach-target email addresses
  (self-reported tool practice, 2021-07-10) — seo.
