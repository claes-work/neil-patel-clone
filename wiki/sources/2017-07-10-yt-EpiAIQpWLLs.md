# How to Use Storytelling to Connect With Readers - Google Analytics Guide to Lower Bounce Rates

## Summary
A five-tactic tutorial on increasing time-on-site (and reducing sub-10-second bounces). Neil's levers: larger text size (because readers are on mobile/tablets), storytelling to hook readers, using Crazy Egg to observe behavior and act on it, diversifying content types (video, audio, tools — not just text), and adding visuals to break up text blocks. The Crazy Egg point carries a concrete self-example: heatmaps revealed readers jump to the conclusion first, so he began adding conclusions to every post and time-on-site rose.

## Key claims
As of 2017-07-10, Neil states:
- Most sites have a minority of visitors who stay a long time (and may buy) while most bounce in under 10 seconds (as of 2017-07-10).
- Make text size large enough: small text drives bounces, not because readers are "blind" but because so many are on mobile/tablets where small text is hard to read (as of 2017-07-10).
- Storytelling keeps people reading and hooked, so they stay longer; he deliberately tells stories in videos, blog content, and even his About page (as of 2017-07-10).
- Use Crazy Egg to see mouse movements, clicks, scroll depth, and mis-clicks (e.g. clicks on non-clickable images), then act on the data (as of 2017-07-10).
- Self-example: Crazy Egg showed readers scrolling to the bottom to read the conclusion then scrolling back up; he hadn't had conclusions on every post — after adding conclusions everywhere, time-on-site increased, because readers use the (summarizing) conclusion to decide whether to read the rest (as of 2017-07-10).
- Use different content types beyond text — video and audio; he has dedicated podcast and video pages, and his tools page has more than double the time-on-site of his text pages (as of 2017-07-10).
- Add visuals/images within text: blocks of text alone lose people; well-chosen images convey the message ("a picture says a thousand words") and keep readers hooked (as of 2017-07-10).

## Notable verbatim quotes
