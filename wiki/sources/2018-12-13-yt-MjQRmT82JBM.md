# How to Leverage Facebook Groups for Building an Email List

## Summary

A tactical monologue giving four ways to convert a Facebook group into email subscribers ("the money's in the list"). (1) **Repost links to lead-magnet landing pages** (ebook/whitepaper/webinar opt-ins) inside your own or others' groups — reposting plain blog articles collects far fewer emails than reposting a gated offer. (2) **Make the group private and ask onboarding/qualifying questions** (name, email, company, company size): this both collects emails and admits higher-quality members — Neil stresses you want the most qualified group, not the biggest — and you can pipe the answers via Zapier into Google Sheets or an ESP like MailChimp. (3) **Add a call-to-action in the group description** (e.g. "check out this webinar/cheat sheet," "start with this step-by-step tutorial") pointing to a landing page. (4) **Put your offer in the group/page cover image** so it's the first thing members see, driving them to your landing pages. He closes by urging viewers to implement these today and to comment if their group's email capture isn't working.
