# You Don't Need a Morning Routine to Be Successful But You Do Need This

## Summary

★ L3-candidate — a persona-defining, contrarian productivity belief: Neil says a morning routine
does **not** make you successful (he doesn't have one and thinks they're "a waste"); what actually
drives success is a **daily checklist** derived top-down from a yearly goal. His method: set a
yearly goal, break it into monthly, then weekly, then **daily** tasks; write a fresh checklist each
day of exactly what must get done, and don't go to sleep until it's all ticked off. Self-reported
bio nugget: he says his company is on track for "3X" that year (revenue three times the prior year)
because he and his team clear their checklists.

- As of 2019-03-23, Neil argued a morning routine will not make you successful — it may get you out of

> "A morning routine will not make you successful."
