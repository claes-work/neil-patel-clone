# How to Get More Instagram Story Mentions (Fast and for FREE)

## Summary
Solo tactical/screen-share video: how to get more Instagram story mentions and turn them
into loyal followers. Demonstrates using MobileMonkey (a chatbot tool) connected to his
own Instagram account to auto-reply with a canned but personal-sounding message ("Hey, I
just want to say thanks for mentioning me in your story. I really appreciate it. Hope all
is well with you") whenever someone mentions him in their story, screen-recording his
actual Instagram inbox with real fan replies as proof. His stated logic: good content is
necessary but not sufficient for story mentions; people who mention you once are more
likely to mention you again if you engage back; manually replying to every mention doesn't
scale, so a canned first response plus manual personalized follow-up (when someone
responds with something specific) builds loyalty at scale. Reports roughly half of people
respond to the canned message. Walks the MobileMonkey setup: Chatbot sidebar → Instagram
story mention trigger → connect Instagram account → build a text-based widget reply
(tested several variants; the one shown tested best for response rate). Frames this as a
free, simple hack for building a more loyal Instagram following via engagement rather than
follower count alone.

## Key claims
- States roughly half of people who receive his canned "thanks for the mention" reply
  respond back, and he follows up personally when they do (self-reported, 2021-05-24) —
  social-media.
- Belief: good content alone doesn't guarantee social mentions/engagement — you also need
  to actively engage back with people who engage with you, which is "the whole concept of
  social media" (paraphrase, 2021-05-24) — social-media.
- Recommends automating the first-touch response (via a chatbot tool) but doing follow-up
  personalization manually, rather than fully automating engagement (paraphrase,
  2021-05-24) — social-media.
