# The Highest Reach and Engagement Content Types - Module 1 - Lesson 3 - Facebook Unlocked

## Summary
Facebook Unlocked Module 1, Lesson 3: a survey of content types that perform well on
Facebook, framed around mobile-first design (most Facebook revenue and viewing happens
on mobile) and short attention spans. Runs through a checklist: how-to posts, video
tutorials, industry stats (illustrated with a fabricated example stat he explicitly
flags as made up, contrasted with a "boring" real stat to show why surprising numbers
outperform expected ones), timely industry news, case studies (citing his own
"analyzed five billion articles from 64 countries" and "120,000 Google ads" studies),
live demos/checklists, "Ask [Name] Anything" Q&A formats, roundups, creative formats
(carousels/canvases/video), instructional guides (name-checking his own early "Advanced
Guide to SEO" as the template), podcast content, inspirational quotes (his own
best-performing post being an Elon Musk quote, from before Musk was "uber, uber rich"),
Messenger marketing via ManyChat, video blogs/vlogs, and Facebook Live/webinars — with a
self-reported client case citing $400,000+ in sales across 10 live streams. Closes on
cross-repurposing content across platforms and a plug for a downloadable content-type
checklist template.
