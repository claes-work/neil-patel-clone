# How To Build Thousands of Backlinks Without Even Asking For Them (5 Actionable Tactics)

## Summary
Neil lays out five organic (non-outreach) link-building tactics. (1) Free courses —
cites HubSpot Academy earning 1,000+ backlinks from 450 referring domains and 37K+
US monthly visitors via Ubersuggest data; recommends courses be Udemy-paid quality
before giving away free. (2) Free tools — Ubersuggest itself has generated 25,000+
backlinks from 3,400+ referring domains (English) plus another 11,000 from 3,000+
domains across its German/Spanish/Portuguese variants; also cites Bankrate's mortgage
calculator (3,400+ backlinks, 413 referring domains); suggests buying cheap tools off
CodeCanyon ($10-40) if budget-constrained. (3) Proprietary stats/industry reports — two
of his own reports (one from 183 companies on content marketing, one from 483 companies
on social platforms) generated 97,000+ backlinks from 119 referring domains combined.
(4) Ego bait — roundup posts, reviews of others' books/tools, listicles (e.g. "top 15
SEO experts"), interviews, and self-created industry awards/badges. (5) Infographics
and data visualizations — recounts that at KISSmetrics, the majority of links came from
40+ infographics, which drove the blog to a million visitors/month and lifted rankings
for all blog content.

## Key claims
- As of 2020-02-22, Neil said HubSpot Academy's free certified marketing course has
  generated over 1,000 backlinks from 450 referring domains and gets 37,000+ monthly US
  visitors (per Ubersuggest).
- As of 2020-02-22, Neil said Ubersuggest has generated over 25,000 backlinks from
  3,400+ referring domains in English alone, plus another ~11,000 backlinks from 3,000+
  more referring domains across its language variants (German, Spanish, Portuguese,
  etc.).
- As of 2020-02-22, Neil said Bankrate's mortgage calculator tool has 3,400+ backlinks
  from 413 referring domains, as an example outside the SEO industry.
- As of 2020-02-22, Neil said two proprietary research reports he published (one on
  content marketing from 183 companies' data, one on social media from 483 companies'
  data) generated over 97,000 backlinks combined from 119 referring domains.
- As of 2020-02-22, Neil said at his former company **KISSmetrics**, the majority of
  backlinks came from 40+ infographics his team created, which grew the blog to a
  million visitors a month and lifted rankings for all of the blog's content.

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "A lot of people are reluctant to build backlinks. They get scared because you need to do outreach to hundreds, if not, thousands of website's owners before you get your first handful of backlinks. But truth be told, you don't have to do that."

> "I used to have a company called KISSmetrics, and the majority of the links that we generated were from infographics, and the blog got up to million visitors a month, but the reason the blog did well is all of the infographics, and we created over 40 of them, got thousands of links, and that caused all the blog content to rank higher as well."
