# Tools To Improve Your Content Marketing - Module 3 - Lesson 3 - Content Marketing Unlocked

## Summary
Week-three lesson 3 of **Content Marketing Unlocked**
([[2020-08-13-yt-SxEEg1tOV70]]), closing out the course's analytics/tracking
lesson. Walks through setting up Google Analytics goals (Admin → Goals → New Goal),
custom alerts (traffic spikes/drops, revenue changes, high bounce rate), and
annotations to log what changed on a given day and correlate it with performance
shifts. Recommends checking Site Content/Landing Pages and Acquisition/Social and
Google Ads reports year-over-year. States his own primary KPIs are revenue and
overall traffic for brand awareness. Directs viewers to Google Search Console for
full keyword-level ranking/CTR data (which Analytics doesn't fully expose), and
repeats the earlier-lesson CTR-improvement tactics (rewrite content, add target
keywords with impressions-but-no-ranking, update title/meta, add images/surveys).
Recommends Google Tag Manager for centralizing tracking tags, and Ubersuggest's
dashboard (which auto-imports Search Console keywords) for tracking desktop/mobile
rank over time. Closes pointing to npdigital.com / Neil Patel Digital for agency
services.

## Key claims
- As of 2020-08-15, Neil stated his primary analytics KPIs are revenue and overall
  traffic for brand awareness (self-reported prioritization).
- As of 2020-08-15, Neil's workflow explicitly chains three tools: Google Analytics
  (behavior/goals) → Google Search Console (full keyword/CTR data) → Ubersuggest
  (rank tracking, auto-importing Search Console keywords).
