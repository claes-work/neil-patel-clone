# 5 Techniques to Promote Your Content When You Don't Have Any Money

## Summary

Neil gives five zero-budget content-promotion tactics. (1) **Answer Quora questions** and link
to your blog when relevant — Quora posts rank broadly in Google, making it one of the easiest
free traffic sources. (2) **Go live on social media** — live video is heavily favored by
platform algorithms (they want to compete with TV) and gets shown to nearly all subscribers;
he cites **Tai Lopez** ("some people may hate on him, but he's a really smart marketer")
generating real sales from live because a live audience is captive. (3) **Video teasers** — use
your phone to tease upcoming content and build pent-up demand, the way **Apple** manufactures
mystery before a product launch. (4) **Answer questions on social media** (Facebook, Twitter,
groups), especially on **competitors'** properties where your ideal audience already is — you
gain followers and can link back. (5) **Direct outreach** — when you link out to people, email
them; some will share. He notes this even works on him ("it even works with me"), and that done
in **quantity** it reliably adds traffic even though not everyone shares.
