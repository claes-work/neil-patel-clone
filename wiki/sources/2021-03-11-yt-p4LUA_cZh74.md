# How to Find The Ideal Backlink

## Summary
Solo tactical link-building walkthrough: Google the target keyword, identify a
competitor's internal (non-homepage, non-exact-match-domain) ranking page, then run
that exact URL through Ubersuggest's backlinks report to see who links to it. Filters
recommended: "follow" links only, one link per referring domain (to avoid over-crediting
sites that link many times), and skip low-content/spammy-looking referring URLs.
Recommends Hunter.io (free tier available) to find contact emails for the sites doing
the linking, then send a manually personalized outreach email referencing the specific
article the target site already linked to, offering a comparable-or-better resource and
asking for a link if they find it valuable. States a rough conversion expectation:
sending ~100 such emails yields roughly 3–6 replies/links, and recommends repeating
weekly, framing the tactic as "tedious... but it works."

## Key claims
- States a specific outreach ratio: ~100 personalized link-outreach emails yields

- Recommends filtering Ubersuggest backlink reports to "follow" links and "one link
