# Welcome to the Instagram Unlocked: From 0 to 100,000 Followers - New Neil Patel Course

## Summary
This is the welcome/getting-started lesson of Neil Patel's free "Instagram Unlocked" course, which promises to take a viewer from zero to 100,000 Instagram followers in under a year across a two-week, six-video program. The video is almost entirely framing and motivation rather than tactics: Neil establishes his credibility (co-founder of Ubersuggest and Neil Patel Digital, blogger at neilpatel.com, large social following, website traffic he values via SEMrush at roughly $2M/month), sets expectations for how to take the course (go step by step, don't skip ahead, take notes, execute every detail), and makes the case for Instagram's importance with a wall of platform statistics. He frames Instagram as a network rather than a photo app, aiming for real engaged followers who comment, buy, and engage rather than "fake" followers. He notes the course targets people going from zero (or the thousands) to their first 100,000 followers, and is less useful for those chasing 1–2 million. He closes by previewing the remaining lessons — profile foundation, content types, case studies, marketing hacks, and behind-the-scenes — and points viewers to neilpatel.com/training for the videos plus downloadable worksheets, files, PDFs, and cheat sheets.

## Key claims
- The course promises to teach going from zero to 100,000 Instagram followers in less than a year, delivered as a two-week program of six step-by-step videos released roughly once every two days (as of 2020-09-14).
- Success depends on completing the course fully without skipping ahead, taking notes, and executing every detail; done that way it is not hard because there are only six videos (as of 2020-09-14).
- The goal is real followers who comment, engage, and buy — not "fake" followers (as of 2020-09-14).
- Neil's credibility framing: he is co-founder of Ubersuggest and Neil Patel Digital, blogs at neilpatel.com, has social profiles some exceeding a million followers, and his own Instagram has over 100,000 followers (as of 2020-09-14).
- His website's monthly traffic is worth roughly $2 million according to SEMrush (as of 2020-09-14).
- Instagram was founded in 2010; Facebook acquired it in April 2012 for roughly $1 billion in cash and stock (as of 2020-09-14). [Caption garble corrected: transcript reads "in April, 2012, Facebook acquired the service" — the acquisition was announced April 2012.]
- Instagram has over 1 billion monthly active users, and 68% of people come to Instagram to interact with creators (as of 2020-09-14).
- Companies and marketers spend anywhere from ~$25,000 to ~$100,000 per year on influencer marketing, and that spend is shifting toward Instagram over platforms like Facebook; many influencers easily earn $100,000+ per post, with top names (e.g., the Kardashians) potentially commanding over $1 million (as of 2020-09-14).
- Instagram sees roughly 4.2 billion likes per day, generates over $20 billion in ad revenue per year, and has a valuation over $100 billion (as of 2020-09-14).
- Instagram has more than 25 million business profiles and more than 2 million monthly active advertisers (as of 2020-09-14).
- Per SimilarWeb, Instagram is among the fifth-most-popular sites in the world; an estimated 75.3% of U.S. businesses are on Instagram, and that share is increasing rapidly (as of 2020-09-14).
- The course is most valuable for people going from zero (or the low thousands) toward their first 100,000 followers, and will help less for those trying to reach 1–2 million (as of 2020-09-14).
- The six lessons are: getting started/fundamentals (this video), profile foundation, content types, case studies, marketing hacks, and behind-the-scenes; Neil names Instagram marketing hacks as his favorite lesson (as of 2020-09-14).
- All videos plus downloadable worksheets, files, PDFs, and cheat sheets are available at neilpatel.com/training under "Instagram Unlocked" (as of 2020-09-14).
