# How I Gain 1,254 Followers Per Week on Instagram Organically

## Summary

Neil shares his organic Instagram playbook, claiming **1,254 new organic followers per
week** with no follow/unfollow automation. Core moves: (1) **test drastically different
content** — the content you personally like may not be what your audience engages with; if
followers aren't rising, the content is missing (he cites Tim Sykes' laptop-in-a-cave/pool
shots that work for that audience); (2) **leverage IGTV**, an under-used long-video surface
Instagram is pushing hard, which lifted his numbers; (3) get **creative with interactive
posts** ("double tap if…" prompts) that play along with Instagram's tap culture and can go
viral; (4) publish **micro-content** — don't over-produce; film your day, chop talks/selfies
into bite-sized posts to engage more often; (5) **constantly post Stories**, which he says
have the best engagement he's seen (feed images sometimes *lose* followers when the audience
is mismatched, while Stories keep the count climbing because Instagram wants to "crush
Snapchat"). Bonus: **Instagram Live** builds the emotional connection that drives follows.
