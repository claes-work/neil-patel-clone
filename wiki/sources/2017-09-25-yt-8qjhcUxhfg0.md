# 4 Facebook Marketing Tips to Boost Engagement on Your Page | How Often Should You Post?

## Summary
Neil answers how often you should post on Facebook and gives four tactics to maximize engagement on a Facebook Page. He recommends posting daily, ideally multiple times per day, while noting a tradeoff: the more you post, the less engagement you get per individual post. He advises using Facebook Insights to find when your audience is online and engaged, posting during those windows, spacing posts several hours apart, and using Facebook's targeting options (country/language) so the right audience sees each post — which he says makes content more likely to go viral.

## Key claims
As of 2017-09-25, Neil states:
- Simply having a Facebook presence is not enough; if you don't post, no one will learn about you or your business (as of 2017-09-25).
- You should post on Facebook daily, and ideally multiple times per day — two or three times (as of 2017-09-25).
- You generally shouldn't post more than five to ten times a day, though you can if you have amazing content getting high engagement (as of 2017-09-25).
- In general, the more often you post, the less engagement you get per post (as of 2017-09-25).
- Check your Facebook Insights to see what time of day people are on your page and engaging, and post during those specific times (as of 2017-09-25).
- On his own Facebook page, Neil says he gets a ton of engagement in the mornings, so he posts more frequently in the mornings and less at night when engagement is lower (as of 2017-09-25).
- Space out your posts: don't post the next one only an hour later — spread them at least two to three hours apart, ideally over four hours (as of 2017-09-25).
- Target your fans using Facebook's per-post targeting option to choose country or language, so the right people see your content, making it more likely to go viral (as of 2017-09-25).
