# Social Media Marketing in 2019: Here's What You Haven't Tried

## Summary

Neil's answer to declining organic social reach in 2019, aimed at getting past ~100 to 1,000+
visitors/month from social. (1) **Share the same content multiple times** — most people post
once; re-sharing the same article ~six times (scheduled via MeetEdgar or Hootsuite) yields
roughly double-to-triple the traffic (not 6x) since most followers never saw it the first time.
(2) **Create engaging content optimized for comments** — in 2019 the algorithms weigh comments
over likes/shares because commenting takes more effort; ask questions in your content and reply
to every comment (with a return question). (3) **Don't be afraid to promote your business** —
social traffic is "useless" only if you never add a call-to-action; he plugs his agency at the
end of every video and says light self-promotion is fine. (4) **Use BuzzSumo** to see what
content is hot by social shares, filterable by timeframe (last week/3/6 months) so you create
what works *now*, not two years ago. (5) **Actually participate on the social web** — comment on
*other people's* profiles and start conversations, not just post on your own. (6) **Optimize
your own conversions** — social traffic won't convert by itself; use Crazy Egg to see where
people click and run A/B tests, and Hello Bar / Subscribers to capture emails and push
subscribers so you can nurture and convert them over time.
