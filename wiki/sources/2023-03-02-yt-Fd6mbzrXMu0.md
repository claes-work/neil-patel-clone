# 5 Ways to Eliminate Wasted Ad Spend

## Summary
Tactical listicle on cutting wasted ad spend. Cites Nextaco Digital Media's waste
report: an average 40% of media spend was wasted in Q1 2022 (as cited, dated
2023-03-02); Google found more than 56% of ad impressions are never seen by
customers (as cited); Proxima estimates $37B of worldwide marketing budgets are
wasted on poor digital performance (as cited). Five strategies: (1) target
audiences precisely — lookalikes, email-list uploads, platform-learning from
existing conversions; narrow focus before scaling broad, citing 66% of customers
expect brands to understand their unique needs (as cited); (2) retarget users at
different funnel stages — cites consumers are 70% more likely to convert after
clicking a retargeting ad (as cited), illustrated with a mattress cart-abandonment
example; (3) cross-channel approach — cites brands using 3+ channels see a 287%
higher purchase rate than single-channel campaigns (as cited), and describes
applying learnings from one channel (e.g. Instagram/TikTok) to others (Facebook,
Google); (4) use his free "Ads Grader" tool at neilpatel.com/blog to audit ad
accounts; (5) dayparting — run ads only when staff can act on the resulting leads
(e.g. a B2B call center open Mon–Fri 9–5 shouldn't run ads 24/7), and pause ads
during periods with nothing to sell (e.g. holidays). Closes with an NP Digital plug
citing a self-reported "Paid Ad Campaign of the Year" award.
