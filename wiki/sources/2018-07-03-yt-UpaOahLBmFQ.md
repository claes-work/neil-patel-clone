# 3 SEO Tips for Voice Search Optimization: Get Ahead of the Competition NOW

## Summary

A solo tactical monologue on optimizing for voice search (framed around the rise of Alexa
and voice assistants). Neil's tips: **(1) HTTPS** — over 65% of voice-search results come
from HTTPS sites, so secure your site. **(2) Build authoritative domain** — you won't
become Forbes/HuffPost/Business Insider overnight, but sites with strong links, great
content, social shares, and good UX (deserved authority) win the lion's share of voice
traffic; if you're not there, improve content/design and do outreach to competitors'
linkers. **(3) Fast load speed** — he cites his friend Brian Dean that pages should load
in **precisely 4.6 seconds or faster** (~50%+ faster than the average page) to win voice
traffic; check via Google PageSpeed. Bonus: **write in-depth content (2,200+ words) with
short, direct answers under headings** — long content ranks for more terms, while concise
one-to-two-sentence answers under question headings (e.g. "What is SEO?") make it easy for
Google to serve them as voice answers. Neil acknowledges voice search isn't the majority of
traffic yet but urges getting ahead of it.
