# 3 Ways to Automate Your Content Curation | The Ultimate Digital Marketing Strategy

## Summary
A short social-media video in which Neil Patel shows a tool chain to automate content
curation so social profiles stay active without you creating every post. The premise:
popular social profiles need a continual stream of new content, and you can post other
people's content, but doing it manually takes a lot of time. His pipeline: (1) On Twitter,
follow respected people in your industry and favorite/star the content you like, building a
running list of good posts in your space. (2) As you read those pieces, save the ones you
like into Pocket. (3) Use IFTTT or Zapier to connect Pocket to Buffer, so everything you
save in Pocket is automatically scheduled and shared across your social profiles
(LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter). The result automates most curation and shares far more
content, faster, than manually reading and uploading one by one — building a bigger
following.
