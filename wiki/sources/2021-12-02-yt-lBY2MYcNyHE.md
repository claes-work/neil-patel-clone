# One e-Commerce Hack That Will Increase Your Sales

## Summary
> ⚠️ **Sponsored disclosure**: Neil states "today's video is sponsored by PayPal."
> Treat this as sponsor-aligned promotion of PayPal's "Pay in 4" buy-now-pay-later
> (BNPL) product, not independent evaluation — same caveat as
> [[wiki/sources/2021-11-25-yt-UFIoJUhKreY]].

Frames affordability, not product quality or marketing, as the ultimate ceiling on
e-commerce conversion, then pitches PayPal's "Pay in 4" (split purchases into four
interest-free, no-late-fee installments) as a no-additional-cost lever for merchants
already on PayPal. Cites third-party stats throughout: Grand View Research puts the
global e-commerce market at over $10T (2020), forecast to reach $27.15T by 2027; Oberlo
data shows only 1.94% of e-commerce visits convert and 69.57% of carts are abandoned;
C+R Research found 51% of US consumers used buy-now-pay-later during the pandemic; a
PayPal-commissioned study found 28% of 18-39-year-olds are more likely to shop again
with a merchant offering BNPL; Forrester (commissioned by PayPal, Nov 2020) found BNPL
drove 21% of surveyed B2C merchants' online sales. Lists standard CRO levers (CRO
testing, abandoned-cart email recovery, limited-time discounts, free shipping,
competitive pricing, live chat, checkout-flow optimization, reviews, mobile
optimization) as the baseline before layering BNPL on top, and gives implementation
tips (visible messaging near add-to-cart and on product/checkout pages, exit-intent
popups).

- Cites Oberlo data: average e-commerce conversion rate of 1.94% and cart-abandonment
