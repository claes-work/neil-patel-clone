# How to Drive More Sales from Instagram

## Summary
Four Instagram sales tactics: (1) Linktree in bio to route followers to multiple
products/services; (2) intersperse educational Stories with promotional "swipe up"
Stories (worked example: three SEO-tip Stories followed by a fourth pitching NP Digital);
(3) sell via Instagram/social Live, citing Tai Lopez as an example of someone who
allegedly built ownership stakes in companies (named: Dressbarn, Pier 1) by pitching
investors while going live — Neil frames this as evidence live video builds the deeper
connection needed to close deals; (4) don't be afraid to post product/service promotions
directly to the feed, especially time-limited offers, even at the cost of lower
likes/comments.
