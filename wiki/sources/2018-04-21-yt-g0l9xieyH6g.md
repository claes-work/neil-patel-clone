# #1 Way To Market Your Business [YOU HAVEN'T TRIED YET]

## Summary

In this short solo video, Neil Patel argues that the single most overlooked marketing tactic is the simplest one: verbally tell people what your business does. He confesses he is guilty of this himself — most of his audience does not know he co-founded an ad agency called Neil Patel Digital because he never says so, even while speaking at conferences. He reframes self-promotion as a non-negotiable entrepreneurial skill ("if you're too afraid to promote your own business... you're not cut out to be an entrepreneur"), and uses it to make a channel-diversification case: relying on any single acquisition channel is dangerous because platform algorithm changes can wipe out traffic overnight (he cites SEO/Google algorithm updates, Facebook's reach collapse, and Gmail's Promotions tab). He closes by explicitly promoting NP Digital as an agency that helps small, medium, and large businesses grow across Google, YouTube, Facebook, email, and conversion optimization.
