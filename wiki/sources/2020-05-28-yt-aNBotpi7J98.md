# Discover Your Competitive Strategy - Content Marketing Part 2 - Lesson 3 - SEO Unlocked

## Summary
Competitive-research lesson: use Ubersuggest's Backlinks and Top Pages reports on a
competitor's domain to reverse-engineer what content is working for them (by
backlinks, social shares, estimated monthly Google visits), then read/download their
lead magnets (ebooks, demos) directly to understand their funnel. Walks through
worked examples by business-model category: affiliate (Wirecutter, Outdoor Gearlab —
product-review content monetized via affiliate links); B2B software (Marketo/Adobe,
Canva — content built around product education/templates); podcasting (Dr. Axe,
Joe Rogan — analyze their backlink/top-pages profile even if you don't have a podcast
yourself, to see what they link to and sell); consulting (points to his own
neilpatel.com Services tab and internal banner/sidebar-ad funnel as a copyable
template); e-commerce (Lego as the flagship example — millions of monthly visitors
via content like character/product breakdowns monetized through affiliate links); and
paid courses (references his own now-discontinued "Advanced Marketing Program," which
he used content marketing to sell before shifting to giving courses away free, as with
SEO Unlocked itself).
