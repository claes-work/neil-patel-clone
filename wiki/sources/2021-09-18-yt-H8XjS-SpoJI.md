# How to Develop & Sell a Digital Product, Step by Step (1 Million Revenue Formula) ★

## Summary
A first-person account of Neil's own past info-product business, disclosed as a
discontinued venture: self-reported average monthly revenue of **$371,882** (peak
~$600K, low ~$200K), sustained "well over a year." States he stopped because he wanted
"a bigger business" rather than a lifestyle business, and frames info-products as a
legitimate lifestyle-business path for others even though scaling to tens of millions
that way is hard. Walks through his own seven-step system end to end: (1) survey the
audience (SurveyMonkey) to find pain points and derive the product — his own product
targeted "get traffic to your website," the dominant pain point named by his audience;
(2) sell via webinar with an early hook, a stick-around incentive (a promised cheat
sheet), and a software/automation-framed pitch (WebinarJam/EverWebinar for in-webinar
checkout); (3) money-back guarantee, extended with a $1, 7-day free-trial offer for
webinar attendees who didn't buy; (4) ongoing training/coaching updates plus an annual
product refresh to sustain sales; (5) build a community around the course cohort;
(6) scale via paid ads — Facebook first, YouTube second — self-reporting over $100K/
month ad spend at peak, which he says outperformed SEO traffic to the webinar;
(7) patience — use Google Analytics funnel diagnostics (webinar drop-off, checkout
drop-off, refund-request rate) to iteratively improve, expecting three to six months
before strong results and dismissing anyone promising results within 30 days. Notes NP
Digital does not offer info-product services, pointing instead to tools like Kajabi.
