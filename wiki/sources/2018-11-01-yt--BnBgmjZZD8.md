# Accepting Bitcoin as Payment: Smart Business Move or (HUGE) Mistake?

_Q&A Thursdays; **Adam Lodolce** (viewership.com) hosts/asks and adds commentary, Neil answers.
Only **Neil-attributed** material trains the persona. Viewer question (Maxwell Graves): what are
the steps to accept Bitcoin as payment for services — and should you? Neil flags crypto as "outside
of my comfort zone" and answers the "should you" part rather than the mechanics._

## Summary

Neil's verdict: **for most businesses right now, do NOT accept Bitcoin as your main payment** —
because **volatility can wipe out your margin**. If your profit margin is 5–30% and Bitcoin swings
**±5–20% in a single day** (he claims to have seen both +20% and −20% days), a payment can lose more
value than you earned. The **only** cases where accepting it is fine: you're **cash-rich and don't
need the money** (so you can hold long-term as a bet), or your **margins are huge** (he says at
"500 percent" margins you have little to lose). Otherwise: **take USD (or your local currency), then
use the profits to buy Bitcoin** if you believe in it — that way a crash doesn't bankrupt you. He
believes **crypto/blockchain is "a large part of the future"** and that **price stability will come as
volume grows and institutions/hedge funds enter** (analogy: the stock market was more volatile when
newer and thinner), but **that takes a long time**; he also notes possible **exchange price
manipulation** (reported in the press; he can't confirm). Tax reminder: **you still owe taxes** on
crypto-denominated sales — "uncle sam doesn't accept bitcoin for taxes" — so you can't take Bitcoin to
dodge taxes. Repeated bottom line: **thin margins → don't take it; take USD.**

## Key claims

- As of 2018-11-01, **most businesses should not accept Bitcoin as their main payment** — daily
  volatility (**±5–20%**) can exceed typical **5–30% margins** and cause losses/bankruptcy.
- As of 2018-11-01, **exceptions**: **cash-rich** businesses (can hold long-term) or **very high-margin**
  ones ("500 percent") — they have little to lose.
- As of 2018-11-01, the safe path is **take USD, then buy Bitcoin with profits** if you believe in it.

- As of 2018-11-01, he notes **alleged exchange price manipulation** (press-reported; unconfirmed).
