# Watch Me Build a Marketing Strategy in 10 Minutes For a Completely Random Business

## Summary
A live-demo format: viewers submitted random businesses via comments/email/social, and
he builds a full marketing strategy on camera for a cosmetic-dentistry business in
Orlando, Florida, sight-unseen. Six-step process: (1) pull region-specific keyword
ideas from Ubersuggest for "cosmetic dentistry"/"dentistry" filtered to Orlando; (2)
compile a keyword set spanning services (implants, veneers, teeth cleaning); (3)
analyze a top-ranking competitor page's structure (heading tags, contact form,
"request my appointment" CTA in the header, click-to-call) as a template for good user
experience; (4) build topic clusters — one hub page per service (teeth whitening,
dental implants, porcelain veneers, Invisalign, dentures, smile makeover, cosmetic
bonding) rather than one generic page, reasoning Google rewards thoroughness over a
single shallow page; (5) optimize Google My Business — complete keyword-rich
descriptions, high-quality facility/result photos (explicitly: photos of outcomes, not
just the location — "if I was a restaurant, I would have pictures of the food, and
people eating"), and actively track reviews; (6) run paid ads with a low-investment,
high-value offer, targeting age cohorts appropriate to the service (e.g., older
patients for denture implants vs. younger for teeth whitening) and testing offers per
segment.
