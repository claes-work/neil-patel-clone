# This Helped Me Get More Traffic Right Away

## Summary
Positions Instagram Stories as a fast, non-SEO traffic lever for when organic search
traffic dips, citing Instagram's 2B+ monthly active users with 59% logging in daily,
and that 33% of the most-viewed Stories come from businesses (vs. individual users).
Notes marketers spend 31% of their Instagram ad budget on Stories, leveraging the
swipe-up-to-website mechanic for instant traffic. Six content types to promote via
Stories: (1) products (36% of Stories promote a product) — recommends building the
pitch across multiple Stories before the swipe-up, so the landing page only needs to
close the purchase, not educate; (2) live events (10% of brand Stories) — cites Apple
product launches as the reference example for anticipation-building; (3) explainer/
how-to videos (5% of businesses use Stories for this) — argues these build audience
loyalty that converts into blog readers, email subscribers, and followers; (4) new
blog posts — self-reports promoting his own posts across Facebook, LinkedIn,
Instagram, and Twitter with a catchy Story hook, giving a "Google's about to release
a new update" example; (5) contests/sweepstakes — framed as a long-proven engagement
tactic (mall/casino giveaway analogy); (6) user-generated content — cites 93% of
marketers agreeing UGC outperforms branded content, recommending re-sharing
customer unboxing/review videos in Stories to build trust.
