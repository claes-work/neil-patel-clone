# eCommerce SEO: How to Bring Organic Traffic to Your Online Store

## Summary

A step-by-step tactical playbook for ranking an online store organically instead of
relying on paid ads (which Neil says Google wants you to buy). The steps: (1) do keyword
research in **Ubersuggest** on your products to learn how people search; (2) analyze
competitors in Ubersuggest — use the **Top Pages** report (their ranking pages, the
keywords each ranks for, and who links to them) and the **Keywords report** in Traffic
Analyzer to see every keyword ranking for them, most-to-least popular; (3) optimize
product pages for those keywords in title tags, meta descriptions, product descriptions,
and H1s; (4) optimize product pages for **user experience** — beautiful images, product
videos, transparent reviews (good or bad) — because a high bounce rate tanks rankings;
(5) build links by emailing the sites that link to competitors (he gives a verbatim
outreach template); (6) continually update pages for freshness (Wikipedia example again);
(7) **interlink related products** to raise cart size and spread link equity; (8) ensure
mobile compatibility with an emphasis on **page speed** (more important on mobile than
desktop); (9) add **schema markup**. He closes recommending you do both SEO and profitable
paid ads.
