# Welcome to the Content Marketing Unlocked! - Free Content Marketing Course with Neil Patel

## Summary
Launch/intro video for a new free course, **Content Marketing Unlocked** (four
modules, one per week, three lessons/videos per week, worksheets/cheat sheets per
lesson at neilpatel.com/training) — this immediately follows the completion of
**eCommerce Unlocked** ([[2020-07-18-yt-u3UjgzlkTzg]]) in the ledger, marking a new
course-era start. Neil opens with a credibility statement — co-founder of Ubersuggest
and Neil Patel Digital, author of neilpatel.com, "recognized as the top 100
entrepreneurs under the age 30 by Obama" (**self-reported**, per SUBJECT.md — no
primary document found for this award) — framing it as evidence, not bragging. Defines
content marketing ("a strategic marketing approach focused on creating and
distributing valuable, relevant, and consistent content to attract and retain an
audience") and stresses the common mistake of treating it as content creation alone
without the marketing/distribution half. Cites Larry Page ("The ultimate search engine
would understand exactly what you mean and give back exactly what you want") and Bill
Gates ("Content is king") as framing quotes, and states 84% of Google's revenue comes
from keywords/content-driven search. Lists content marketing's benefits: organic
traffic, social traffic, brand loyalty/virality, and indirect upside like speaking
engagements, book deals, and investment interest, which Neil says have happened to him
personally. Closes with two case-study claims: growing Timothy Sykes from "$600
grand a year to 15 million" largely through content marketing, and helping Gawker
Media add "5 million pages a month" through SEO and content marketing (both
self-reported, unaudited figures).
