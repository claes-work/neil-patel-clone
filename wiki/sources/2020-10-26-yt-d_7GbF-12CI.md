# LinkedIn Advanced Strategies to Turn Business Connections Into Leads & Customers - FREE Course

## Summary
Launch video for **LinkedIn Unlocked**, a free six-lesson course (Module 1 Lesson 1)
delivered over two weeks at neilpatel.com/training, each lesson (~10 min) paired with
downloadable worksheets/cheat sheets/PDFs. Neil opens with a credibility bio (clients:
Amazon, NBC, GM, Viacom, Yahoo, Facebook; NYT bestselling author; Obama top-100-under-30
recognition — self-reported, per SUBJECT.md), frames LinkedIn as the top B2B distribution
channel, and lays out platform-scale stats to justify the course's premise: LinkedIn is
underused relative to its reach because content sharing is concentrated in roughly 1% of
active users.

## Key claims
- As of 2020-10-26, LinkedIn reported ~675M users and ~260M monthly active users, with a
  stated internal goal of reaching ~3B users; Neil cites 94% of B2B marketers using
  LinkedIn as their top content-distribution channel and estimates that non-active
  companies miss "80% of your B2B leads" (self-reported/platform-cited stats, not
  independently verified in this video).
- As of 2020-10-26, Neil states LinkedIn's feed carries ~9B content impressions per week
  (~36B/month, ~468B/year) by his own extrapolation, but only ~3M of the 260M active
  users (~1%) share content weekly — framed as an opportunity: becoming an active
  sharer faces comparatively little competition (paraphrase, self-reported napkin math).
- Course structure: six ~10-minute lessons over two weeks (three per week), each with a
  fill-in-the-blank worksheet requiring ~10-25 minutes of follow-up work.
- Quotes LinkedIn founder Reid Hoffman's "intelligent and bold risks... build a network of
  alliances... pivot to a breakout opportunity" as a framing epigraph (third-party quote,
  not Neil's own words).
