# 3 Step Formula to Making 'Display Advertising' Profitable (So You Can Make More Money Online)

## Summary
Neil argues that banner (display) ads are worth using because, by supply-and-demand logic, poorly-converting inventory that few people click stays cheap, giving display a low cost per impression and cost per click. He then lays out a three-step formula for making display advertising profitable: use banner ads for remarketing so visitors who did not buy keep seeing your product across the web; rotate creatives at least once a week to beat banner blindness and keep click-through rates up; and keep the banner's messaging congruent with the landing page—for instance, if the ad promises a discount, show that discount on the landing page (crossing off the original price and displaying the reduced one) so the offer feels consistent and converts into more income and sales.

## Key claims
As of 2017-10-04, Neil states:
- You should use banner ads, and part of the reason is that ads which do not get clicks do not cost much (as of 2017-10-04).
- Display pricing follows supply and demand: when conversions are poor, few advertisers bid, so the cost typically goes down (as of 2017-10-04).
- Banner ads typically carry a low cost per impression or a low cost per click (as of 2017-10-04).
- Step one is to create banner ads for remarketing campaigns, so someone who visits your site without buying then sees ads for your products and services while browsing the web (as of 2017-10-04).
- Step two is to rotate your creatives often—at least once per week—because people develop banner blindness and stop clicking ads they have seen repeatedly (as of 2017-10-04).
- Rotating banners means that, since you pay per thousand impressions, you continually earn more clicks over time (as of 2017-10-04).
- Step three is to keep the banner's messaging congruent with the landing page; e.g., if the banner offers a discount, emphasize that discount on the landing page by crossing off the original price and showing the discounted price (as of 2017-10-04).
- Following these tips—and running display ads at all—will start generating more income and more sales (as of 2017-10-04).
