# How to Run TikTok Ads 2023 - TikTok Advertising Tutorial (TikTok for Business)

## Summary
A seven-step TikTok paid-ads tutorial. Opens with TikTok's scale (1B+ monthly
active users as of January 2022; 160M videos watched per internet minute; most-
downloaded mobile app of both 2020 and 2021). Steps: (1) install the TikTok Pixel
to enable retargeting cohorts (e.g. cart-abandoners), with a specific tactic of
showing a customer/product-usage video in the retargeting ad; (2) start with
broad audience targeting (no gender/age narrowing) and let TikTok's delivery
algorithm find the responsive segments before optimizing; (3) restrict placement
to TikTok only, not its off-platform ad network; (4) target only one interest at
a time so results are attributable; (5) start with a minimum daily budget of $50
to give the algorithm enough signal to optimize quickly; (6) cap cost-per-
conversion at breakeven; (7) use TikTok's own ad-library/transparency tooling to
study and model top-performing ads. Closes with a pitch for NP Digital's paid-
social services.

## Key claims
- Cites TikTok's own reported scale: 1B+ monthly active users as of January 2022,
