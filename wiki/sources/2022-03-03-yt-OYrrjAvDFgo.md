## Summary
A phone-only-marketing capability breakdown: Instagram and TikTok marketing (content
creation, engagement) work well or better from a phone since both platforms are
mobile-first; content creation (video, selfies, shorts, reels) works well from modern
phone cameras plus apps like Canva and Adobe Lightroom; social scheduling (Hootsuite,
Buffer, Facebook Page Manager) works from a phone. States he would personally never
run paid ad campaigns from a phone despite having seen others do it, and that SEO
work (keyword research, code/meta changes, blog writing/optimizing) is "tricky" from
a phone even though Ubersuggest has a mobile-friendly version. Net verdict: phone-only
covers daily social workflows but not one-off, precision tasks like ad management and
SEO execution.

## Key claims
- States Instagram and TikTok were both built mobile-first and are better worked from
  a phone than a desktop, including for engagement/commenting (self-reported, dated
  2022-03-03) — social-media.
- Personally would never run his own ad campaigns from a phone, despite Ubersuggest
  having a mobile-friendly interface (self-reported, dated 2022-03-03) — paid-ads.
