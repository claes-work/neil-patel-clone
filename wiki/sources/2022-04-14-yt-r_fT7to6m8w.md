# Generate 6 Months' Worth of Content in 3 Days (Content Strategy 2023)

## Summary
A five-hack content-ideation system built entirely around mining Google Search
Console (GSC) impression data and expanding it with Ubersuggest. Opens with
self-reported neilpatel.com stats (see Key claims), then walks the hacks: (1)
install GSC and harvest keywords already generating impressions — even
low-ranking, page-7/8/10 long-tail terms — as a strong signal Google already sees
the site as relevant for that topic; (2) feed those GSC keywords into Ubersuggest
to multiply them into hundreds or thousands of related terms, sorted by SEO
difficulty (lowest first); (3) mine Ubersuggest's Questions/Prepositions/Comparison
tabs for buyer-intent seed topics, explicitly including competitor-comparison
articles (his own example: "MailChimp or ConvertKit," or an agency-comparison
article naming NP Digital); (4) group keywords into topic clusters so one piece of
content can target dozens or hundreds of related terms at once; (5) generate blog
titles via frase.io's AI title generator, then draft matching meta descriptions.
Closes by recommending prioritizing high-traffic, high-CPC, low-competition
keywords first (CPC as a proxy for conversion value, per Ubersuggest data).
