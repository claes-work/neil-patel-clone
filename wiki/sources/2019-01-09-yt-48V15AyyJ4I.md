# How to Grow Your Business With PayPal

> ⚠️ SPONSORED: Neil discloses on-camera that this video is **sponsored by PayPal**.
> The revenue/conversion figures below are Neil's self-reported claims for a paid promo
> and should be treated as self-reported and promotional, not audited.

## Summary

A PayPal-sponsored tactical video on four ways to lift sales using PayPal. (1) **Add
PayPal as a checkout payment option** — Neil says it added ~6% conversions and builds
trust plus lets a global audience pay you. (2) **Offer payment plans** instead of a
single flat upfront charge — even though some buyers cancel, total revenue rose (~13%),
and PayPal makes payment plans simple; combined with tip 1 that's ~19% more sales.
(3) **Take money offline** with the PayPal Card Reader — close deals in real time at
conferences, trade-show booths, and brick-and-mortar. (4) **Leverage business loans** —
because PayPal sees your monthly revenue flow, it can offer/qualify loans more easily
than a traditional bank, letting a fast-growing, profitable business fund more paid
advertising (Google/Facebook).

## Key claims

- As of 2019-01-09, Neil said adding PayPal as a checkout option raised his conversions
  by ~6% and that "little tweaks add up," while also building trust and enabling global
  payments. [self-reported; sponsored]
- As of 2019-01-09, Neil said switching from a single $997 flat charge to offering
  payment plans raised sales ~13% — even accounting for some cancellations, total
  revenue was higher than charging a flat fee upfront. [self-reported; sponsored]
- As of 2019-01-09, Neil framed the combined effect of the checkout option (+6%) and
  payment plans (+13%) as ~19% more in sales. [self-reported; sponsored]
- As of 2019-01-09, Neil recommended the PayPal Card Reader to accept money offline at
  conferences, exhibit booths, and brick-and-mortar to "close deals in real time," citing
  his own company's exhibit booths at conferences he speaks at.
- As of 2019-01-09, Neil said running revenue through PayPal makes business loans easier
  to qualify for than a bank, which lets a profitable business spend more on Google/
  Facebook advertising when each dollar spent returns more than a dollar.
