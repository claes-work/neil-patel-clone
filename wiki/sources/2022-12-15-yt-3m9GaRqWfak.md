# 7 Marketing Tactics That Provide Quick Results

## Summary
A quick-wins list explicitly positioned against his usual SEO/long-term-strategy
preaching: seven tactics for fast results. (1) Launch a referral program — cites an
18%-more-loyal stat for referred customers and self-reports NP Digital's own
referral/affiliate partnerships as "a large source of our revenue" (unspecified,
"more than a million dollars a year"), naming HasOffers and Ambassador as affiliate
tools. (2) Build a content calendar as the structural backbone of a blogging
strategy — doable "in just an hour." (3) Use VistaCreate (70,000+ templates, 70M
creative assets) for fast social visuals. (4) Give away a free tool — self-reports
Ubersuggest has 20,000+ natural backlinks and generates 2M+ monthly visitors from
being free, crediting it as a core driver of NP Digital's Inc. Magazine "21st fastest
growing companies" ranking and 40% of its consulting customers. (5) Batch social posts
(via Buffer/Hootsuite) — cites 27.2% more social engagement from consistent batch
posting, "forced" onto NP Digital clients. (6) Host a contest (via ViralSweep),
pointing to YouTube's early-days contests and live-sporting-event contests (Vegas
Golden Knights, NHL/football games) as validation that they work. (7) Write about
new/exciting topics early via Exploding Topics and AnswerThePublic (which has an alert
feature for emerging keywords), then draft with the Ubersuggest AI writer to save an
hour. Closes with an NP Digital plug.
