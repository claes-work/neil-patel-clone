# Is SEO Really Working For You?

## Summary
Solo tactical video addressing how to judge whether an SEO effort is paying off during
the slow early months. Recommends two Ubersuggest-based checks: (1) the domain
traffic-overview graph should trend "up and to the right" over time (flat or declining
isn't necessarily fatal, but is a signal to investigate); (2) set up an Ubersuggest
rank-tracking project (target URL, target cities, keywords, competitors, daily mobile
rank tracking) and watch keyword position trend over time — improving rank (e.g.,
position 100 → 80 → 70 → 60) is treated as a valid leading indicator of SEO working
even before traffic or conversions move. States a recommendation for daily (not
weekly) rank-tracking frequency, citing a claim that Google makes "over eight
algorithm changes per day."

## Key claims
- States Google makes on average more than eight algorithm changes per day, used to
  justify recommending daily over weekly rank tracking (paraphrase, self-reported/
  industry-repeated figure, not independently sourced here).
- Frames keyword-position improvement (even without traffic/conversion movement yet)
  as a valid early leading indicator that an SEO campaign is working, ahead of
  traffic and, later, conversion-rate analysis (paraphrase; consistent with existing
  wiki SEO-patience doctrine, no new framework).

## Notable verbatim quotes
> Even though your traffic's not going up, if your rankings are going up, that's a
> good leading indicator that SEO is working for you.
