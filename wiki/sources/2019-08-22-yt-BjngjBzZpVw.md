# How to Get The Most Out of Your Instagram Followers

## Summary

Neil's premise: Instagram doesn't let you link out and sell on every post, so you have to
extract value differently. His tactics: (1) **Use followers as custom/lookalike audiences for
ads** — quality followers are loyal, so build lookalikes to grow the base; he uses a **"well"
analogy** (build your own well once vs. buying bottled water forever). (2) **Post IGTV videos**
(Instagram is pushing IGTV, so they get more reach) that are both **educational AND mention
your product/service**. (3) **Leverage Stories with swipe-up** to drive people off Instagram
to your site to convert — an **animated GIF telling people to swipe up beats plain text**
(he's tested both; uses text + interactive GIF). (4) **Go Live** — Instagram notifies all
followers when you start, and an engaging, helpful live lets you sell/pitch a webinar right
then. (5) **Invest in proven content** — cut big pieces (like this video) into bite-size
posts, photos, and quotes, see what performs, do more of it, drop the rest. (6) **Create
engagement/interaction content** — ask for double-taps ("if you agree") to earn likes (more
likes → more likely to go viral) and reply to comments; a relationship built before a pitch
makes followers far more likely to buy than a stranger saying "buy this."
