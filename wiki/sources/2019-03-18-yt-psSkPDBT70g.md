# The Best Way to Get More Instagram Followers

## Summary

An 8-tip tactical playbook for growing Instagram followers, dated 2019 (title's "2023 Update" is a
re-upload label). The through-line: stop chasing perfection, post volume, and lean into video and
authentic micro-content. Tips: (1) stop stressing about perfect lighting/makeup — just post; (2)
stop posting what isn't working; (3) post videos (they out-engage text/images because Facebook and
Instagram favor video); (4) get creative with engagement prompts ("double tap if..."); (5) care
about your audience — reply to every comment; (6) post frequently — roughly once every 3–6 hours to
feed, and ~9 stories a day; (7) use micro-content — chop long talks/speeches into bite-size clips;
(8) be your real self, not the Photoshopped version. Contains dated platform-mechanics claims that
have since shifted.
