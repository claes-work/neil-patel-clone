# This Low Effort Social Strategy Brings in a Ton of ROI

## Summary
Recommends carousel ads as the highest-ROI, lowest-effort social format, framed
around "work smarter, not harder." Cites Statista: 4.59 billion social media users
worldwide. Gives an engagement-rate stat bank: carousel ads 1.9% engagement vs. 1.74%
for images and 1.45% for videos; carousels using all 10 slides exceed 2%; carousels
mixing images and videos hit 2.33% — the highest of any format cited. Notes only 88%
of carousels use images-only and 4% videos-only, implying most advertisers aren't
using the higher-performing mixed format. Cites carousel ads getting a 72% higher CTR
than single-image ads. Explains the mechanism: platforms (Facebook, Instagram,
TikTok) are incentivized to keep users on-platform, so a single static ad asking for
an immediate click has a high drop-off, whereas a carousel lets the advertiser
"sell" the product feature-by-feature within the platform itself (worked example:
an iPhone carousel walking through camera, battery life +50%, storage, front camera,
AR, and noise-cancellation one slide at a time) so that by the time the user clicks
through, they're already sold and ready to buy rather than needing to be convinced
on the landing page. States the tactic works across both B2B and B2C and "almost
every industry," and that it's used for NP Digital's own clients.

## Key claims
- Cites Statista: 4.59 billion social media users worldwide (as cited, dated
  2023-02-09).
- Cites (source unnamed): carousel-ad engagement rate 1.9% vs. images 1.74% and
  videos 1.45%; carousels using all 10 slides exceed 2% engagement; mixed
  image+video carousels reach 2.33% engagement — the single highest engagement
  figure among ad formats cited; 88% of carousels use images only and 4% videos
  only; carousel ads have a 72% higher CTR than single-image ads.
- States the platform-retention incentive (Facebook/Instagram/TikTok wanting to keep
  users on-platform) as the causal mechanism for why carousel/native-format ads
  outperform ads that ask for an immediate off-platform click.
- Self-reports using carousel ads for NP Digital's clients "across almost every
  industry, both B2B, B2C" (self-reported, dated 2023-02-09).
