# STOP Trying to Compete Against These Websites in SEO (And Finally Start Getting Traffic)

## Summary
A "who NOT to compete with" framework: three types of sites to stop treating as SEO
competitors — (1) huge indirect competitors (Amazon, Microsoft, eBay, Wikipedia,
Investopedia, even Google itself ranking for "SEO"); (2) sites that rank for head
terms purely on age/backlink volume but don't offer competing products/services
(recommends pursuing affiliate deals with these instead of fighting them); (3)
competitors with too-high domain authority to beat in the short run (accept them as
long-run targets only). Prescribes an Ubersuggest workflow to find winnable
opportunities instead: run competitors' URLs to see their top pages/keywords/linkers,
feed those keywords into the Keyword Ideas report (own-URL filtering shows which are
achievable *today*, not "in six months"), use the Backlink Opportunities report to find
sites linking to 3+ competitors but not you (highest-probability outreach targets), and
the Keywords Opportunity report for competitor keyword gaps. Closes with a Google
Search Console tactic: find pages with high impressions but low clicks, and work the
underlying keywords into title tag/meta description/body where relevant.

## Key claims
- Names Google itself as an example of an "indirect competitor" that outranks him for
  the term "SEO" — presented as an unbeatable category to simply accept rather than
  fight (self-reported framing, dated 2021-11-22) — seo.
- Explicit diagnosis for link-building frustration: outreach fails when targeting sites
  that don't link out to the space at all; the fix is to target sites already linking
  to 3+ competitors, since they've proven willing to link to peers (paraphrase, dated
  2021-11-22) — seo. Consistent with the backlink-opportunities-report tactic logged on
  [[wiki/sources/2021-11-01-yt-QnBHcjQOIBM]] and earlier pages; reinforced, not new.
- Recommends striking affiliate deals with high-ranking non-competitor sites rather than
  trying to outrank them (self-reported, dated 2021-11-22) — seo, agency-entrepreneurship.
  A monetization angle on "don't compete" not previously logged this explicitly.
