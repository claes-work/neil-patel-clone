# How Kylie Jenner Built Her Empire — Instagram Unlocked, Module 2, Lesson 3 (course COMPLETES)

## Summary
Final lesson of the two-week Instagram Unlocked course (launched
[[wiki/sources/2020-09-17-yt-IJkYLI1oM4s]]), built around case studies. Opens with an
Instagram-founder quote on focus ("Focusing on one thing and doing it really, really
well can get you very far"). Case study 1: Kylie Cosmetics/Kylie Jenner — cites
(third-party, unverified) figures: ~$900M net worth, founded 2015, grew to 100M+
Instagram followers, 2019 revenue of $300M, Coty Inc.'s $600M purchase of a 51% stake,
12 employees, zero outside investment, and a table of celebrity per-post earnings (The
Rock ~$1M, Kylie Jenner "almost a million," etc.). Case study 2: Frank Body ($20M in
sales in one year), attributing its success to tight niche/content-theme focus, a
personified brand voice targeted at young women, and consistent user-submitted-photo
reposting. Covers community monetization (business profile, Instagram Insights,
Facebook product tagging, influencer partnerships/co-lives) and core analytics metrics
(follower growth rate, engagements/comments per post, website traffic, link clicks,
Story engagement). Gives a platform-by-platform repurposing guide: Facebook (events,
timelines, video/photo; direct site links underperform), LinkedIn (case studies,
white papers, articles), Instagram (personal-life content, products, quotes, video,
inspirational, swipe-up), Twitter (storytelling, news, quick tips), Pinterest
(how-to, aspirational photography — interior decorating, cooking). States he
personally shares content daily across all his platforms. Repeats the posting-
frequency floor (48 hours worst case). Addresses buying established Instagram accounts:
says it's technically permitted and he knows people who do it (mentions socialtradia
as a service), but he personally doesn't — "I'd rather build them from scratch... I
found when you buy an account, the engagement just isn't the same." Closes with a
passion appeal ("If you don't love what you do, go do something else. Life is too
short") and course wrap-up instructions (Instagram Taskmaster Worksheet at
`neilpatel.com/training`).
