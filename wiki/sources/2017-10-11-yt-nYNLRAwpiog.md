# How to Get More Customers to Contact YOU (Hint: Optimize Your Contact Page)

## Summary
Neil argues that a well-optimized contact page directly increases how many people reach out, which in turn increases customers. He invites viewers to copy his own contact page outright, framing it as beautiful and heavily optimized ("good marketers borrow, great marketers steal"). He shares the three strategies behind it: create a story about yourself or your company to encourage people to contact you; explicitly state who you do and don't want contacting you so you filter out irrelevant inquiries and attract more relevant ones; and break the contact page down with a dropdown that routes each inquiry to the right person depending on what the visitor needs help with, so Neil and his team don't waste time on irrelevant requests. He stresses the page is highly visual, which he says helps engage visitors and make them want to reach out.
