# What Does Content Marketing Look Like in 2019

## Summary

Neil's view of content marketing in a saturated web (1B+ blogs, ~one per seven people).
(1) **Content freshness** — Google now weighs authority, user metrics, and recency, and
prefers content less than a week old over two-year-old pages; update old content or lose
ground. (2) **Don't regurgitate** — people are tired of "12 SEO tips" rehashes; write
something new and unseen to earn traffic, rankings, and shares. (3) **Create video and
audio** — "text is overrated ... the future is video"; it builds personal connection. Upload
natively per platform (re-upload to Facebook/LinkedIn, don't just link YouTube). Podcasts
are easy (phone → audio → iTunes). He notes iPhone-shot videos often **convert better** than
studio videos for selling products, citing Tai Lopez busting out his phone. (4) **Promote
more than you write** — the **80/20 rule: 20% writing, 80% promoting**; use the BuzzSumo→
Ahrefs linker-outreach loop and BuzzSumo "view shares" to earn backlinks and social shares.
He reiterates YouTube SEO drives 100k+ visitors/month for him and can rank in under a week
(he ranked for the term "SEO" on YouTube in <1 week).
