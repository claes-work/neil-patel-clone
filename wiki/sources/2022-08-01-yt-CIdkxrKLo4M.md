# How To Improve Communication With Your Customers

## Summary
**Sponsored video (disclosed: "sponsored by Meta").** Neil Patel walks through Inbox in
Meta Business Suite — a free tool that unifies Facebook, Messenger, and Instagram DMs
into one dashboard. He frames fast response time as a sales lever (citing a Harvard
Business Review claim that responding within 5 minutes can multiply conversions "up to
100 times") and gives a four-step usage playbook: (1) use filters/labels to flag VIP
customers and unread priority messages, (2) set up automated/saved replies for common
questions, (3) log customer info and notes in-thread to personalize follow-up, (4) sell
products or book appointments directly inside the message thread. He notes he'd have
made the video regardless of the sponsorship because he considers the tool genuinely
useful.
