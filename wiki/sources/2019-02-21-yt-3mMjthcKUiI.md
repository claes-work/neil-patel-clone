# How To Maximize Your Reach and Sales Using Video Marketing

## Summary

Neil's ordered workflow for driving traffic and sales from video, pitched as faster than
SEO. He notes he now produces more video each week than text content. Steps: (1) **Use
Ubersuggest** (free) to find popular keywords/topics so you don't make videos nobody wants.
(2) **Record on your phone** (video, not audio-only), break down the popular topic, stay
engaging — "always smile" (a note his cameraman reminds him of). (3) **End with a call to
action** driving people to your site — he plugs his agency; without a spoken CTA at the end,
getting sales from the video is very hard. (4) **Get an SRT file from Rev.com** — transcribe
the video and upload the caption file. (5) **Upload the video + SRT to every social network**
(Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube at minimum) natively; captions matter because these sites autoplay
muted, especially for people at work, so viewers read along and engagement rises. (6) **Include
the right keywords** in the title, description, and copy before publishing. (7) **Promote in
the first hour** — blast your email list and send a push (via a tool like Subscribers) to drive
early traction and virality. (8) **Respond to all comments** to build connection, which makes
viewers more likely to buy later.
