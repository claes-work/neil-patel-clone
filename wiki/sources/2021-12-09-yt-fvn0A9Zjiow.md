# One FREE Tool to STEAL Your Competitor's Best Keywords

## Summary
A short Ubersuggest tutorial: use the Traffic Analyzer report on a competitor's URL to
see their top pages and ranking keywords, then filter for keywords with at least
200-1,000+ monthly searches and an SEO difficulty under 40-50 (fast-win keywords). Also
run the Similar Websites report to surface other sites targeting the same keywords and
surface "keyword opportunities" (terms they rank for that you don't). Bucket the
resulting keyword list into topical groups (e.g. a dog-food site splitting into
miniature-dog, overweight-dog, organic-dog buckets), then run candidate keywords
through Ubersuggest's Keyword Ideas report with the site's own URL entered to filter
down to keywords realistically rankable today. Restates the niche-authority doctrine:
focus content narrowly on one sub-topic (e.g. SEO before expanding to email, CRO, paid
ads) so Google recognizes topical authority, which is framed as how smaller sites beat
high-authority incumbents like Wikipedia without matching their backlink counts.
