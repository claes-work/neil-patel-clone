# How to Grow Your Website in a Competitive Space | Neil Patel

## Summary

A seven-tip playbook for growing traffic in a competitive space, front-loaded with
conversion foundations before traffic tactics. (1) **Collect emails** — "the money is in
the list"; a bigger list converts more, and you can start free with hellobar.com.
(2) **Optimize for conversions first** — most companies neglect A/B testing, yet it is
the best ROI from marketing spend; tools like Crazy Egg, Optimizely, Hotjar, VWO show
where people click, how far they scroll, and where the page confuses. (3) **Write
exceptional posts** (transcript garbles "10x"/"end-up" blog post) — don't chase quantity
against competitors' 1,000 posts; write fresh, unique, high-quality content, link out to
sources/competitors, then email everyone you linked to and ask them to share. (4) **Build
a push-notification list** with subscribers.com and drip-feed subscribers (Neil's first
drip promotes Ubersuggest; he also promotes NP Digital this way). (5) **Spy on
competitors** with SEMrush (organic + paid keywords), Ahrefs (who links to them not you),
BuzzSumo (their popular articles) — do more of what works. (6) **Trade shows / speaking**
— generate business offline; apply to every industry event, "it's a numbers game."
(7) **Get press** via performance-based PR (prserve.com) for mentions on TechCrunch,
BuzzFeed, Entrepreneur, Forbes.
