# 4 eCommerce SEO Techniques for Beginners (Ranking Your Products and Getting FREE Google Traffic)

## Summary
A four-step ecommerce SEO tutorial: (1) list on **Google Merchant** for free Google
Shopping-tab exposure, noting many ecommerce platforms auto-generate/submit the
shopping feed; (2) product-page SEO — target transactional-intent keywords (e.g. "best
laptop for college students") over high-volume head terms, sourced via Ubersuggest's
Questions/Prepositions/Comparisons reports; include brand/model in title-meta, add
structured data and FAQ content, use real (not fake) testimonials, write unique
product descriptions rather than reusing manufacturer copy (flagged as a common
duplicate-content mistake), use canonical tags/301s instead of deleting discontinued
product pages, and optimize for mobile (breadcrumbs, imagery/video, even 3D views);
(3) minimize load time/maximize site speed (Google PageSpeed Insights or Ubersuggest's
Site Audit, tied to Core Web Vitals); (4) use short, keyword-relevant URLs, avoiding
model-number strings and overly long URLs (argued to hurt ranking for short-tail
queries within the same long URL). Closes citing that over 40% of NP Digital's clients
are in ecommerce.
