# The 3 SEO Tools I Use to Rank #1 on Google | Neil Patel

## Summary

Neil argues you don't need 5–20 tools; three (plus a bonus) do the job. (1) **Ubersuggest**
(free): take head terms you already rank for and pull the long-tail variations, then blend
them naturally into that content — not keyword-stuffing, but expanding with a few
paragraphs where needed — and within ~30 days you rank for the long-tails too. (2)
**Google Search Console**: don't just use it for error alerts (404s, etc.) — use it to
A/B test title tags. If searchers naturally click the #2 listing over #1, Google reads
that as relevance and promotes it; so check which page-one terms are in your title tag,
add the missing ones, and make titles more appealing with action words/verbs and
magazine-headline inspiration to earn more clicks. (3) **Ahrefs** (his favorite paid
tool) and its **Link Intersect** feature: enter three top competitors plus your own
domain to find sites linking to competitors but not you — sites linking to multiple
competitors are receptive to linking out, so they're the best outreach targets. Bonus:
**Subscribers** (push notifications) to bring people back repeatedly — invoking the "rule
of seven" (seven brand exposures build connection/conversion) and Google's growing
emphasis on brand mentions, with Google Trends to track brand size vs. competitors.
