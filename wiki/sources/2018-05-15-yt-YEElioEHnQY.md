# 3 Unorthodox SEO Tips to Increase Organic Website Traffic (RIGHT NOW)

## Summary

A Neil-fronted monologue giving three "unorthodox" ways to boost rankings faster than conventional SEO. (1) **Buy other properties/websites in your space** — sites that already rank but aren't making money can be bought "for pennies on the dollar," rebuilt, and 301-redirected into your domain, which is cheaper than earning rankings from scratch. His example: buying Ubersuggest for "a hundred and something thousand dollars," 301-ing it to neilpatel.com/ubersuggest, adding features, releasing it free, and gaining ~400,000 extra page views/month plus backlinks. (2) **Go after people who promote your competitors** — approach bloggers/affiliates already promoting a competitor (his example: convincing AWeber promoters to add ConvertKit) with higher payouts or free product access; when people genuinely like the product they often link for free without being asked (no-follow paid links, but organic mentions can be do-follow). He cites early Crazy Egg giving free accounts to TechCrunch/Mashable, who then wrote about it. (3) **Rank in non-English markets**, where competition and content scarcity mean a brand-new site can rank in one to two months. He says 51% of his revenue is from overseas, so they translate top content manually, use hreflang to signal language/region, and build cheap links/social shares in those regions; Brazil traffic is now ~50% the size of his US traffic. He cites a friend's wife's site "Esposes Online" [likely a caption garble] hitting 100,000 visitors/month within six months on fewer than ten backlinks.
