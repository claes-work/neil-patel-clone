# Welcome to Ecommerce Unlocked: Your Free Ecommerce Marketing Course

## Summary
Launch video for **"eCommerce Unlocked"**, a free four-week Neil-fronted course
(neilpatel.com/training), released Mon/Thu/Sat, three videos per week, four modules
(Module 1: getting started, Module 2: SEO/CRO, Module 3: sales channels, Module 4:
marketing channels — confirmed by the subsequent lessons in this batch). Neil opens
with a brief credential recap (Crazy Egg, KISSmetrics, Quick Sprout, neilpatel.com,
NP Digital) and frames the course as distilled from working with e-commerce clients
across verticals (cars, hotel bookings, marketplaces like eBay, Amazon, American
Greetings). He recounts starting his career "working at a theme park" with no formal
marketing training, learning "by making a lot of mistakes." Covers SMART goals
(specific/measurable/achievable/relevant/time-bound) with a worksheet, sets course
expectations (step-by-step, no skipping, take notes, implement), and closes with a
1995 Steve Jobs quote on e-commerce's future scale ("billions and billions... up to
tens of billions of dollars") which Neil says undersold the actual trillion-dollar-plus
outcome.
