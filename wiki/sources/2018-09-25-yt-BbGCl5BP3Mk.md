# One (Simple) Hack to Get More Website Traffic FAST

## Summary

Solo tactical. Neil's hook: one simple hack has generated him "over 40,000 visitors in the
last 30 days." The hack is **browser push notifications via Subscribers.com** — a free tool
(no premium tier) that works for any website. It prompts visitors ("Would you like to get
notifications from neilpatel.com?"); those who accept can be pinged any time you publish new
content. From the Subscribers dashboard you send a notification and see how many
subscribers received it, clicked it, and returned to your site, plus who's active vs
inactive (unsubscribed). Setup is simple — enter your URL, then install either the WordPress
plugin or a JavaScript snippet. His learnings for getting the most traffic: because opting
in and out is easy, you get **a lot of unsubscribes**, so **monetize the subscriber quickly**
with a **drip sequence** (Subscribers has a drip feature: send messages minutes, a day, or
days after subscribing). His own 3-message drip on neilpatel.com: (1) on subscribe →
promote **Ubersuggest** (his marketing tool); (2) two days later → his **ad agency** (drives
leads/revenue); (3) → subscribe to his **YouTube** channel — with ongoing website/blog
promotion in between. Cadence rule: even light senders get unsubscribes, so send **once
every 2–3 days** — daily is "too obsessive." He cites his own result: neilpatel.com has
**over 100,000 subscribers**, calling it the simplest, easiest, free way to get more
traffic.
