# How to Generate 10K visitors from a Brand New Blog In Under 6 Months

## Summary

Neil gives a repeatable playbook to take a brand-new blog to **10,000+ visits per month within
six months**. (1) **Skyscraper technique** — use **SEMrush** or **Ubersuggest** on competitors'
URLs to find their traffic-driving articles, then write versions "five, 10 times more detailed"
so people prefer and link to yours. (2) **Email everyone you linked out to** — you naturally
cite sources when writing; email them a short "I linked to you, feel free to share" note; they
usually won't link back but will share socially. (3) **BuzzSumo** — find who shared competitors'
top articles ("view shares") and outreach them that yours covers more. (4) **Collect emails**
with **Hello Bar** or **MailChimp** (popups/sliders, free). (5) **Collect push-notification
subscribers** with **PushCrew** or **subscribers.com**. Then **blast** every new post to both
lists and **repeat the cycle** (blast → push → outreach linkers → outreach sharers). He says
it's "not hard" if you target a big enough market and publish **at least two, ideally three,
blog posts per week**.
