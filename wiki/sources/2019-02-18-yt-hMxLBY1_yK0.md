# How to Growth Hack Your Personal Brand

## Summary

Neil's step-by-step playbook for building a personal brand faster than the usual "it
takes forever" wisdom. He opens by claiming a strong personal brand can earn over $1M/yr,
and that he himself generates over $1M/yr **just from speaking fees** because of his brand.
Seven growth hacks: (1) **Pick a big niche** — use Google Trends and make sure your niche
is bigger than "digital marketing" (his benchmark comparison term); a niche only 10 people
care about has no money in it. (2) **Leverage video** — video over YouTube/Facebook/LinkedIn
builds the in-person feel faster and more effectively than offline events; shoot on your
phone and use the right keywords (via Ubersuggest) so people find the videos. (3) **Interview
other experts** — being seen next to established experts (he name-checks Gary Vaynerchuk, Tony
Robbins, Seth Godin) borrows their credibility; a 10-minute Skype interview posted across
socials brands you as a peer. Pick experts *within your field*, not just anyone famous.
(4) **Speak at four conferences a year** (one per quarter) — apply to every event in your
space, some say yes; attendees photograph you and share it, compounding your expert brand.
(5) **Help people** genuinely (Facebook groups, Twitter, blog comments) — do it because you
care, not to farm a brand. (6) **Stand out by being yourself** — find what's unique about you;
his example: Gary Vaynerchuk cusses naturally, not to be cool. (7) **Be patient** — it takes
3–4 years, not 1–2, but tracking your name in Google Trends should show up-and-to-the-right
growth each quarter as a leading indicator.
