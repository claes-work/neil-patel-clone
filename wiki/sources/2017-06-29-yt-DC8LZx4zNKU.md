# Should You Link Out to Your Competitors? #ASKNEIL

## Summary
A short #ASKNEIL video in which Neil Patel answers whether a site should link out to its competitors, and argues emphatically yes — even to direct competitors. He grounds the advice in his own experience at Kissmetrics, where he linked out to competitor Mixpanel in blog posts whenever it was relevant and beneficial to the reader. His reasoning has two prongs: doing what is best for visitors builds loyalty and growth, and outbound links to competitors and other sites in your space give Google topical/neighborhood signals that help it understand and rank your site. He adds that linking out often prompts those sites to link back, further reinforcing rankings.

## Key claims
As of 2017-06-29, Neil states:
- You should link out to your competitors — the answer is yes even when someone is a direct competitor (as of 2017-06-29).
- Link out when it is relevant, makes sense, and benefits the reader or user, because doing what is best for visitors helps you grow (as of 2017-06-29).
- At his last startup Kissmetrics, he linked out to competitor Mixpanel in many blog posts (as of 2017-06-29).
- One benefit of linking out to competitors is building reader loyalty (as of 2017-06-29).
- ★ A benefit most people don't realize: linking out to other websites, especially competitors, tells Google what "neighborhood" your site belongs in, what it's about, and what it's topically relevant to — and with that information Google can rank you better; it's all about giving Google signals (as of 2017-06-29).
- He also links out to non-competitor informational sites in his space that have amazing information or original research, regardless of whether their products are similar to his (as of 2017-06-29).
- Linking out signals to Google that NeilPatel.com is about marketing in general (not just about Neil Patel), so if the linked sites rank well, Google should rank his site well too (as of 2017-06-29).
- ★ Because he links out to other sites, many of them link back to him, which further supports ranking his site highly — just as the competitors are ranked highly (as of 2017-06-29).
