# 7 eCommerce Email Marketing Tactics That Work Like a Charm

## Summary

Neil frames email as the lifeblood of e-commerce (citing Overstock getting the majority of
sales from email) and lists seven tactics. (1) **Scrub your list** — stop emailing
non-openers; it lifts deliverability, inbox placement, opens, clicks, and sales;
ConvertKit auto-scrubs, whereas Mailchimp resists it (scrubbing lowers their revenue).
(2) **Trigger-based emails** — abandoned-cart emails about the *specific* products left
behind (with reviews), not a generic "check out our store." (3) **Time-based emails** —
send at the same time of day the person first opted in (usually within an hour), since
unopened emails pile up and get ignored. (4) **Promotional emails** — pre-write Black
Friday/Cyber Monday/holiday campaigns; peak seasons can drive 25–30% of yearly sales.
(5) **Short, text-based emails** — image-heavy emails get filed under Gmail/Outlook
Promotions; text reads as a friend. (6) **Upsells/downsells by email** — most buyers skip
the on-checkout upsell, so follow up by email a week or two after delivery, more in-depth.
(7) **Combine with push notifications** (Subscribers) for cart reminders and offers.
