# 6 Advanced SEO Techniques To Use

## Summary

Seven "advanced" SEO plays that go beyond on-page and links. (1) **Know your users** —
Google ranks what's best for users, measured through **user metrics** (back-button/
pogo-sticking after a click, time on site, bounce rate, pages/visitor, load speed); survey
users with SurveyMonkey/Hotjar to fix what drives them away, which lifts both conversions
and rankings. (2) **Schema markup** (star ratings/reviews in the SERP) via the Yoast SEO
plugin — no coding needed. (3) **Expand your most popular pages** — take a page already
ranking, pull its long-tail phrases from Ubersuggest, and integrate them so a 3,000-word
post grows toward 10,000 words, earning extra traffic in <30 days with little work.
(4) **Send link juice to lower-ranked pages** by internal-linking from your strong pages.
(5) **Animated infographics** — GIFs inside infographics ("how a car engine works," "how
fast do cheetahs run") go viral and attract backlinks; he cites a creator whose animated
"how a car engine works" got 1M+ visitors with zero marketing. (6) **Roundup posts**
(e.g., "31 SEO tips from 31 experts") — participants share and link back. (7) **Prune and
crop** — cut dead-weight non-ranking pages so authority flows to pages that do rank.
