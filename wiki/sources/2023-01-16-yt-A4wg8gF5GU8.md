# Do This Email Marketing Strategy for 100 Days

## Summary
A 5-step, 100-day email-marketing plan aimed at fixing poor open/click-through
rates. Opens citing 89% of marketers use email as their primary lead-gen
channel, and that 22% of marketers report click-through rates over 20% versus
a 10.69% industry average. Steps: (1) scrub the list — remove non-engaging
subscribers, since low engagement from part of a list (illustrated with a
"John"/"Betty" example) pushes emails into spam/promotions folders even for
engaged recipients; (2) personalize messages — cites internal data that
personalizing subject lines lifts open rates by up to 74%, including using the
recipient's name and casual, lowercase, buddy-style subject lines (e.g. "John,
check this out") rather than formal business phrasing — with the caveat not to
"bait" recipients with misleading lines; (3) segment the audience — cites 32%
of marketers rank segmentation as a top organizational objective for the next
12 months; states segmenting generates more revenue than not segmenting,
illustrated by not re-pitching an already-purchased item to a repeat buyer;
(4) test everything — cites 50%+ of marketers use A/B testing to boost
conversions; explicitly caveats that NP Digital client results won't
generalize 100% to every industry/list, hence the need to test; (5) automate
via trigger-based emails — self-reports seeing open rates as high as 49% for
trigger emails (e.g. post-purchase upsell/downsell messages), the highest
figure cited in the video. Closes with an npdigital.com plug.

## Key claims
- Cites (source unnamed, as cited, dated 2023-01-16): 89% of marketers use
  email as their primary lead-generation channel; 22% of marketers report
  click-through rates over 20% versus a 10.69% industry average.
- Self-reports (NP Digital data, dated 2023-01-16): personalizing subject
  lines (including recipient's name, casual/lowercase phrasing) lifts open
  rates by up to 74%.
- Cites (source unnamed, as cited, dated 2023-01-16): 32% of marketers say
  segmenting their email database is a top objective for the next 12 months.
- Self-reports (NP Digital data, dated 2023-01-16): segmenting an email list
  generates more revenue than not segmenting.
- Cites (source unnamed, as cited, dated 2023-01-16): more than 50% of
  marketers use A/B testing to boost email conversions.
- Self-reports (dated 2023-01-16): trigger-based emails (e.g. post-purchase
  upsell/downsell) have produced open rates as high as 49%, the highest of
  any tactic cited in the video.
