# 3 Ways to Save Time on Email Outreach (Quickly Reach More Bloggers and Influencers!)

## Summary
A short outreach video in which Neil Patel argues that getting guest posts, backlinks, and
content shares requires email outreach, which is time-consuming — and gives two tools to
speed it up. (1) Yesware provides email templates, shows you when people open your emails,
and lets you schedule sends; the open-tracking tells you when to change your opening line
(people opening but not replying) and lets you stop emailing prospects who never open,
saving time. (2) Boomerang automatically follows up when people don't respond within a set
time, so you're not the one manually chasing leads — and it's smart enough to suppress the
canned follow-up if the person does reply quickly, so a fast responder doesn't get an
awkward automated email. Use both to save time on outreach.

## Key claims
As of 2017-09-01, Neil states:
- To get guest posts, backlinks, and content shares as a marketer you have to do email
  outreach, but outreach is really time-consuming (as of 2017-09-01).
- Tool 1 — Yesware provides email templates, open-tracking, and scheduling; open-tracking
  signals when to change your opening line (opens but no replies) and lets you stop
  emailing people who never open, so you don't email the same non-opener 5-10 times (as of
  2017-09-01).
- Tool 2 — Boomerang auto-follows-up when people don't respond within a set time, so you
  don't have to chase leads manually (as of 2017-09-01).
- Boomerang is sophisticated enough to not send the canned automatic follow-up if the
  person already responded within the window — avoiding sending a fast responder an
  awkward automated email (as of 2017-09-01).

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "As a marketer, if you want more guest posts, backlinks, if you want people to share your content, what do you have to do? You have to start doing email outreach. The problem with email outreach, though, is it's really time-consuming."

> "Why would you keep emailing the same person five or 10 times if they haven't opened up any of your emails? Yesware will save you time. It'll show you who to email and who not to email."

> "Boomerang is sophisticated enough where it knows when to send the person an email, and when not to send them an email."
