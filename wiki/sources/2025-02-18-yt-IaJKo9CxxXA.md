# How to Get Rich in the New Era of A.I. (2025)

## Summary
Neil Patel argues that the 2025 "AI Gold Rush" — which he compares in scale to the arrival of Google and smartphones — is an opening for anyone to build an AI-services business, even starting from zero. He first frames niche selection: start with something you know and are passionate about, check the market cap of established companies in that vertical (the bigger, ideally into the billions, the better the opportunity), and begin with a subset of the market before expanding, citing Facebook (originally Ivy League students) and Amazon (originally only books) as precedents. He then walks through five money-making opportunities. First, AI agents — described as the "body" to the "brain" of models like ChatGPT, Gemini and Grok — which can be built without coding on platforms like CrewAI to handle sales lead scoring, marketing personalization, analytics, and finance/lifetime-value analysis. Second, translation services using tools like ElevenLabs to localize websites, content and video for a globalized market. Third, AI podcast services: turning blogs and newsletters into podcasts via voice cloning, and turning long-form clips into short-form via Opus. Fourth, AI data visualization and infographics (via a Piktochart-style tool) that earn backlinks and lift conversions. Fifth, AI website building on platforms like 10Web, targeting newly formed LLCs, with SEO and blog content as upsells. He closes by stressing that getting customers matters most, recounting how he cold-called advertisers at age 16 and offered free-unless-satisfied work to build case studies, and recommending targeting recently funded companies on Crunchbase.

## Key claims
- The AI "Gold Rush" is here and is comparable in significance to the arrival of Google and smartphones; vertical AI agents are about to start replacing entire teams (as of 2025-02-18).
- The rate of model and technique improvement shows the industry has not hit a scaling wall, with things getting progressively better every few months (as of 2025-02-18).
- Before making money, you must pick a niche for your AI services; the best way is to start with what you know and are passionate about, or you will burn out (as of 2025-02-18).
- When evaluating a vertical, look up the other companies in it and check their market cap — the larger the market cap (into the billions, tens of billions, or hundreds of billions), the bigger the opportunity (as of 2025-02-18).
- You should start with a subset of a market and expand to the larger market later; going after everyone at the beginning is too time-intensive and costly (as of 2025-02-18).
- Facebook was originally only for Ivy League university students and Amazon originally only sold books, illustrating the start-narrow-then-expand pattern (as of 2025-02-18).
- Opportunity 1 is AI agents: if ChatGPT, Gemini and Grok are the "brain," AI agents are the "body" that can go and do things on their own (as of 2025-02-18).
- Hundreds of thousands of businesses are looking to use AI agents this year, and you can build them with no existing coding or tech expertise using sites like CrewAI (crewai.com) (as of 2025-02-18).
- Sales AI agents can score leads by analyzing interactions, engagements and patterns so teams know which leads to prioritize and which to ignore (as of 2025-02-18).
- Marketing AI agents can personalize copy and content per individual and appeal to different audience segments, improving conversion rates (as of 2025-02-18).
- Analytics AI agents can pinpoint which traffic generates the best revenue and identify high-value repeat customers versus one-time buyers, showing where to focus marketing (as of 2025-02-18).
- Finance AI agents can tell you the lifetime value of customers and campaigns and reveal which campaigns create the highest LTV versus which are unprofitable, so you can focus on profitability, not just revenue growth (as of 2025-02-18).
- Opportunity 2 is translation services; the world is increasingly globalized and the most successful brands are international (e.g., Tesla, Coca-Cola, Nike, Mr. Beast's multi-language channel, Apple's multi-language website) (as of 2025-02-18).
- Neil Patel's own agency website, NP Digital, is in multiple languages because that helps it make more revenue (as of 2025-02-18).
- There are more people (and, combined, more money) overseas than in the United States, even though the US has the highest GDP (as of 2025-02-18).
- Translation is high potential because most people only know the languages they speak; you can use ElevenLabs to translate and transcribe content and audio into different languages for businesses (as of 2025-02-18).
- Opportunity 3 is AI podcast services; the global podcast market is projected to grow from $36.2 billion in 2024 to $47.83 billion in 2025, a compound annual growth rate of 31.8%, and to reach $134.4 billion by 2029 (as of 2025-02-18).
- You can turn people's blog articles and newsletters into podcasts by cloning their voice and feeding written content into text-to-voice software, and earn bonus cash by handling their publishing and marketing (as of 2025-02-18).
- You can upload people's long-form clips into Opus AI to get short-form clips for social media; uploading the same long-form clip multiple times yields more short-form recommendations to pick the best from (as of 2025-02-18).
- Opportunity 4 is AI data visualization — beautiful images and infographics that make complex data easy to understand (as of 2025-02-18).
- Companies pay for infographics because they go viral, generate backlinks that help them rank higher on Google, and increase conversion rates when people understand complex information (as of 2025-02-18).
- You can create infographics through AI by putting your data sets into a Picto chart tool (as of 2025-02-18).
- Opportunity 5 is AI website building, described as very easy; you can target newly formed LLCs who just want something that works and looks decent, using platforms like 10Web (as of 2025-02-18).
- Because website building has a lot of competition, you must get good enough to be better than 99% of people by building many websites yourself — spending three to four hours learning which prompts give good versus terrible outputs (as of 2025-02-18).
- You can earn bonus cash by providing SEO optimization and blog content for clients' websites, since companies want traffic without paying for Google ads (as of 2025-02-18).
- It is easier to get an existing customer to pay you more than to find a new customer (as of 2025-02-18).
- Getting customers is the most important part of any of these AI business models (as of 2025-02-18).
- At 16, Neil Patel would run home after school, get on Google around 3:00, and cold-call people spending money on ads to offer services; most said no (as of 2025-02-18).
- He gained traction by telling people to pay only if they liked what he produced, then used the resulting case studies to charge for work upfront (as of 2025-02-18).
- If starting from scratch, he would target recently funded companies on Crunchbase, because raising money makes investors expect faster growth and those companies value time most, making them more likely to pay (as of 2025-02-18).
