# Paid Ads VS SEO: Which is Better for Lead Generation?

## Summary
A structured pros/cons comparison of paid ads vs. SEO for lead generation, closing with
"do both." Paid-ads pros: fast results, fast/rich data, granular targeting; cons:
requires ongoing spend, cost-per-lead tends to rise over time as campaigns scale.
SEO pros: scalable/compounding, works without ongoing spend once ranked, cites a
Forrester Consulting (2020) study that SEO traffic has higher customer lifetime value
than paid, a HubSpot stat that 61% of B2B marketers say SEO/organic generates more
leads than any other channel, and organic's ~31.7% CTR (Brian Dean/Backlinko, 2019) at
#1 vs. Google Ads' ~3.17% average CTR (WordStream) — Ubersuggest's own recent pull puts
organic CTR above 24% globally. SEO cons: slow (three-month minimum for any results),
increasingly competitive, content-heavy pages convert worse than dedicated PPC landing
pages, and vulnerable to sudden algorithm changes. Walks a worked example (Las Vegas
apartment-rental keyword) comparing modeled PPC cost-per-lead (~$33.85) against modeled
SEO traffic value if ranked #1 (~$13,221/month in equivalent paid-traffic value).
Conclusion: SEO has the better long-run ROI, but pay-per-click remains worth running
in parallel wherever it's profitable — not merely as a bridge until SEO ramps up.

## Key claims
- SEO has higher customer lifetime value than paid traffic, per Forrester Consulting
  (2020) (third-party stat, dated 2022-02-12) — seo, analytics-cro.
- 61% of B2B marketers say SEO/organic search generates more leads than any other
  marketing channel, per HubSpot (third-party stat, dated 2022-02-12) — seo.
- Organic result #1 gets ~31.7% average CTR (Brian Dean/Backlinko, 2019) vs. Google
  Ads' ~3.17% search CTR / 0.46% display CTR (WordStream) — first-party Ubersuggest data
  pulled at time of filming shows organic CTR still above 24% globally for most
  keywords (mixed third-party + self-reported, dated 2022-02-12) — seo, paid-ads.
- Explicit position: he does not treat PPC as merely a stopgap while SEO ramps —
  profitable paid spend should continue indefinitely alongside SEO, run in parallel for
  the long run (opinion, dated 2022-02-12) — paid-ads, seo, agency-entrepreneurship.
- Worked example: for a Las Vegas realtor targeting "Las Vegas apartments" (33,100
  monthly searches per Ubersuggest, $1.26 CPC), modeled Google Ads cost-per-lead is
  ~$33.85; ranking #1 organically for the same keyword is modeled at ~$13,221/month in
  equivalent paid-traffic value at ~10,493 monthly visitors (self-reported worked
  example, dated 2022-02-12) — seo, paid-ads, analytics-cro.
