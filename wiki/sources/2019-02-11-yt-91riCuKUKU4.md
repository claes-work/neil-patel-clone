# Top 5 Digital Marketing Conferences You Should Attend

## Summary

Neil's five recommended digital-marketing events and why each is worth it. (1) **Traffic &
Conversion Summit** (San Diego; created by **Ryan Deiss**) — cutting-edge traffic/
conversion tactics; Neil's angle: it draws aggressive ("shady") cutting-edge marketers
alongside mostly legitimate businesses, so you can take their *ethical* tactics and apply
them to your legitimate business. (2) **Affiliate Summit** — affiliate marketers only get
paid on a sale/conversion and spend their own money, so their tactics are battle-tested.
(3) **Affiliate World** — same value, with editions across Asia, Europe, etc. (4) **SMX
Advanced** (Seattle) — advanced SEO and social techniques, with Google/Facebook speakers
previewing the future of search and social. (5) **CXL** (by ConversionXL) — conversion
optimization, funnels, and checkout bumps to extract more revenue per visitor as Google/
Facebook ad costs rise. Recurring theme: learn from the most aggressive performance
marketers, then apply their tactics ethically.
