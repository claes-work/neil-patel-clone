# The Best Digital Marketing Strategy You've Never Tried

## Summary
Advocates browser push notifications as an underused, zero-ad-spend channel. States it
drives him "over 3,000 visitors a day" (~120,000/month). Argues push notifications avoid
the personal-information friction of email/SMS opt-ins (no email address or phone number
required — the browser handles delivery via a one-click "allow"). Describes running a
drip-style push sequence (tool: subscribers.com) analogous to an email autoresponder: new
subscribers first get pushed NP Digital agency-services messaging, then Ubersuggest
software messaging, then ongoing notifications for each new YouTube video and blog post.
Closes citing NP Digital as "performance marketing agency of the year."
