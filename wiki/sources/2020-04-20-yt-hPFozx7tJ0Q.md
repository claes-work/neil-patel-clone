# 14 Chrome Extensions That Will Save HOURS of Marketing Work

## Summary
A named tool-stack rundown of 14 Chrome extensions Neil says he uses regularly, grouped
by function. Content: Grammarly (Neil discloses "I have terrible grammar" and uses it
in place of an editor), Buzzsumo (in-page content-performance metrics for real-time
ideation). SEO: Keyword Surfer (successor to the once-free Keyword Everywhere; volume +
related keywords + estimated domain traffic + top-10 backlink counts), MozBar (on-site
SEO overview: domain/page authority, backlinks, social engagement), SimilarWeb (traffic
sources, engagement metrics, audience demographics, ad-spend estimates), Redirect Path
(broken-link/redirect-issue checker), Google PageSpeed (desktop + mobile speed
diagnostics), SEO META in 1 Click (headers/images/links/social-tag audit). Backlinks:
hunter.io (contact-finding for outreach), Majestic Backlink Analyser (backlink quantity/
quality). Email: Boomerang (best-send-time + email scheduling). Research: Linkclump
(bulk-open SERP links), Liner (in-page highlighting/annotation). Management: One Click
Extension Manager (toggle/organize installed extensions — Neil self-describes as an
"OCD marketer" running many extensions at once).

## Key claims
- As of 2020-04-20, Neil disclosed he has "terrible grammar" and relies on Grammarly
  rather than a human editor for his own writing.
- As of 2020-04-20, Neil named his standing SEO Chrome-extension stack as: Keyword
  Surfer, MozBar, SimilarWeb, Redirect Path, Google PageSpeed, and SEO META in 1 Click.
- As of 2020-04-20, Neil named hunter.io and Majestic Backlink Analyser as his
  link-building/backlink-research extensions, and Boomerang for email send-time
  optimization.
