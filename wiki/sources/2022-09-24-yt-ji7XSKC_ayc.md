# The Long Term Marketing Tactic You Need to Start Focusing On

## Summary
Argues that partnerships are an under-leveraged growth channel for small/mid-size
companies despite driving a large share of revenue at big companies. Worked example:
NP Digital (marketing services, no website-building) partners with design agencies (no
marketing services) — client bases mutually cross-refer. A 5-step framework: (1) add a
public partnerships page inviting applicants, with a reciprocal cross-promotion
expectation; (2) only pursue partnerships that are mutually revenue-beneficial —
one-sided arrangements fizzle; (3) equip partners with presentations/documentation/
support, since a partnership requires a "double sell" (partner sells you internally, then
sells to their own customer); (4) actively maintain the relationship (referrals, calls,
emails) or it decays — cites a recent joint webinar with WP Engine, cross-promoted to
both audiences; (5) set multi-year expectations — results take three to four years to
materialize meaningfully, and revenue is typically distributed thinly across many small
partners rather than concentrated in one. Ends with a partnerships-inquiry email address
(johnh@npaccel.com).
