# 7 Marketing Tools That'll Instantly Boost Your Traffic

## Summary

Neil runs through a seven-tool stack he says will lift traffic "instantly" for people who
don't have time to make all the changes he usually recommends. The tools: **Ubersuggest**
(all-in-one SEO — site audit + keyword research; put terms you already rank for back in to
find easy long-tail variations); **Mailchimp** (free email list — but segment by user
intent: a dog visitor never gets cat emails, or they tune you out), paired with **Hello
Bar** (free) to collect the emails and feed Mailchimp; **Subscribers** (browser push
notifications — works even when the computer was off, "more effective than email");
**MobileMonkey** (a Facebook Messenger "list" with higher open/click rates than email —
build it now before it gets competitive, as Messenger will eventually unify WhatsApp and
Instagram DMs); the **Yoast SEO plugin** (one-click WordPress optimization + XML sitemap,
fed into **Google Search Console** for faster indexing); **Buffer** (re-schedule evergreen
content repeatedly rather than sharing once and forgetting); and **UpViral** (build a viral
loop — reward existing visitors with an e-book/coupon/gift for sharing your content,
webinars, or tools). His close: combine all the tools for the biggest lift.

## Key claims

- As of 2019-08-05, Neil's fastest-traffic tool stack is **Ubersuggest, Mailchimp (+ Hello
  Bar), Subscribers, MobileMonkey, Yoast + Search Console, Buffer, and UpViral**.
- As of 2019-08-05, Neil said if you already rank for a head term it's **much easier to rank
  for its long-tail variations** — put the head term into Ubersuggest to harvest them.
- As of 2019-08-05, Neil said email must be **segmented by user intent** (dog vs. cat
  visitors); irrelevant emails get ignored over time.
- As of 2019-08-05, Neil claimed **browser push notifications are "more effective than
  email,"** and that **Facebook Messenger lists (MobileMonkey) have higher open and click
  rates than email** — build one now before the channel saturates.
- As of 2019-08-05, Neil predicted **Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and Instagram DMs will
  all become connected** through Messenger (a dated forward-looking take).
- As of 2019-08-05, Neil said **evergreen content should be re-shared repeatedly via Buffer**,
  not posted once and forgotten.
- As of 2019-08-05, Neil recommended **incentivizing existing visitors to share** (viral
  loop via UpViral) rather than only chasing new traffic.
