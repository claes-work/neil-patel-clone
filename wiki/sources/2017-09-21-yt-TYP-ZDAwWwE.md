# 3 Ways to Make Eye Popping Social Media Images FAST

## Summary
Neil walks through a fast three-step method for producing unique, original social media images without design skills. He recommends signing up for Canva, a free image-creation tool; deciding which social platform (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter) the image is for so the correct dimensions can be used (Canva lists all the platform dimensions for you); then picking one of Canva's templates, modifying it, and adding your own images or text. Neil frames this as a quick workflow that lets marketers create their own unique social media visuals fast rather than relying on stock or generic images.

## Key claims
As of 2017-09-21, Neil states:
- Canva is a free tool that lets you create images on the fly (as of 2017-09-21).
- You should decide which social site you're creating for first, because that determines which dimensions the image needs (as of 2017-09-21).
- Canva tells you all of the platform dimensions, so you don't have to look them up yourself (as of 2017-09-21).
- Canva offers both free templates and premium (paid) templates (as of 2017-09-21).
- The fast workflow is: pick a template, modify it, and add your own images or text to get a unique image (as of 2017-09-21).
- Following these three steps in Canva lets you create your own unique social media images fast (as of 2017-09-21).
