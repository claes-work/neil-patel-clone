## Key claims
- Formula for writing blog content: conversational tone (use "you and I"), paragraphs
  capped at 5–6 lines, subheadings for skimmability, hook the intro with a bold statement
  plus a question, and end the conclusion with a question to drive comments (paraphrase,
  ~2018) — content-marketing.
- Names his biggest SEO-career failure as early link-buying/"cheating Google" instead of
  providing reader value — reinforces the previously-documented black-hat-era regret
  (paraphrase, ~2018) — seo.
- Content-operation split: 40% beginner content, 10% advanced/data-driven content, 20%
  rewriting older articles (via Google Search Console year-over-year comparison), 30%
  promotion — cited as "a formula that's been working really well" (direct quote framing,
  ~2018) — content-marketing. **Not new**: this is the same underlying split already
  promoted into [[wiki/topics/content-marketing/content-marketing]] as the "10/40/20/30
  content-time-allocation breakdown" (2021-04-03 source), just narrated with the
  categories in a different order — treat as a reinforcement/repeat, not a fresh ★. Also
  recurs near-verbatim in the 2021-07-03 source in this same batch (same underlying stock
  interview footage reused across two edited uploads).
- Best advice to his younger self: focus — describes having "ADD" at 20, chasing too many
  side ventures instead of doubling down on one marketing business; cites an unfunded
  "Invisalign through 3D printers" pitch he almost backed as an example (self-reported,
  ~2018) — biography / mindset.
- States he has ~37 million video views achieved "without going viral," attributing it to
  consistency rather than one-hit-wonder virality (direct quote below, ~2018) — voice /
  content-marketing. ★ candidate (specific quantified claim).
- Names team members: **Nick Rashaun** or **Jeff Johnson** as executives who help run the
  company; "Curtis" set up this interview; "Mike" (co-founder Mike Kamo) goes into the
  office daily (self-reported, ~2018) — biography / entities. ⚠️ Name spelling variance:
  cross-reference the 2021-06-24 source in this same batch, which spells the same
  apparent VP-of-Sales name "Nick Roshon" — needs reconciling at next synthesis (likely
  caption-garble of one real name).
- Wants to build an in-person "mini university" education company in India, but has not
  found the right local business partner (self-reported, ~2018) — biography.
- Google Search Console content-refresh workflow: compare the last 30 days of traffic to
  the same 30-day window one year prior, identify decaying articles, and rewrite them to
  beat what competitors currently rank with (paraphrase, ~2018) — seo / content-marketing,
  consistent with the already-documented "rank-OLD-content" doctrine.
