# The Easiest Way to Get More SEO Traffic

## Summary
Solo tutorial on ranking via embedded video content rather than more written articles.
Shows his Google Search Console "Search appearance → Videos" filter: 318,000 clicks and
roughly 100,000 visitors/month over the trailing 3 months, over 1 million over the
trailing 12 months, from video-driven search results. Notes some of that traffic is
irrelevant (Instagram-follower-related video posts) versus relevant ("keyword research
tool"/Ubersuggest videos), echoing his relevance-over-volume doctrine. Workflow: publish
video content embedded on the blog (not just to YouTube) on the same keyword targets
found via Ubersuggest keyword research (prioritizing high-CPC, business-relevant terms
like "digital marketing agency" over generic high-volume ones), add each target keyword to
an Ubersuggest keyword list to track, and rely on video being a less-competitive Google
result format than text alone to rank faster. Also mentions testing YouTube Shorts/
Instagram Reels. Closes noting a 28-day traffic sample already shows a shift away from
irrelevant (Instagram-follower) queries toward relevant ones (keyword research, affiliate
marketing, Google Analytics) as he retargets his video content.
