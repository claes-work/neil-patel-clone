# Is Your Offline Marketing Working? Here are 3 Easy Ways to Track Your Offline Marketing Online

## Summary
Neil explains that although the majority of marketing spend is still offline and hard to track, it can be measured online through three tactics. He recommends assigning a unique URL to each offline channel (flyers, TV ads) so responses can be attributed, using channel-specific discount codes that both incentivize online purchases and reveal where buyers came from, and giving each campaign a unique phone number ("call tracking") so software can attribute inbound calls—and ultimately sales—to specific offline campaigns such as flyers versus postcards. He points to CallFire as a simple call-tracking service for offline and online phone numbers.

## Key claims
As of 2017-10-03, Neil states:
- The majority of marketing spend today is offline, and while it is hard to track, tracking it online is still possible (as of 2017-10-03).
- Assigning a unique URL to each offline channel (flyers, TV advertisements) lets you track that channel's results online (as of 2017-10-03).
- Using channel-specific discount codes gives people a reason to buy online and, once entered on your website, reveals which offline campaign they came from (as of 2017-10-03).
- Assigning a unique phone number to each campaign (call tracking) lets call-tracking software attribute inbound calls—and therefore sales—to specific offline campaigns, e.g., distinguishing flyers from postcards (as of 2017-10-03).
- CallFire is a simple service for tracking phone numbers whether they are used offline or online (as of 2017-10-03).
