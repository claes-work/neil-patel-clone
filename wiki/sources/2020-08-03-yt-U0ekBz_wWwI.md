# 18 Content Types to Dominate Content Marketing - Module 2 - Lesson 1 - Content Marketing Unlocked

## Summary
Week-two lesson 1 of **Content Marketing Unlocked** ([[2020-08-01-yt-W13rm0OZ6dI]]):
a survey of content-format types beyond text — tools/interactive content
(Ubersuggest, SEO Analyzer, an A/B-test significance calculator embedded in
neilpatel.com posts), infographics, instructional guides, data-driven case-study
posts, native social content (upload natively per platform rather than
cross-posting links), video, podcasts (references the **Marketing School** podcast
with co-host **Eric Siu**, including live-recorded episodes with audience Q&A),
webinars, hybrid posts (embedded video/audio/infographics), eBooks/white papers
(more effective for B2B lead-gen per Neil), "journey" posts (cites his own popular
"journey to $100K/month" post), online courses, live video, guest posting (says he
does much less of it "than four years ago"), magazines, listicles, and multi-image
carousel posts (claims carousels get "almost double" the shares of normal posts on
Instagram/LinkedIn). Recounts a data point from "one of my older companies": a
weekly-infographic program (47 infographics) that generated 2.5M visitors, 41,000
backlinks, and 3,000 referring domains for a $28,000 spend, versus an estimated ~$1M
to buy that traffic via ads.

## Key claims
- As of 2020-08-03, Neil claimed a past company's 47-infographic campaign (paid

  **Eric Siu**, including live-recorded episodes taking audience questions.

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "I wouldn't go and create an infographic on computers when I'm a dental shop, it has to be related [to your core business]."

> "You want to take that video again, even the same one, and re-upload it to Facebook... you got to natively upload that content to each site, versus sharing the link from one of the networks on another social network."

> "It's about providing a better experience [not] just for the sake of being like, 'Oh, let me embed a video so I can get more video views.'"
