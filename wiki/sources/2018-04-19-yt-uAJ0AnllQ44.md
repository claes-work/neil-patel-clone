# 3 Advanced Hacks to Grow Your Business with Instagram Stories

_Q&A Thursday: Neil with co-host **Adam** (from Viewership). Only Neil-attributed
material is persona data; Adam's storytelling and tracking tips are marked below._

## Summary
On using Instagram Stories, Neil's core advice is to use them for **lead generation via
storytelling**. He dislikes Instagram because it's hard to link out, but Stories make it
easier and the people who manually go to your site are highly engaged and convert well.
His playbook: run an **hour-by-hour tip drip** (e.g. "over the next 8 hours I'll teach
you to double your Google traffic"), delivering value in short clips, then **CTA to
your blog (neilpatel.com) or agency site (neilpateldigital.com)** to capture leads — plus
an end-of-story recap/checklist CTA. Adam adds storytelling craft (start with the end/
core message; body language and emotion) and a tracking hack: a **unique campaign URL
301-redirecting with UTM parameters** so Story-driven traffic is measurable. Neil adds
that the **swipe-up** link works — better for consumer/B2C products than B2B — and should
be combined with verbal CTAs since many people don't swipe. Uses a teeth-whitening
"tissue-paper test" demo as a consumer Story example.

## Key claims
As of 2018-04-19, Neil states:
- **Use Instagram Stories to generate leads through storytelling**; Stories are inherently
  a story format that carries people along a journey.
- Instagram is **hard to link out of** ("this is why I hate Instagram"), but Story viewers
  who manually visit your site are **highly engaged and more likely to convert**.
- **Hour-by-hour tip drip**: open with a big promise (e.g. "double your Google traffic
  over the next 8 hours," no clickbait), then teach one tip per hour (URLs, updating
  content, cannibalization) in short clips.
- Add **CTAs mid- and end-story** to a content page (**neilpatel.com**) for more
  readers/opt-ins, or to the **agency site (neilpateldigital.com / "Neil Patel Digital")**
  for services — plus an end recap/checklist page collecting everything shared.
- **Swipe-up works** and can drive site traffic, but **many people don't swipe**, so
  combine swipe-up with spoken CTAs. Swipe-up performs **better for consumer/B2C** products
  (also works for B2B). Uses a **teeth-whitening "tissue-paper test"** demo as an example.

### Adam (co-host, NOT persona data)
- **Storytelling craft**: start with the end / core message in mind; use strong **body
  language and emotion** so viewers don't click away.
- **Tracking hack**: create a **unique campaign URL** (e.g. neilstips.com / tubetraining.com)
  that **301-redirects** to a squeeze page with **UTM parameters** to track each campaign's
  performance — applicable to Instagram Stories and paid video ads.
