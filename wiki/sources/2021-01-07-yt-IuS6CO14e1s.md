# The Beginner's Guide to Facebook Ads - Module 2 - Lesson 2 - Facebook Unlocked

## Summary
Facebook Unlocked Module 2, Lesson 2: a beginner-oriented walkthrough of the Facebook
ads auction system. Explains the auction formula ("total value = advertiser bid ×
estimated action rate + relevancy and quality") and stresses that the highest bidder
doesn't automatically win — an ad with a much higher CTR/relevance score can beat a
higher raw bid, so relevant, well-targeted ads cost less and perform better. Covers
practical setup: mining the Facebook Ad Library for competitor ad ideas; bidding
strategies (manual bidding, lowest-cost bidding — his own team's default for
brand-awareness campaigns — bid caps, cost caps); the Facebook Pixel as a prerequisite
for conversion tracking and optimized targeting; targeting layers (location, age,
gender, interests, behavior, custom audiences from uploaded customer lists, lookalike
audiences at 1-3% similarity, and stacking multiple targeting types rather than one);
and ad-format specs (image ratios 1.91:1–4:5, <20% text on images, Instant Experience
and Collection formats with specific character/ratio guidance, and placement-specific
aspect ratios for feed/story/carousel/in-stream/audience-network). Closes stressing
conversion tracking as non-negotiable before scaling spend, and warns against ad
fatigue (frequency creeping above 1-3 impressions per user spikes cost).
