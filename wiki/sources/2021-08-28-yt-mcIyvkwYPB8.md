# 5 tools To Create a Profitable Online Business

## Summary
A five-tool stack recommendation, mostly promoting his own products. (1) **Crazy Egg**
— AB testing/CRO via click heatmaps and mouse recordings, plus a WYSIWYG editor to
adjust page elements and retest. (2) **Hello Bar** — free lead capture (name/email/
phone, CRM sync), reinforcing the "money's in the list" doctrine. (3) **MailChimp** —
free email tool for smaller lists. (4) **MobileMonkey** — an Instagram/Facebook DM
autoresponder; Neil describes using it on his own Instagram account (auto-pitches NP
Digital when someone DMs him) and a keyword-triggered autoresponder for posts (DM a
specific word like "SEO" to get a specific reply). (5) **Ubersuggest** — free keyword
research, content ideas, competitor traffic estimates, and the backlink-gap report.
Closes with an overall recommendation: bootstrap with free tools before hiring
agencies/contractors.
