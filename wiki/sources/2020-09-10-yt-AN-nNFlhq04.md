# The Best Traffic Hack (Works Even If You're a Complete Newbie)

## Summary
Neil walks through his own Google Search Console top-pages report to make the case
that interactive tools out-earn blog content: of his top-5 traffic pages, "60%... are
tool oriented" (Ubersuggest #1 by more than 3x the #2 page, plus an SEO analyzer and an
A/B testing calculator), versus one Instagram blog post and the homepage. Cites the A/B
testing calculator specifically — 28,000 clicks over three months despite not being
updated "probably in three plus years" — as evidence tools compound traffic without
ongoing content-refresh labor, unlike blog posts. Then gives the ★ tactic: for readers
without dev budget to build a custom tool like Ubersuggest, buy a pre-built, white-label
tool from CodeCanyon/Envato Market (e.g. SEO tools, real-estate portals, mortgage
calculators, priced roughly $16–32) and install it directly on the site under the
site's own branding — "in the long run funny enough, it's cheaper than creating
content."
