# Get THOUSANDS of Backlinks On Semi-Autopilot - Module 05 - Lesson 3 - SEO Unlocked

## Summary
SEO Unlocked link-building module, lesson 3: relationship-based outreach and content
promotion at scale. Opens with a Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson consistency quote as the
frame — link building is outreach, and outreach only works if done consistently.
Step one: build genuine relationships with ~20 influencers per niche by engaging in
their comments/retweets/shares before ever asking for anything. Step two: promote
every piece of content — recommends publishing blog posts around **Monday, ~8am** in
readers' timezone (Pacific/Eastern for his English blog, Brazil time for his
Portuguese blog) and emailing the list around **6am**, with at least **four emails a
month** to the list. Describes his outreach stack: source link-opportunity contacts
via **Ubersuggest**, find emails via a site's privacy policy or a Google `@domain
name` search (flags GDPR/privacy-policy compliance), then run outreach campaigns
through **Mailshake** (his tool of choice; alternatives named: Ninja Outreach, Buzz
Stream, Pitch Box) — building a CSV of contacts, using or customizing templates, and
using Mailshake's reply-logic/follow-up automation. General tips: relevant lists,
persuasive sequences, correct timing, personalization, and continually tracking
open/reply rates. Closes on a "maintain your velocity" doctrine — build links steadily
(a few per week, ramping over time) rather than in bursts, because a burst-then-stop
pattern signals something wrong to search engines; action item is 50 outreach emails/
week for at least three months.

## Key claims
- As of 2020-06-04, Neil said his ideal blog-post publish time is Monday ~8am in his
  readers' timezone (Pacific/Eastern for his main site; Brazil time for his Portuguese
  blog), and his ideal list-email send time is ~6am, with a minimum of four emails a
  month to the list.
- As of 2020-06-04, Neil's outreach stack is Ubersuggest (finding link opportunities/
  contacts) feeding into Mailshake (sending and managing outreach), and he named Ninja
  Outreach, Buzz Stream and Pitch Box as alternative outreach tools.
- As of 2020-06-04, Neil said email-address discovery should respect privacy policies
  and cited GDPR explicitly as a constraint on how outreach contact data is sourced.
- As of 2020-06-04, Neil's link-building "velocity" doctrine: build links steadily (a
  consistent few per week, ramped over time) rather than in a single burst, because
  a burst followed by silence "shows search engines that there's something wrong."
- As of 2020-06-04, Neil's action item was to send at least 50 outreach emails a week,
  consistently, for a minimum of three months.
