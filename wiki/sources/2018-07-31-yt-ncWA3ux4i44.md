# 5 Underutilized Social Media Hacks to Drive Traffic

## Summary

Solo tactical video: five organic (non-paid) ways to drive blog traffic from social,
even with few followers. **(1) Use Instagram to promote blog posts** — most people push
products on IG; almost no one pushes *educational* blog content. Neil says posting images
tied to educational info and telling people to click the link in your bio converts well
(a product "link in bio" ask does not), and it scales with follower count. **(2) Use
Snapchat + Instagram Stories "swipe up"** — with 10,000+ IG followers you unlock the
swipe-up link; he cites Gary Vaynerchuk and Tim Sykes as people who drive podcast/blog
traffic this way. **(3) Leverage live video** (Facebook Live, YouTube Live, Instagram
Live) — a plain link post gets almost no engagement, but a live video gets far more, and
telling engaged viewers what they'll learn before sending them to your post drives real
clicks. **(4) Upload native videos** (not just live) to Facebook/YouTube — video
out-engages image/text posts; drop in a URL verbally and use a **shortened link (e.g.
Bitly)** rather than a long ugly URL. **(5) Use Quora** — answer questions in your niche
in depth (a paragraph or two) and link to your deeper blog post; he says his team "used
to do this all day long" and it generated hundreds of thousands of views/month at peak
(he's since dropped it for time but calls it one of the best content strategies he's used).

## Key claims

- As of 2018-07-31, promoting *educational* blog content on Instagram (link-in-bio)
  converts, whereas pushing products via link-in-bio generally does not.
- As of 2018-07-31, Instagram Stories/Snapchat swipe-up (unlocked at ~10,000 IG followers)
  is an effective, underused traffic driver; he cites Gary Vaynerchuk and Tim Sykes as
  practitioners.
- As of 2018-07-31, live video (FB/YT/IG Live) earns far more engagement than a static link
  post, and directing engaged live viewers to a post drives clicks, comments, and return
  visits.
- As of 2018-07-31, native uploaded videos out-engage text/image posts; pair the verbal CTA
  with a shortened link (Bitly) instead of a long URL.
- As of 2018-07-31, in-depth Quora answers linking to a deeper blog post are a strong
  traffic source — his team drove hundreds of thousands of views/month from Quora at peak.

## Notable verbatim quotes
