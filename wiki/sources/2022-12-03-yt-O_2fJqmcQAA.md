# The Easiest SEO Strategy I've Ever Used ★

## Summary
Names AI/GPT-3 as his go-to SEO shortcut for delegating repetitive work: keyword and
topic ideation, meta text, titles, blog outlines. Cites Gartner-attributed 2021 stats
(86% of CEOs consider AI mainstream in their offices; 91.5% of leading businesses
continually invest in AI; expected 25% customer-satisfaction growth from AI adoption)
and an Outranking-cited stat that 30% of AI adopters saw a 6-10% revenue lift. Frames
GPT-3 (available via OpenAI) as "sweeping the SEO marketplace" in 2022, powering tools
like Search Atlas, CopySmith, Jasper, and Copy.ai for titles/meta tags/outlines/full
drafts. Explicitly says AI shouldn't replace the whole content team — it's a
productivity multiplier and a cure for creative ruts, not a fully autonomous writer;
says AI can save "roughly half" the time for new bloggers, but polished, differentiated
articles still need human rewriting. Also plugs his own company's Answer the Public
acquisition (earlier in 2022) as a complementary tool for surfacing raw, uncompetitive
search-intent phrases, citing an ~8.5 billion daily Google searches figure and Oberlo's
claim that ~20% of searches have never been seen before; quotes former Google data
scientist Seth Stephens-Davidowitz calling Google searches "the most important data set
ever collected on the human psyche." Closes plugging NP Digital and the free Ubersuggest
AI writer.
