# SEO in 2019: What Will and Won't Work | Neil Patel

## Summary

A forward-look at what works and what doesn't in SEO for 2019, opening with the argument
that SEO is worth it because most clicks go to organic, not paid. Six tips: (1) **Unique
content beats volume** — regurgitating won't rank; a fresh, unique post (weekly or monthly)
earns shares, backlinks, comments, and traffic because people crave new information.
(2) **Build a brand** — Google looks for brand signals to build trust and fight fake news;
check brand size in Google Trends vs. competitors, and grow it via an omni-channel
(online + offline) approach over 1–2+ years to rank more easily. (3) **Link building is
harder than ever** — everyone's being pitched, so use Ahrefs Link Intersect to target
sites already linking to several competitors but not you. (4) **Site speed** — with
Google's mobile-first index, use Google PageSpeed; fast phones don't guarantee fast
reception. (5) **Voice search** — growing fast (2 of 5 adults; Neil predicts 50%+ by
2020); answer questions in short 1–2 sentence responses and be on HTTPS with a fast site.
(6) **Update your content** yearly because competitors copy fresh content and Google tires
of ranking outdated pages.
