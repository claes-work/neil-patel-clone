# How to Get More Views on Your Instagram Stories | Neil Patel

## Summary

A six-tactic formula for maximizing Instagram Stories reach. (1) **Post frequently** —
unlike traditional social feeds, Stories reward volume; post ideally four times a day,
spread throughout the day, for far more views than one post. (2) **Use video** (and
Boomerangs) over static photos, because engaging motion content spreads and earns more
views. (3) **Tag relevant accounts** — tag brands or friends you genuinely mention (e.g.
Nike) to gain views, but keep tags relevant and don't spam-tag to game the system. (4)
**Use hashtags** — still effective despite claims they're "played out"; more relevant
hashtags means more traffic, and to find ones people actually search, use Ubersuggest
(its search data transfers well to Instagram hashtag/keyword popularity). (5) **Ask a
question or run a poll** (including the emoji sliders) to lift engagement, which lifts
views. (6) **Use location-based tags** — Instagram sometimes pushes a random-seeming
story, and that push is often driven by location tags, so adding one increases the chance
your story gets amplified.
