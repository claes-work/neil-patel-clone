# My Favorite Webinar Tip

## Summary
Recommends co-hosting webinars with a partner brand as the single best webinar
tactic. Self-reports hosting webinars at least monthly for NP Digital, driving
"a lot of leads" and revenue, plus brand-building value; cites 67% of
marketers were increasing webinar investment, 76% say webinars help reach more
leads, 75% say webinars extend brand reach, and 69% say webinars help scale
marketing efforts. Explains the partner-webinar method in four steps: (1) find
a partner with a similar-but-not-identical audience — cites NP Digital's
BigCommerce partnership (NP Digital's SEO-interested audience vs.
BigCommerce's e-commerce-platform audience) as the worked example; (2) align
promotional schedules, design aesthetic, and brand voice across both
partners; (3) run a practice/technical rehearsal beforehand; (4) define a
post-webinar strategy — cites email as the top registration channel (up to
57% of registrants) with a conversion rate ~27% higher than average, and
describes simulcasting the webinar live across social channels via Restream to
reach followers directly. Also recommends emailing both no-shows and
non-openers a recording after the event to recapture additional attendance,
and pitching products/services only after delivering value during the
session. Closes with an NP Digital plug.
