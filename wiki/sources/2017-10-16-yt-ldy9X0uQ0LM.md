# 3 Ways to Use Facebook Live To Generate Sales

## Summary
Neil explains how to generate sales with Facebook Live, noting that Facebook (and YouTube) promote live video heavily because it functions as an alternative to reality TV that keeps viewers on the platform. He lays out a simple three-step strategy: first, offer education or a clear benefit to the viewer (he educates people on marketing during his own streams); second, transition the educated viewer to a bonus offer hosted on your website — for example a free consultation call at neilpatel.com/consulting — ideally with a landing page tailored specifically to that Facebook Live video; and third, once the stream ends, boost and promote the video so more people continue watching it, which drives additional visitors back to your website.

## Key claims
As of 2017-10-16, Neil states:
- Facebook and YouTube promote live video heavily because it serves as reality-TV-style content that keeps people watching on the platform (as of 2017-10-16).
- Facebook Live is simply someone pulling out their phone and showing what is happening in their life, visible to everyone in real time (as of 2017-10-16).
- Step one to generate sales is to offer education or a benefit to the viewer; Neil educates people on marketing so they perceive him as smart and keep watching (as of 2017-10-16).
- Step two is to transition to a bonus offer for the viewer, usually hosted on your website — for example inviting them to a free consultation call at neilpatel.com/consulting (as of 2017-10-16).
- The landing page for that bonus offer should be tailored specifically to the Facebook Live video (as of 2017-10-16).
- Step three is to boost and promote the video once it is done, so more people continue watching and more visitors return to your website (as of 2017-10-16).
