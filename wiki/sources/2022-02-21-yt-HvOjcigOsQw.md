## Summary
A Facebook-specific engagement playbook: model and repeat your best-performing post
types, cut back on underperformers; keep posts short (cites that 98.3% of Facebook
usage is mobile); post consistently at data-informed optimal times using Facebook Page
Insights, invoking the marketing "rule of seven"; get active participating (not
promoting) in relevant Facebook Groups (cites 1.8B monthly Facebook Group users as of
2020); use Facebook Stories (cites 500M+ daily Stories viewers and 1B Stories created
daily across Facebook/Instagram/WhatsApp) while keeping production quality low and
authentic; explicitly avoid engagement-bait phrasing ("like and share and comment")
since Facebook's algorithm detects and suppresses it (no penalty/ban, just reduced
reach); and finally, boost (pay to promote) posts that are already organically
performing well, to compound their reach.
