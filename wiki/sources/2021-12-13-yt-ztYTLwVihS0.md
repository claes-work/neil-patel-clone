# Finding The Easiest Opportunities for Link Building In Less Than 3 Minutes

## Summary
A short tutorial on Ubersuggest's "Backlink Opportunities" report: enter your URL plus
up to three competitor URLs (found via Ubersuggest's Competitors report if unknown), and
the report surfaces sites that link to multiple competitors but not to you. His stated
rationale: a site linking to three-plus competitors shows no exclusive bias toward any
one of them, so it is more persuadable to also link to you — but only after you create
content demonstrably better than whatever they're currently linking to (more in-depth,
step-by-step, with GIFs/infographics/video), which then becomes the pitch for outreach.
Explicitly frames this as one of the shortest SEO videos he's made and defends the
brevity ("didn't want to go on for 10 minutes rambling").

## Key claims
- Prescribes a specific link-building workflow: find sites linking to 3+ competitors
  via Ubersuggest's Backlink Opportunities report, then create superior content before
  pitching them, reasoning that multi-competitor linkers show no single-site bias and
  are more likely to add another link (self-reported tactical framework, dated
  2021-12-13) — seo. Consistent with, and more procedurally detailed than, prior
  competitor-backlink-mining material already logged.
