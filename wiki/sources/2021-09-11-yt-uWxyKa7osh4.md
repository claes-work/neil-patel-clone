# The ONLY Features You Need to Use in SEO Tools

## Summary
An Ubersuggest feature tour framed as "the four features you need" from any SEO tool:
(1) Rank Tracking — daily/weekly, mobile vs. desktop, with a YouTube-SEO-is-fast vs.
Google-SEO-is-slow (months to a year) contrast; (2) Competitive Analysis — Top Pages,
per-page keywords, per-page backlinks, traffic trend, and CPC-as-commercial-intent
signal; (3) Keyword Ideas + Content Ideas reports, filterable by country/city/state;
(4) Backlinks + the Backlink Opportunity report (sites linking to multiple competitors
but not you). Reiterates the links-as-votes analogy (with a link-quality tier: NYT/CNN
> Neil Patel) and closes with an NP Digital pitch.

## Key claims
- Contrasts YouTube SEO (results in a day or two even from zero subscribers) against
  Google SEO (three months to a year-plus) as a reason rank tracking must tolerate a
  long game (paraphrase, dated 2021-09) — seo. Reinforces prior "YouTube SEO is the
  opposite of traditional SEO" doctrine (already in wiki/topics/seo).
- Reiterates backlinks-as-votes with an explicit quality hierarchy: a New York Times or
  CNN link outweighs a link from Neil Patel himself (paraphrase, dated 2021-09) — seo.
  Reinforcing, not new (link-voting-system already promoted at synthesis pass 9).
- High CPC on a competitor's ranking keyword is used as a signal the keyword converts
  well and is worth pursuing organically (paraphrase, dated 2021-09) — seo.
