# 4 Ways to Use Pinterest to Drive Traffic and Grow Your Ecommerce Business

## Summary
Neil frames Pinterest as an especially strong channel for e-commerce businesses selling products, and lays out four practices for using it to drive traffic. First, pin frequently — 10 or more times a day rather than once or twice — because engagement with the Pinterest community correlates with results. Second, don't limit yourself to unique content: alongside your own pins, you can save (re-pin) other people's great content to your boards, which he likens to sharing someone else's Facebook update and which lets you pin far more. Third, relate the content you pin to the products and services you sell; he cites Kissmetrics, where marketing-related infographics went viral on Pinterest and made it one of the company's top traffic sources, driving visitors and leads. Fourth, participate in the community by commenting on other people's boards — without growing your follower count that way, new pins on your own boards won't perform well.
