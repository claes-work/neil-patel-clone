# The 6 Highest-Paying Affiliate Platforms to Use in 2022

## Summary
An affiliate-marketing primer. Cites Statista: US affiliate marketing spend expected
to reach $8.2B by 2022, over 3x the figure of 10 years earlier; cites an Awin report
that 65% of affiliate marketers use content marketing to drive traffic. Explains the
two affiliate payout models — one-time payout (larger single sum, less predictable
month to month) vs. recurring commission (smaller per-month amount, compounds over a
customer's lifetime, predictable if the underlying product retains customers well).
Lists affiliate networks: ShareASale, ClickBank (known for eBooks), CJ, Amazon
Associates, eBay Partner Network (EPN), FlexOffers. Cites specific example payouts:
Fiverr (up to $150 CPA per first-time buyer, or a $10 CPA + 10% rev-share hybrid, on
services priced up to $10,000), Bluehost (payouts starting at $65), Hostinger (tiered,
up to $150/sale, base ~$60). Explicitly states he does not personally run an
Ubersuggest affiliate program (used only as a hypothetical illustration in the video).
