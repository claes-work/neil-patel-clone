# How to Become an SEO Expert

## Summary

Neil's path to SEO expertise, ordered as a sequence. (1) **Truly love SEO** — passion, not
money, is what makes someone put in the hours to become an expert. (2) **Set up your own
website** (WordPress.org/.com) so you can actually practice the tactics. (3) **Write
content** on whatever you're passionate about (even your SEO journey) — "content is king,"
and without content you won't rank. (4) **Build links** using a BuzzSumo→Ahrefs outreach
loop: find popular articles in your niche, pull their linkers from Ahrefs, and email a
personalized pitch ("mine covers A, B, C that theirs didn't") — expect ~100 emails for a
few links, but they compound. (5) **Get social shares** the same way via BuzzSumo's "view
shares." (6) **Follow the top marketing blogs** (Backlinko, Moz, neilpatel.com) to stay
current — "you don't have to be the best, just better than most." (7) **Run one big
experiment a month** (e.g., delete non-ranking old content, add sidebar links to top
pages) — big tests, not tiny title-tag tweaks, and not so frequently you can't attribute
the impact. (8) **Be patient** — rankings take 6 months to a year and get harder yearly as
the web grows (he cites ~1.7–1.8B websites, 1B+ blogs).
