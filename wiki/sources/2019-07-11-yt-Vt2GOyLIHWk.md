# How to Find Endless Content Ideas With One FREE Tool

## Summary

Neil pitches **Ubersuggest's Content Ideas report** as a free, endless source of content ideas
amid **4 million+ blog posts published per day**. Enter any broad keyword (or 3–4-word phrase)
and the report shows popular posts in your space, the keywords each ranks for, and everyone
linking to it. Use it to learn what people **like and hate** (low-performing content is shown
too, so you avoid it) — don't create "me-too" copies. For topics people like, come up with
similar ideas and apply **Brian Dean's Skyscraper technique** (explicitly credited to Brian
Dean): make your piece more thorough than the tallest existing one — but **don't add fluff word
count**; every word should have impact and help the reader get the job done, and integrate
custom design elements/graphics. Then re-open the Content Ideas report, find everyone linking to
competitors, and email each one a short outreach note pointing to your better piece. Bonus:
email everyone you **linked out to** and ask them to share — people are flattered and usually do
(cites getting such an email from **Larry Kim** about an Inc. post and naturally tweeting it).
