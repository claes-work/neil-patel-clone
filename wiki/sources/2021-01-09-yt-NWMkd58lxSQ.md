# Growing Your Brand on Facebook - Module 2 - Lesson 3 - Facebook Unlocked ★

## Summary
COMPLETES the **Facebook Unlocked** course (launched 2020-12-21,
[[2020-12-21-yt-LInnulL_6is]]). Neil gives "five rules" for brand growth on Facebook:
(1) set advertising goals/budget before spending; (2) map the sales process from
unaware prospect to customer; (3) test everything, check your ego, double down on what
works; (4) nail audience targeting at each stage of the buying cycle; (5) relationships
take time — a complete strategy converts more than a one-shot pitch (his
"asking a stranger to marry you" analogy for expecting immediate conversions). Walks
through a two-ad remarketing funnel his agency uses (cold traffic → blog post →
Facebook retargeting → landing/sales page), framed as cheaper on a CPA basis than
sending cold traffic directly to a sales page. Covers the Facebook Pixel as the
conversion-tracking backbone, Messenger as a high-open-rate promotional channel (again
flagged with uncertainty about how long its effectiveness will hold), typical
close-rate variance (some businesses see 50-60%, others just a few percent — "test, don't
rely on other people's numbers"), and closes with core ad metrics (results, reach,
impressions, cost per result) and a caring-for-people-over-self-promotion framing as
the course's summary takeaway. Cites the 90%-of-sales-still-in-store /
66%-of-purchase-interactions-on-mobile-web stats as the course's macro framing, plus a
COVID-accelerated-digital-shift framing consistent with other 2020-era sources already
in the corpus.
