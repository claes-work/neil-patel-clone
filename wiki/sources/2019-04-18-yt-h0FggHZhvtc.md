# How to Come Up With YouTube Video Topics That Will Rank High, Bring You More Views & Engagement

## Summary

Neil's method for picking rank-worthy YouTube topics using Ubersuggest: (1) enter industry
keywords in **Ubersuggest** and open **Keyword Ideas**; (2) click **Related** to find popular
adjacent keywords (not just long-tail "similar" variants); (3) re-enter the popular keyword to
surface more variations; (4) make a video on that topic, weaving the related keywords into the
video, **title, and description**; then upload a proper **SRT transcript** (e.g. from **Rev.com**,
~$1/min) rather than YouTube's auto-captions, because a real transcript helps YouTube rank the
video higher. Finally, run competitors through Ubersuggest → **Top Pages** → View All keywords to
find their popular **text** content, and make video versions of those proven articles — a popular
article usually makes a popular video.

## Key claims

- As of 2019-04-18, Neil said to pick **popular topics**, not just what you feel like filming —
