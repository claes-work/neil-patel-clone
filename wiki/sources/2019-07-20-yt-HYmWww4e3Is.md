# Why Your Facebook Ads Don't Work (and How to Make Them Profitable)

## Summary

Neil diagnoses why Facebook ads fail and how to fix them. (1) Don't start with cold
audiences — **re-market your existing audience first** and build a **1% lookalike
audience** off your buyers. (2) **Spend enough to test** — $100 won't reveal what works;
but don't scale too fast either (Facebook returns similar clicks for the same money if you
ramp too quickly), so **increase budget gradually**. (3) **Re-market people who almost
bought** (abandoned checkout, service pages that didn't convert) with a video showing what
it's like to be a customer — on both Facebook and YouTube. (4) Fix your **copy** by
studying the **Facebook Ad Library** to see competitors' long-running (i.e., working) ads.
(5) Look at your own **funnel/site**, not just Facebook — study competitors' funnels
(via their ads → landing pages), use tools like **ClickFunnels**. Final tip: **video ads
outperform** text/image ads in his testing, and you must **optimize for revenue (or
leads), not clicks or impressions** — "clicks don't matter, sales matter."
