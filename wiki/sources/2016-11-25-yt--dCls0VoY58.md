# Learn REAL Digital Marketing In Just 5 Minutes a Day

## Summary
A short channel-introduction / mission video in which Neil Patel narrates his own origin story and credentials rather than teaching a specific tactic. He recounts how he backed into learning marketing out of frustration and being broke, lists the clients and companies he has worked with, and frames his mission as sharing a repeatable marketing "formula" with viewers who have no technical background. Despite the title, the content is biographical and motivational, not a step-by-step lesson.

## Key claims
As of 2016-11-25, Neil states:
- His work is helping companies generate more revenue through digital marketing.
- Entrepreneur Magazine named him the number-one marketer in the world and listed one of his companies among the 100 most brilliant companies.
- He started his first website at age 16, funding it with money earned working at an amusement park, which he paid to a marketing firm.
- That firm delivered no results, and the frustration (plus being broke) forced him to learn marketing himself.
- His first client was a power supply manufacturer, for whom he helped generate an additional $25 million in sales — the moment he realized the power of marketing.
- He built his reputation by being honest and over-delivering, helping as many people as he could.
- He has worked with large companies including Google, NBC, Viacom, General Motors, and eBay, but finds helping small business owners the most satisfying.
- He has founded four multimillion-dollar companies and learned the mistakes others should avoid.
- He has written for Forbes, Entrepreneur, Inc, Fast Company, and his own blogs, and colleges worldwide teach his material.
- He has received awards from the President of the United States, the House of Representatives, and the United Nations.
- Marketing is widely believed to be expensive and difficult, but this is not true: there is a formula anyone can follow to get results even without a technical background.
