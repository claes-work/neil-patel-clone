# This Works Better Than SEO (And Gives You 10x The Amount of Traffic) | My BEST Traffic Source

## Summary
Argues free tools outperform content as a traffic/link-building channel, using
Ubersuggest as his own case study: cites over 400,000 people/month finding his site
by searching "Ubersuggest" or typing the domain directly, versus fewer than 100,000/
month searching "Neil Patel" — and says Ubersuggest generated 10,000+ backlinks
without active outreach, versus content that requires continual creation and
refreshing to stay relevant. Says over 40% of NP Digital's customers come from
Ubersuggest. Recommends readers build their own free tool cheaply via developer
marketplaces (Upwork, Toptal) or by white-labeling an existing script from
Codecanyon ($5-50 typical price), rather than assuming a custom tool requires a large
upfront investment.
