# CRO Unlocked - Free Conversion Rate Optimization Course by Neil Patel

## Summary
Launches the free "CRO Unlocked" training course — two weeks, six lessons plus cheat
sheets/worksheets/handouts, hosted at neilpatel.com/training — with Week 1, Lesson 1
("Overview"). Neil introduces himself (NP Digital co-founder, Ubersuggest owner, prior
companies Crazy Egg, KISSmetrics, QuickSprout, Hello Bar) and frames the course around
the basic revenue formula "new signups = traffic × conversion rate," attributed to his
Crazy Egg co-founder (identified in this video as his **brother-in-law**, now full owner
of Crazy Egg — see attribution note below re: a tension with the existing Hiten Shah
co-founder narrative). Cites internet commerce projected to grow to $6.54 trillion by
2023 and pitches CRO as the highest-leverage lever available: raising conversion rate
grows revenue without additional ad spend, and doubling it literally doubles sales,
freeing budget to spend more on acquisition. Walks a worked example (500 daily orders,
upsell conversion 5%→15% nearly triples upsell revenue, +$2,750/day). Presents two case
studies: **Skincare by Alana** (friends Jared & Alana Mitchell) — already >$3M in website
revenue, improved CVR >200% via surveys/split-tests/SEO, materially changed their family's
life; and an unnamed **Atlanta wholesale extension supplier** — full-funnel CRO + content
+ link building grew revenue from $3.1M to $7.9M and landed them on the Inc 500 fastest-
growing-companies list two years running. Closes on the Dave Brailsford "marginal gains"
Tour de France framing (recurring in his content) paired with a new Toyota analogy —
Toyota didn't build a $272B business overnight, it took 82 years of small optimizations —
as the philosophy underlying the whole course.

  anecdote, 2021-04-26) — analytics-cro.
- Case study: an Atlanta-based wholesale extension supplier grew revenue from $3.1M to
