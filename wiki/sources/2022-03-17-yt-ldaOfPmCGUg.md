# Top 5 Best FREE WEBSITES to Learn a New Marketing Skill

## Summary
A recommendation list of five free places to learn marketing skills, framed by an
observation that COVID-era lockdowns forced many businesses that had ignored digital
marketing to either learn it or go out of business, accelerating an already-booming
trend. Warns against two failure modes: overly academic courses disconnected from
market reality, and generic advice that doesn't teach execution. The five picks: (1)
Google's Fundamentals of Digital Marketing — free, certified, 26 modules, ~40 hours,
broad overview from planning to paid ads to local SEO; (2) OptinMonster's Digital
Marketing Training, focused on email marketing, which he calls the area that produced
the most business results for himself and clients; (3) his own neilpatel.com
Courses/training section — free, with homework, cheat sheets, and answer keys,
covering SEO/social/platform-specific courses, and floats (undecided) adding
accreditation; (4) Copyblogger's "Internet Marketing For Smart People," valued for
copywriting/content-marketing fundamentals, running since 2006; (5) WordStream's PPC
University for paid-ads fundamentals.

## Key claims
- Frames the pandemic as an accelerant that forced marketing adoption on previously
  digital-marketing-averse businesses, and expects the trend to keep growing
  (self-reported opinion, dated 2022-03-17) — content-marketing.
- Self-identifies email marketing as the specific channel that generated "the most

- Is undecided about adding an accreditation/certificate to his own neilpatel.com

- Repeats the "human attention span is shorter than a goldfish's" claim as

  dated 2022-03-17) — content-marketing.
