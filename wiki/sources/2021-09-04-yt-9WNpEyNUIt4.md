# 10 Most Profitable SEO Niches to Start an Online Business

## Summary
A listicle of ten niches Neil says are high-traffic and monetizable for a new
content/affiliate site, framed by a "find a customer first, not a product" opening
heuristic: (1) health/personal care (nootropics, keto, teeth whitening, hypnotherapy —
notes teeth whitening has gotten much more competitive over the years), (2) vaping/CBD
oil/oil diffusers (notes people he knows generating "millions of dollars a year" from
CBD gummies specifically), (3) tools/home improvement (reverse-osmosis systems as a
$500–$1,000 high-ticket example), (4) alternative bicycles/e-bikes, (5) solar
panels/eco-friendly products/vegan protein powder, (6) house/kitchen/outdoor furniture
(patio furniture called out as underserved by content), (7) wardrobe/closet
organization (says demand held up through COVID), (8) board games and action figures,
(9) electronic-vehicle accessories, (10) pet supplies. Recommends affiliate content
and reviews as the monetization path for most of these rather than building the
underlying product company.
