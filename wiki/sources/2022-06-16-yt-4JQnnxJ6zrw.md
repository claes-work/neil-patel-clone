# Learn SEO in Just 5 Minutes a Day

## Summary
This short video is a tactical, habit-based tutorial in which Neil Patel argues that anyone can become competent at SEO by investing just five minutes a day over a few months, rather than blocking off large chunks of time. He opens with a credibility and value hook — his company NP Digital was named the 21st fastest-growing company in the United States by INC Magazine, and he attributes much of that success to SEO, which delivers traffic, leads, and sales without paying "an arm and a leg." He dramatizes SEO's cost advantage by claiming that buying the equivalent traffic with ads would cost him roughly $5.1 million a month (a figure he says he pulled by Googling his keywords), while SEO costs him less than 1% of that. He then lays out a simple three-step daily routine: (1) sign up for Ubersuggest, create a project, and enter target keywords so the tool emails weekly diagnostics and two or three simple fix-it tasks that both improve rankings and teach SEO by doing it on your own site; (2) read one or two articles a week from NeilPatel.com, Backlinko, and Search Engine Land to stay current on tactics; and (3) listen to the Marketing School podcast, noting that it releases daily, runs under five minutes per episode, and covers SEO at least once a week. The core message is that consistency over months beats intensity — give it time, do a little each week, and you'll pick up SEO quickly. He closes with his standard call to action, offering NP Digital as the done-for-you alternative for those who would rather have experts do it and get results faster, plus the usual like/share/subscribe/comment ask.

- You can learn SEO in just five minutes a day by following a simple daily/weekly routine over a few months (as of 2022-06-16).
- Step one: go to Ubersuggest, create an account and a project, and enter the keywords you want to rank for; Ubersuggest will also suggest keywords you can rank for if you don't have any (as of 2022-06-16).
- Every week Ubersuggest emails you what's wrong with your site, how to fix it, and two or three simple tasks that improve rankings over time and teach you SEO because you're doing it on your own website (as of 2022-06-16).
- Step two: read NeilPatel.com, Backlinko, and Search Engine Land — not every day, just one or two articles a week you like — to stay up to date with new tactics and strategies (as of 2022-06-16).
- Step three: listen to the Marketing School podcast; it publishes a new episode every single day, each is under five minutes, and at least one episode a week is usually on SEO (as of 2022-06-16).
- The method works by consistency, not intensity: give it time, do it for a few months (picking some episodes/articles each week rather than every single day), and you'll learn SEO quickly without spending more than five minutes a day (as of 2022-06-16).
- For those who don't want to spend the time, NP Digital is offered as the done-for-you agency that does SEO — and all forms of marketing — for companies to get results faster (as of 2022-06-16).
