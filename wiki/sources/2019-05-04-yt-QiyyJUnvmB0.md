# Use These 7 Tools to Spy On Your Competitors and Steal Their Best Marketing Tactics

## Summary

A tool round-up for competitor intelligence, most free: (1) **SimilarWeb** — traffic volume
and channel mix (referral/social/search) for any URL; (2) **BuiltWith** — the tech stack a
competitor runs (e.g. spotting **Optimizely** tells you they A/B test, which you can then
cross-reference against the **Wayback Machine** to see past page variations and infer what
converts); (3) **Ubersuggest** — organic vs. paid search traffic by region, plus a **Top
Pages** report with each page's keywords, positions, backlinks, and social shares, so you
create proven-popular content; (4) **BuzzSumo** — like Ubersuggest but blog-focused,
breaking content down by backlinks/social shares and over time periods to read industry
trends; (5) **Socialblade** — competitors' social-channel size, daily follower gains vs.
posting cadence (revealing which single posts caused follower spikes, with dates, so you can
replicate); (6) **WhatRunsWhere** — competitors' banner/display ad copy and images. Combined,
these let you see what's working and "one-up them."
