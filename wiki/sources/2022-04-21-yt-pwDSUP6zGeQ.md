# The 4 Best Digital Marketing Careers to Pursue in 2023 and Beyond

## Summary
A career-advice video opening with a condensed origin story — entering digital
marketing "20 years ago" while still in college, landing his first client
unintentionally, building two companies to eight figures in annual revenue before
turning 30, being featured in Forbes as a top marketer, and a self-reported Obama
nomination as a top young entrepreneur — then pivots to four in-demand digital
marketing career paths with sourced salary figures: (1) AI/data specialists,
citing an Economist Group survey that 39% of senior marketers find data/analytics
recruiting the hardest marketing hire, with average US salary $162K (top 10%
earning $100K more); (2) UX designers, citing "Ping Pong" research for a $97K US
average ($76K at 0–3 years experience); (3) digital marketing managers/directors,
citing salary.com for a ~$109K average (up to $148K at senior levels), alongside
CMOs/VPs/creative directors earning $100K+; (4) marketing analytics roles,
framed around the gap between having data (via Google Data Studio/Analytics) and
actually knowing what to do with it.
