# Add This to Your Website for an Instant SEO Boost

## Summary
Neil says adding a free tool to a website is what boosted his own SEO numbers (as of
2022-05-19): a self-reported 120% increase in time-on-site, +24,453 referring
domains, and 240,392 organic visitors/month from a single keyword — all attributed to
adding Ubersuggest to neilpatel.com. His generalized tactic: most sites can't build a
full free tool from scratch, so he points to CodeCanyon, a marketplace selling
white-label scripts/tools (real estate, automotive, storage, SEO/marketing verticals,
etc.) for roughly $10-50, which buyers can re-skin with their own branding and host on
their own domain. Over time the tool page naturally attracts backlinks and social
shares as one of the site's most popular pages, and because it lives on the same
domain, that authority lifts the whole site's rankings — including unrelated blog
content. This CodeCanyon-as-free-tool-asset tactic recurs from
[[2022-04-09-yt-egKFQRuCERE]] (infographic-backlink/CodeCanyon tool tactic); this
video reframes it explicitly as an SEO/authority mechanism rather than a traffic-asset
tactic and adds the specific Ubersuggest-attributed metrics.

## Key claims
- Neil attributes a self-reported 120% time-on-site increase, +24,453 referring
  domains, and 240,392 monthly organic (non-paid) visitors from one keyword to adding
  Ubersuggest to his website (self-reported, as of 2022-05-19).
- Recommends buying white-label tools from CodeCanyon (~$10-50) for sites that can't
  build a free tool from scratch, across essentially any industry vertical (as of
  2022-05-19).
- A free tool hosted on a site's own domain naturally accrues backlinks/social shares
  over time and, because it shares the domain, lifts the ranking authority of the
  entire site, including unrelated content (as of 2022-05-19).
- Frames the price-anchoring logic explicitly: competitors charging ~$100/month for
  similar SEO data create a "$100 vs. $0" choice that drives adoption toward a free
  tool regardless of relative product quality (as of 2022-05-19).

## Notable verbatim quotes
> The longer people stay on your website it tells Google that people like your site, they find it valuable and that Google should consider ranking it higher.

> It doesn't matter if my product is better or worse than theirs... when people look at it they say, 100 dollars or zero. Zero creates a lot of traffic and backlinks.

> When that tool gets all that authority, remember that tool's on your site, your domain. So it's actually boosting the overall authority for the whole site.
