# I Found the BEST Way to Run Google Ads in 2023: Here's How

## Summary
Solo product-promo video for Neil's free "Ads Grader" tool (adsgrader.com). Opens with a stat that
Google made $162B from search ads in 2022, framing paid search as high-ROI because it captures
already-motivated buyers instantly (no multi-month ramp, unlike SEO/content). Pivots to the problem:
despite that spend, "billions of dollars" are wasted on badly-run campaigns. Pitches Ads Grader as a
free URL-based analyzer that scores a campaign's quality score (target: 7+, "10 is unrealistic"),
flags specific wasted-spend dollar amounts, compares actual vs. target click-through rate, and
breaks down impressions/clicks/cost-per-acquisition by channel type (Performance Max, Search,
Display). Walks through a live example: a real-estate ad campaign with a 5.89 quality score, over
$6,000 in identified waste, and a 2.69% actual vs. 4.76% target CTR. States the tool has surfaced
companies wasting "over a million dollars a month" in mismanaged campaigns. Closing stance: yes,
businesses should spend on Google Ads, but only if they're doing it efficiently — the real question
is the marginal return per dollar spent.
