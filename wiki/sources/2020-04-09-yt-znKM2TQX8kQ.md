# The Biggest SEO And Digital Marketing SCAM (That You're Still Falling For)

## Summary
Neil warns against automated/"SEO on autopilot" tools and tactics, framing full
automation as still unsolved even by well-funded companies ("I'm trying to still build
something like that, and it's not easy, and I haven't figured it out yet"). Content
scams to avoid: article spinning (illustrated with a garbled synonym-swap example
sentence), content scraping (bots copying others' content without attribution — okay
only with citation/permission), and full content-generation automation ("worst
automation tools out there"). Link-building scams to avoid: link farms (low-value
sites that exist only to link out — Neil recommends disavowing if your site ends up in
one), private blog networks (PBNs — undisclosed site networks used for link building),
and expired-domain 301-redirect schemes (redirecting an expired domain's backlinks to
your own site, which he says rarely helps because the links aren't topically
relevant). His prescribed alternative: write original content in your own brand voice
(hiring freelancers via problogger.net if needed) and build links manually through
genuine value creation.

## Key claims
- As of 2020-04-09, Neil said article-spinning software produces low-quality,
  unreadable content that Google's readability/bounce signals penalize, illustrating
  with a garbled synonym-swapped sentence.
- As of 2020-04-09, Neil said content scraping (bots copying content from other sites
  without attribution) creates duplicate content that Google will not rank highly,
  even though Google doesn't formally "penalize" for duplicate content per se.
- As of 2020-04-09, Neil said links have "a high correlation with rankings according to
  Moz," but that link farms, PBNs, and expired-domain redirect schemes create
  unnatural link profiles that hurt rankings and may require a Google disavow.
- As of 2020-04-09, Neil recommended finding affordable freelance writers via
  problogger.net rather than using content-spinning or full-automation tools.
