# My Favorite Social Media Strategy You Can Implement ASAP

## Summary
His stated favorite social-growth tactic: use the email list to drive social
following, since email subscribers are already-engaged "enthusiastic leads." Cites a
self-reported client result of up to $70 returned per $1 spent on email marketing at
his agency, and a HubSpot stat that 77% of marketers saw increased email engagement
over the prior 12 months. Five actionable tactics: (1) weekly email out the best
social content to subscribers (his own example: emailing YouTube videos on
Saturdays) to convert blog/email readers into social followers; (2) run live sessions
on social platforms (via tools like Restream or native to Instagram/LinkedIn),
recommending a monthly live session promoted by email; (3) do interviews/co-streams
with other creators and cross-promote to both email lists — "I'll email out to my
list, you email out to your list" — as a mutual follower/awareness exchange; (4) post
platform-unique content at least monthly (weekly if time allows) on each social
channel and tell subscribers about it by email, reasoning that algorithmic reach caps
per-post visibility so followers need to be present on multiple platforms to reliably
see content; (5) run contests promoted via email, requiring actions like reposting,
commenting, or tagging to grow followers/engagement. Notes NP Digital was "awarded
'Agency of the Year'" (self-reported, unspecified awarding body). Closes with an NP
Digital plug.
