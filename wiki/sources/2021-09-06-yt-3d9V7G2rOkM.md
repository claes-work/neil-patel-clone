# Should You Design Your Landing Pages From Scratch or Use Templates?

## Summary
Compares building a landing page from scratch (needs a designer, copywriter, marketer,
and developer; harder to manage load speed/image optimization; more A/B testing and
higher odds of early failure) against using battle-tested templates (LeadPages,
Unbounce, ClickFunnels, Instapage, HubSpot named), which come pre-optimized by
category (book, SaaS, service-based) and let a business focus on driving traffic
instead of build quality. Cites a **Portent study**: landing-page conversion rates
dropped by an average of **4.42% per additional second of load time** between 0 and 5
seconds. Recommendation: new/smaller businesses should start with templates; larger,
marketing-mature companies should still start from a template as a validated baseline
but customize/build on top of it rather than fully from scratch.

## Key claims
