# How to Create Content That Ranks in Local Areas - Module 2 - Lesson 1 - Local SEO Unlocked

## Summary
Local SEO Unlocked, module 2 lesson 1. Three-part local content-creation framework:
(1) identify local keywords — study market/search intent, head terms vs. long-tail
terms (favors long-tail as less competitive/higher-converting, especially early), four
local-modifier types (superlative like "best/top," occasion like "fall/Christmas,"
type, and special-offering modifiers), and competitor keyword-gap mining via
Ubersuggest; (2) build local content — a writing workflow (ideation sheet → outline in
≤45 min with the hook as the critical element → drafting in Google Docs, coaching
writers on Google's **E-A-T** (expertise/authority/trust) as a citation-and-data
discipline → publish with consistent NAP+website in the footer/header); (3) beat
competitor content — a repeatable Ubersuggest workflow (Keyword Ideas Report → Top
Pages → group related keywords → build pages around them → evaluate multi-format
opportunities like video).
