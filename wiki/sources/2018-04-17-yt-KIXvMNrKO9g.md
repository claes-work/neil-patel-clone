# 5 Proven Ways to Make Money on Social Media (No Product Needed)

_Neil solo, direct-to-camera. All persona data._

## Summary
Neil argues you don't need your own product to monetize a social audience and lists five
methods plus a bonus. **(1) Promote others' products via affiliate** — even Amazon/Best
Buy have affiliate programs; he drives signups to tools like Ahrefs/BuzzSumo/SEMrush and
could easily get commissions. **(2) Partner with a product-owner who lacks an audience**
and be the personal brand behind it — take an equity cut, not just per-sale (Kardashians;
Beats by Dre + Jimmy Iovine → Apple for >$1B). **(3) Put ads in your videos** (YouTube
auto-ads, ~$2–3K/month) — modest but can buy financial freedom, which he values over
raw income. **(4) Paid influencer posts** on contract (per-month, not per-sale; Fit Tea
example). **(5) Premium content** via Patreon/subscription (exclusive long-form videos or
articles). **Bonus**: no product needed — white-label/drop-ship someone else's product
and keep the margin. Throughout he frames method selection as "spaghetti against the
wall" — test them all.

## Key claims
As of 2018-04-17, Neil states:
- **You don't need your own product** to make money on social — five models work.
- **(1) Affiliate promotion**: sign up for existing affiliate programs (Amazon, Best Buy,
  and SEO tools like Ahrefs/BuzzSumo/SEMrush) and use tracked links. He drives those tools
  real signups and could get commissions if he asked.
- **(2) Be the personal brand for someone else's product**: influencer/brand-behind-
  product deals; take **a fraction of the business (e.g. 2–10%)**, not just per-sale.
  Examples: **Kardashians** (hair extensions/makeup selling out), **Beats by Dre** (Dr.
  Dre's brand + Jimmy Iovine's connections → sold to **Apple for over $1 billion**).
- **(3) Ads in videos**: YouTube auto-inserts ads; can earn **~$2–3K/month** — small but
  can free you from a job. **"Money isn't everything… aim for financial freedom."**
- **(4) Paid influencer marketing**: get paid a **monthly retainer/contract** to post
  (e.g. one photo/week or /month), not per-sale (Fit Tea-style promos).
- **(5) Premium content**: charge via **Patreon**/subscription for exclusive, detailed
  videos or articles (he could sell step-by-step long-form but chooses not to).
- **Bonus — white-label/drop-ship**: sell someone else's product (e.g. protein powder),
  they ship it, keep the margin (charge $50, pay $25); no inventory or holding costs.
- **Test everything**: treat models as spaghetti against the wall; one may fit you better.
