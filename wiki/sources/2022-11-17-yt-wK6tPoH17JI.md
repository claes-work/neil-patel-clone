# How I grew my email subscriber list to 873,049 subscribers

## Summary
Explains how he grew his email list to "873,049 subscribers," claiming an open rate
"of over 30%" (~260,000 opens per send). Five strategies: (1) Gate a free tool or piece
of content behind email signup — cites his own experience buying Ubersuggest in 2017,
making it free, and using the free-tool funnel to collect emails; also gates post-
webinar ebooks/content the same way. (2) Run an explicit opt-in/opt-out campaign rather
than forcing subscription, citing a 2021 Campaign Monitor report that average email
unsubscribe rate is "0.1%" — argues this de-risks giving people an opt-out and improves
list quality/engagement. (3) Lead with incentives (sign-up offers/discounts), citing a
stat that up to "60%" of users sign up for newsletters specifically for offers, and
that "21%" of sent emails are opened within the first hour. (4) Partner with like-
minded brands to cross-pollinate audiences, citing stats that "57%" of organizations
use partnerships to acquire customers and that high-maturity partnership programs can
generate "28%" of revenue versus an "18%" average from paid search. (5) Run contests/
sweepstakes requiring email signup to enter, citing a claim that contest signups have a
"3.7% higher conversion rate" versus other CTAs; mentions giving away things like an
hour consultation with himself as an incentive. Closes with an NP Digital ("agency of
the year") plug.
