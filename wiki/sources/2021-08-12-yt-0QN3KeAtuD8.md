# The Most Effective Ways to Market Your Business With No Budget

## Summary
A wide zero-budget marketing-tactics list. SEO via Ubersuggest's Content Ideas report
plus outreach to people who already shared/linked comparable content (via Twitter
search). **Google My Business** for local rankings. **PR via HARO** (Help A Reporter
Out) — answering reporter queries for free press mentions, named explicitly as a
budget-free publicity channel (not previously logged this specifically). Public
speaking, framed as low-volume but high-conversion audience-building. Guest
posting/cold outreach on industry sites (names Moz, Search Engine Journal, Search
Engine Land as examples), stressing referral traffic over link-building as the goal.
Facebook-group participation (give before you sell). Email marketing under an
80/20 give/sell framing, with a **more specific personal ratio**: "I myself probably
educate 90, 95%, sell no more than five to 10%" — a tighter number than the general
80/20 rule stated elsewhere. Video marketing across all platforms; going live, citing
**Tai Lopez** by name as an early live-streaming adopter and framing live video as
platforms competing with television. Influencer deals via influence.co. Infographics
via the Content Ideas report. Applying for business awards for credibility (not
traffic). Brand partnerships/co-promotions (toaster/microwave cross-sell example).
