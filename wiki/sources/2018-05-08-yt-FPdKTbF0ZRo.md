# 3 Outstanding Wordpress Plugins That Will Make Your Website Go Viral

## Summary

A short solo video recommending three WordPress plugins to make content spread. (1) Click to Tweet — embed tweetable factoids/quotes throughout an article, not just once or twice; Neil spreads it five or six times (intro, body, conclusion) across long-form posts to maximize Twitter shares and traffic. He notes he used it heavily before his Twitter audience was large and still uses it on every new blog his team launches. (2) Referral Candy — build Uber/Dropbox-style two-sided referral incentives; you don't need a physical product, you can gate access to an info product behind inviting a friend. (3) Invite Referral — "unlock when we hit X shares" mechanics that encourage the community to share content in exchange for bonuses/cheat sheets. He frames the brevity as the point: three effective plugins beat 20–100 plugins. He also drops a growth aside — leave a comment with your Twitter handle and, if he likes your content, he may follow you and share it to his audience.

## Key claims

- As of 2018-05-08, Neil recommends Click to Tweet and says the trick is spreading it five or six times through a post (intro, body, conclusion) rather than once or twice, to generate more Twitter shares and traffic.
- As of 2018-05-08, Neil states that page-one Google results tend to have over 2,000 words, and advises writing 2,000+ word evergreen articles.
- As of 2018-05-08, Neil recommends Referral Candy for two-sided referral incentives (modeled on Uber's/Dropbox's "invite a friend, both get credit"), noting you can gate an info product behind inviting a friend rather than giving it away free.
- As of 2018-05-08, Neil recommends Invite Referral for "unlock when we reach X shares" mechanics that encourage the community to share content in exchange for bonuses or cheat sheets.
- As of 2018-05-08, Neil argues you don't need 20, 50, or 100 WordPress plugins — three effective ones are enough for virality.
