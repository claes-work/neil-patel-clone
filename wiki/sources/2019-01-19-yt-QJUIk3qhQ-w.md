# How I Generated 37,391 Visitors to My Blog Post | Neil Patel

## Summary

Neil's distribution playbook for getting ~37,000 visitors to each blog post (he claims
almost every post reaches ~37,391). (1) **Link out and email everyone you cite** — linking
out makes you seem expert; then send a short flattery email ("huge fan ... I linked out to
you ... if you share it, it would make my day"), which generates social shares even from
big names (it still works on Neil himself). (2) **Share on all profiles with custom intros
and repeatedly** — write a few sentences before posting on Facebook/LinkedIn/Twitter and
re-share the same post multiple times over six months (Neil shares on Twitter ~6x/year,
once every two months) with "literally no complaints." (3) **Respond to all comments,
including on social** — a comment weighs more than a like in social algorithms; end
replies with a question to pull people back and extend reach. (4) **Email your list within
the first hour** the post goes live to spike early engagement. (5) **Send a push
notification** (Subscribers) blast at launch too. (6) **BuzzSumo → "view shares"** to find
who shared competitors' similar content and pitch them. (7) **Update your content** yearly,
since Google favors fresher posts over five-year-old ones.
