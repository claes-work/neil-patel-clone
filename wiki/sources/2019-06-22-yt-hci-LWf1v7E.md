# The Ultimate SEO Checklist For New Websites | Get Traffic & Rankings FAST

## Summary

Neil gives a **four-step SEO checklist for a brand-new website**. (1) **Map out your content
strategy** — topics and keywords, using Buzzsumo, SEMrush, Ahrefs, or Ubersuggest to see
what's popular for competitors so you don't waste resources on content no one cares about.
(2) **Build a backlink hit list** — put competitor URLs into a backlinks tool (or Ahrefs),
but specifically find their **most relevant content** related to yours, see who links to it,
and outreach those linkers because you have a better, more up-to-date, more thorough piece.
(3) **Accessibility** — is the site mobile-compatible and fast? Delete unnecessary plugins;
speed is part of Google's algorithm; check with Google PageSpeed on both desktop and mobile.
(4) **Content distribution** — push each post to all social profiles simultaneously, do
Instagram and Facebook **live stories** (swipe-up to the blog post), email the people you
linked to for shares, and send an **email blast plus push notification** on every new post
(tools: Hello Bar, Mailchimp for email; subscribers for push).
