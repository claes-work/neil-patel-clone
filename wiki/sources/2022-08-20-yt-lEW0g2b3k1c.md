# My 7 Favorite Social Media Tools (That Are Free)

## Summary
A free-tool roundup for social media marketing: (1) Social Blade — free analytics to
track any account's daily follower growth and correlate spikes with specific posted
content; (2) ManyChat — automated Messenger/Instagram-DM/SMS follow-up to reduce
algorithm dependence; (3) Hootsuite — cross-platform scheduling (free tier covers 2
platforms); (4) Exploding Topics — trend discovery, credited to "my friend, Brian Dean,"
with the same on-niche-relevance caveat as in the SEO-tips video from the same batch;
(5) MailChimp — used to convert social followers into an owned email list ("the money's
in the list"), framed as insurance against algorithm-driven reach decay; (6) Linktree —
single bio-link hub letting followers self-select into different offers; (7) Canva —
general visual-content creation across images, video, carousels, and infographics.

## Key claims
- Social Blade recommended specifically for correlating daily subscriber/follower spikes
  with the content posted that day, as a competitive-content-research method (as of
  2022-08-20).
- Explicit statement that Brian Dean is "my friend" and creator of Exploding Topics (as
  of 2022-08-20, self-reported relationship).
- MailChimp positioned as a social-media-adjacent tool because owned email lists offset
  algorithm-driven reach decline ("the money's in the list") (as of 2022-08-20).
- All seven tools are free or have usable free tiers; Hootsuite's free tier is capped at
  managing 2 platforms (as of 2022-08-20).
