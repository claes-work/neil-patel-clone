# How To Repurpose Your Blog Content For Social Media | Content Marketing Strategy

## Summary

Neil argues you should **rarely create net-new content just for social** — instead repurpose
blog content for efficiency and the "biggest bang for your marketing bucks." Five tactics:
(1) **Share every new blog post** across Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram (including an
Instagram Story with a swipe-up to the post). (2) **Re-post the full text** of the article
natively on Facebook/LinkedIn about a week later to harvest engagement again. (3) **Cut content
into micro-content** — chunks and quotes for LinkedIn/Facebook/Instagram, and pay someone on
**Fiverr** ~$5–$10 to turn ~50 quotes into images, because image-based quotes perform well.
(4) **Turn blog posts into video** shot on your phone — LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, and
Facebook all favor video over text. (5) **Repost your best content at least once a year** (he's
done it quarterly, and even 3× in 24 hours on Twitter, though Facebook hates that) to squeeze
more traffic out of the same asset without creating anything new.
