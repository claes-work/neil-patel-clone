# Does Blogging Still Work In 2019 | Neil Patel

## Summary

Neil's answer: **blogging still works in 2019, but it has changed** — you now need
unique, high-quality content plus conversion and distribution work. Tips: (1) **Quality
over quantity** — with over a billion blogs (roughly one per seven people), don't
regurgitate; write new, unique posts weekly or monthly, which earns social shares,
backlinks, and higher rankings. (2) **Respond to comments** — treat the blog as a
communication channel; end posts with a real question to encourage comments. (3)
**Encourage conversation** — write conversationally using "you" and "I," and keep
paragraphs to ~5–6 lines so posts are skimmable. (4) **Add calls to action** — traffic
without CTAs produces no sales; put CTAs in the sidebar (as on neilpatel.com) driving to
services/homepage. (5) **Collect emails** ("the money's in the list") with a free tool
like hellobar.com and a lead magnet (e.g. a "double your traffic in 30 days" ebook), using
a dating/marriage analogy — people say yes once they know you. (6) **Collect push
notifications** (subscribers) to pull people back and promote products. (7) **Leverage
MobileMonkey** (free chatbot tool) for high click-through rates.
