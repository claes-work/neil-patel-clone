# How To Market Your Instagram Content — Instagram Unlocked, Module 2, Lesson 1

## Summary
Opens Module 2 of Instagram Unlocked (marketing/growth, following Module 1's
profile/content-type foundation). Frames Instagram's algorithm as optimizing for
dwell time/user experience, and argues the full engagement stack — feed posts + Story
+ Highlights + Live + IGTV + DMs + comment engagement (plus Reels) — has to be worked
together. Recommends posting consistently (ideally 1-2x/day, "worst case" once every
48 hours), remixing/reposting top performers, and actively liking/commenting on
similar accounts found via hashtag search — explicitly credits this grind tactic to
Gary Vaynerchuk's early engagement strategy ("It's one of the key strategies to Gary
Vaynerchuk success... he was doing this all day long, back in the day on Twitter...
and still does it today as well as I do because it works"). Covers blue-tick
verification (rates it low-impact personally); influencer collabs; hashtag strategy
(geolocation + Ubersuggest-sourced keyword hashtags, stuffing extras into comments);
automation tools (Instashot for photo/quote-graphic editing, Commenbot for automated
comments — rated as working "somewhat," worse than manual); getting featured on large
third-party accounts for organic reach; a content-format ranking (vertical video,
Stories — especially personal ones, influencer marketing, Shopping, Live, DIY/behind-
the-scenes, giveaways, motivational quotes; bright images outperforming darker ones
in his observation); caption craft (short, relevant, front-load value); and closes
with "Instagram SEO" — placing primary/secondary Ubersuggest-sourced keywords in
display name, username, bio, image alt text, and captions, while warning against
black-hat growth services (fake likes/followers).

## Key claims
- As of 2020-09-21, Neil explicitly attributes his own account-engagement grind
  tactic (liking/commenting on similar accounts to draw them back) to Gary
  Vaynerchuk's playbook, and says he still does it himself because "it works."
- As of 2020-09-21, Neil's personal assessment of Instagram's blue-tick verification:
  "I have one, I don't notice one bit of a difference from when I didn't have one to
  when I have one. It's not a big deal, it does build trust."
- As of 2020-09-21, Neil's posting-frequency floor: "once every 48 hours" worst case,
  ideally once or twice daily.
- As of 2020-09-21, Neil rates automated-comment tools (e.g. Commenbot) as working
  "somewhat well... not as well as you want," with manual engagement outperforming
  automation.
- As of 2020-09-21, Neil promotes an "Instagram SEO" practice: using Ubersuggest to
  find keywords, then placing them in display name/username/bio/alt-text/captions —
  an explicit cross-application of his core SEO toolset to a non-Google platform.
