# How to Rank High on Google Without Writing Content

## Summary

Neil argues that written articles are only one of several ways to earn Google traffic, and lays out five tactics to rank without writing content. **(1) Create a podcast** — Google now indexes and auto-transcribes podcast audio, making it searchable in Google Podcasts; he cites his own daily 5-minute show **Marketing School**, where he and his co-host batch-record a full month of episodes in roughly four to five hours across one or two sit-downs, and points to Google's podcast guidelines for indexability. **(2) Double down on videos** — video appears in 16% of US SERPs per Rank Ranger (21% per Moz), and the videos that rank best are at least five minutes long, not one-to-two-minute clips. **(3) Get guest bloggers** — at Kissmetrics he grew to over a million visitors a month without writing his own content, recruiting guest writers (sometimes paid, eventually free) and publishing seven to ten articles a week for over a year; the traffic accrues to your site. **(4) Optimize old content** — use Search Console to find page-two/three articles and improve CTR via title tags and meta descriptions, and use **Ubersuggest** to fold long-tail keyword variations into existing copy without rewriting. **(5) Create tools** — buy cheap scripts from **CodeCanyon** (calculators, his first SEO analyzer came from there); tools are his neilpatel.com's most popular pages and his favorite tactic because they continually attract backlinks that lift the whole site.
