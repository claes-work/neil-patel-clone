## Summary

A single-product walkthrough of the free **WooCommerce Google Listings & Ads**
extension: cites Matt Mullenweg (State of the Word 2020) that WooCommerce facilitated
over $20B in sales in 2020, and a Datanyze stat that WooCommerce powers 27% of all
online stores (highest e-commerce market share). Cites a Catalyst Digital report that
50% of shoppers discover new products/brands on Google. Explains the plugin syncs a
WooCommerce store's product/store data to Google, enabling both free unpaid product
listings (Google Shopping tab, Search, Images) and paid Smart Shopping Campaigns
reaching Google's networks (YouTube, Gmail, Search, Display). Advises richer product
data for better placement odds, notes a $150 ad-credit signup incentive, and a bonus
tip to leave Smart Shopping campaigns untouched for a minimum two-week learning period
so Google's automation can optimize targeting.
