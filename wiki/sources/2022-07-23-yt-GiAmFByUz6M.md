# Exposing My Social Media Strategy

## Summary
Neil Patel states his own follower counts as of 2022-07-23 (1M+ YouTube, 1M+ Facebook, 315K+
Instagram, 440K+ LinkedIn, 430K+ Twitter) and reiterates that he does not optimize for follower
growth, measuring success instead by revenue generated (noting YouTube is now NP Digital's #3 channel
for driving clients — consistent with [[2022-07-18-yt-CeWlh0rEE-8]]). He lays out three rules he
says have worked unchanged since he started, despite algorithm changes: (1) post consistently, at
least daily up to ~3x/day, because reach and content performance are unpredictable and volume is how
you generate enough "hits"; (2) target content at the ideal customer rather than the masses — he
notes his "life lessons" content outperforms his marketing-tips content on views/likes but converts
worse, since no one pays him for life lessons; (3) genuinely help others, which he says he stumbled
into "10 plus years ago" by accident and only later recognized as building brand evangelists who
generate referral leads and sales.

## Key claims
- Self-reported follower counts as of 2022-07-23: 1M+ YouTube, 1M+ Facebook, 315K+ Instagram, 440K+
  LinkedIn, 430K+ Twitter.
- Rule 1: post consistently, daily minimum up to ~3x/day, because platform reach and content
  performance are inherently unpredictable — volume increases the odds of "hits" (as of 2022-07-23).
- Rule 2: target content at the ideal customer, not the broadest possible audience; cites his own
  "life lessons" content out-performing marketing-tips content on views/likes while converting far
  fewer leads (as of 2022-07-23).
- Rule 3: genuinely (not performatively) help the audience, which builds "brand evangelists" who
  promote the brand unpaid; says he adopted this instinctively "10 plus years ago" before recognizing
  its revenue effect (as of 2022-07-23).
- Reiterates YouTube as NP Digital's third-most-popular channel for driving clients, aligned with
  [[2022-07-18-yt-CeWlh0rEE-8]] (as of 2022-07-23).
