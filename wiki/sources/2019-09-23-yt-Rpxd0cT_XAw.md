# How to Get Your First 1,000 Visitors Without Spending Money | How to Get Traffic FAST

## Summary

Neil argues the first 1,000 visitors are the hardest and most crucial — harder to get than the next 10,000 — and lays out a free, six-step playbook. **Step 1: identify your target audience** (where they hang out, their fears, goals, age, gender, dominant social channel) so you don't build content for the wrong people. **Step 2: research what they want** by mining **Amazon two- and three-star book reviews** (which reveal what a book is missing), **Quora** questions and most-upvoted answers, and popular **Reddit** subreddits/top posts — taking notes on everything. **Step 3: create the most epic, mind-blowing resource** on one topic: bullet out every idea, then use **Ubersuggest's content ideas report** to validate popularity across social shares, backlinks, and search traffic (don't rely on SEO alone — check referral and social too), read blog comments to find gaps, and write **25 headlines**, cut the weakest 20, keep the best five, and crowd-source the winner via a poll in a niche Facebook group. **Step 4: add a gated content upgrade** (PDF, ebook, crash course) using OnePress Social Locker on WordPress so people must share to unlock it — driving hundreds of social shares. **Step 5: use the content for promotion** — link out to others, tell them, and ask for a share (higher take rate) or a backlink (better long-term referral traffic and rankings), following up with a different headline/ask if ignored. **Step 6: enable push notifications** (plus Mobile Monkey for Messenger, Hello Bar for email) and pixel visitors with Google AdWords and Facebook to retarget them back. Follow all of it and you reach 1,000 visitors without spending a dollar.
