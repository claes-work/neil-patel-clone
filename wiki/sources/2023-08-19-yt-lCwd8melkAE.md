# Reaction to LinkedIn's Latest Features

## Summary
Short co-hosted reaction segment on new LinkedIn features. Eric opens with a company-interest
recruiting feature (using his own agency, Single Grain, as the example) that lets users signal
interest in working somewhere, visible to that company's recruiters. Neil's take: this improves
recruiting efficiency by surfacing warm-candidate signals instead of relying only on job postings
or cold manual outbound. On LinkedIn's new "collaborative articles" (editorial-team-plus-AI
content) and collab-tagging, Neil draws the direct parallel to Instagram Reels collaborator
mechanics — multiplying reach for both tagged parties — and cites a concrete example: an Eric Siu
Reel that grew to roughly 207,000 impressions after adding Neil as a collaborator, versus its
organic trajectory which "would've grown naturally either way... but it just grew much faster." He
predicts the same reach multiplier will apply on LinkedIn once collab-tagging is available.
Closing takeaways he gives: (1) leverage collab-tagging for reach, and (2) actively respond and
engage with people on business-profile posts, since engagement builds the relationships that
convert into sales — calling those "the two big takeaways" from a longer list of minor features
(messaging, writing suggestions, About-section skills, content-performance analytics, LinkedIn
Live, Newsletters, Audio Events) that Eric runs through.

## Key claims
- States LinkedIn's new "interested in working here" recruiting-intent feature reduces manual
  outbound-recruiting effort by surfacing warm candidate signals directly to a company's recruiters
  (self-reported opinion, dated 2023-08-19).
- States LinkedIn's collab-tagging (modeled on Instagram Reels collaborator mechanics)
  significantly multiplies reach for both tagged accounts, citing a specific Eric Siu Reel that
  grew to ~207,000 impressions after adding Neil as a collaborator, growing "much faster" than its
  organic trajectory would have (self-reported/observed example, dated 2023-08-19).
- Names two top LinkedIn takeaways: use collab-tagging for reach, and actively engage/respond on
  business-profile posts to build the relationships that convert into sales (self-reported
  framework, dated 2023-08-19).

## Notable verbatim quotes
