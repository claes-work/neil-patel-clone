# My Favorite SEO Hack to Increase Website Traffic | Neil Patel

## Summary

A single-tactic monologue on Neil's favorite under-used traffic hack: combining Google
Search Console with Ubersuggest instead of using them separately. The workflow: (1) in
Google Search Console, find the keywords already driving the majority of your traffic
(sorted by clicks) and note which page each ranks; (2) drop those head terms into
Ubersuggest to pull all the Google-suggest long-tail variations; (3) integrate the
relevant long-tails back into the ranking page — sometimes a straight add, sometimes
new paragraphs, and irrelevant ones become a separate article; (4) update the title tag
and meta description with the most popular terms; (5) resubmit the URL in Search Console
for re-indexing. Within ~30 days the page that already ranked for a head term also starts
ranking for the long-tail phrases, adding traffic. Neil frames it as simple and free
(both tools are free) but rarely done because people use the two tools separately rather
than combined.
