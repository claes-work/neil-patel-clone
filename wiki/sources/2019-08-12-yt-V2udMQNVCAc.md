# The Anatomy Of A Perfect SEO Campaign

## Summary

Neil walks through how to assemble the individual SEO tactics he teaches into one coherent
campaign, in five steps. (1) **Map content gaps** — put a competitor's URL into Ubersuggest,
open the **Top Pages** report (shows their popular pages and the keywords each ranks for),
export to CSV, delete topics you already cover, and target what's left. Write those articles
using the Skyscraper Technique (out-detail the current top 10). (2) **Identify internal-linking
opportunities** — via Ubersuggest or Google Search Console, find your best-ranking pages and
consistently link to them from existing pages to push them higher. (3) **Leverage existing
ranking assets** — a "land and expand" play (caption renders it "LAN and expanse"): in Search
Console find keywords you get impressions for that **aren't actually in your page text/title/
meta**, add them, and mine their long-tail variants in Ubersuggest (if you rank for the head
term you'll likely rank for the long-tail without link building). (4) **Keep a cohesive content
calendar** — consistency is critical; he recounts a month off blogging that dropped his traffic
and took **three months to recover**. (5) **Build content clusters + pillar pages** — a deep
(~10,000-word) pillar page, surrounded by sub-articles on long-tail phrases, questions
(AnswerThePublic, Quora), and comparisons, all interlinked back to the pillar.
