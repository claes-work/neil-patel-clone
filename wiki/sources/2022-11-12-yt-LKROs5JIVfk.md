# Here's What Google Classifies as Helpful Content

## Summary
Breaks down Google's "helpful content update" (content for humans, not search engines)
into seven practices. (1) Only write on what you know — recounts personally building
sites in medical/financial/poker niches he wasn't expert in, which underperformed
despite some short-term rankings, versus his marketing content which does well because
he's a genuine expert. (2) Focus a site on one niche/industry rather than spreading
across topics, per Google's own stated preference, contrasting with broad sites like
Wikipedia. (3) Make content executable — end articles with a clearly labeled
"Conclusion" heading summarizing key takeaways. (4) Continually update content (aim for
at least once a year, his own team reviews content quarterly to twice-yearly); decide
scope of updates by benchmarking against currently-ranking competitors rather than an
arbitrary percentage changed. (5) Add "secret sauce" — personal experience/insight not
found elsewhere, to earn shares and backlinks. (6) Appeal to multiple content-
consumption preferences (video, infographic, image, audio, text). (7) Do keyword
research via Ubersuggest's Keyword Ideas tool (Related/Questions/Prepositions tabs) to
ensure topical thoroughness. Closes with an NP Digital plug (helpful-content-update
recovery/prep services).

## Key claims
- States his own non-expert-niche sites (medical, financial, poker) underperformed
  long-term despite occasional short-term rankings, attributed to lack of genuine
  expertise (self-reported personal history, dated 2022-11-12).
- States Google has said it prefers focused, single-topic sites over broad-topic sites
  (paraphrased Google guidance, not directly quoted/sourced, dated 2022-11-12).
- Recommends benchmarking against current top-ranking competitors to decide how much
  to update an article, rather than a fixed rewrite percentage (self-reported practice,
  dated 2022-11-12).
- States his own team reviews content "at least once a quarter, if not twice a year"
  (self-reported operational detail, dated 2022-11-12).
