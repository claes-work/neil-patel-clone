# The Best Way to Use WordPress

## Summary
A short tutorial recommending the "All in One SEO" WordPress plugin. Opens
citing 455M+ websites hosted on WordPress as of 2022. Four features covered:
(1) custom SEO titles/meta descriptions per page, with a search-preview and
canonical-URL support — recommends keeping title tags under 50 characters
(often under 40 performs even better, per NP Digital's own findings) and
researching competitor titles/descriptions on Google for keyword ideas; (2)
on-page optimization — the plugin evaluates keyword usage across title, meta
description, URL, intro, subheadings, and checks content length and internal/
external links; (3) readability analysis — checks images/visuals, paragraph
and sentence length, passive-voice usage, transition words, and repetition;
(4) customized search-result appearance — full control over how posts/pages
render in SERPs. Closes with an NP Digital plug.

## Key claims
- Cites (as of 2022, as cited, dated 2023-01-14): more than 455 million
  websites are hosted on WordPress.
- Self-reports (NP Digital's own findings, dated 2023-01-14): title tags
  under 50 characters get more clicks than longer ones, with tags under 40
  characters performing even better in many cases.

## Notable verbatim quotes
> With your title, short and to the point is better than using up all the characters.
