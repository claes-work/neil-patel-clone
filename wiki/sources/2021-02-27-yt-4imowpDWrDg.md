# 7 Landing Page Hacks That'll Double Your Sales - Part 2

> ⚠️ **Title/content mismatch**: the title promises landing-page hacks, but the video
> is an open audience Q&A segment at an affiliate-marketing conference (attendee
> questions from the floor, mentions of "Berlin"/AWA — likely Affiliate World Europe —
> and a "Merry Christmas" sign-off, so probably recorded around a December event and
> published later). Landing pages come up only briefly (a remarketing-pixel tactic and
> a tool recommendation).

## Summary
Fields rapid-fire audience questions. On landing pages/AI: predicts virtual reality
(e.g., skincare product demos) will see practical marketing adoption sooner than AI,
partly because of tightening EU privacy regulation; recommends pixeling visitors who
click a landing-page CTA (even without the sale) and remarketing them via YouTube/
Facebook video ads for 7 days with the "opposite pitch" of the original page; names
LeadPages and ClickFunnels as go-to landing-page tools (custom builds need cheap
Upwork labor). On Messenger marketing: says its click-through/conversion rates have
declined over the prior 6–12 months, so he now favors push notifications served from
HTTPS squeeze pages instead. On SaaS/free-tier business models: states typical
free-to-paid SaaS conversion is 4–5%, up to 8–9% for the best operators, and free
tiers work because they lower blended ad costs; states his own SaaS companies have
sold at 6–10x annual revenue while only breakeven on profit, because of recurring-
revenue predictability (private-equity buyers leverage bank debt against the
recurring cash flow). States his neilpatel.com non-paid email list is ~550,000 active
subscribers with a 30%+ open rate, attributed to never selling to the list directly.
On high-ticket ($2,000+) offer sales: recommends webinars over content marketing,
citing an unnamed ad-buyer contact ("Jason Hornik") who reportedly got him to a
2:1 return on ad spend and ~$500–600K/month in webinar-funnel revenue, and cites
"Sam Ovens" as an acquaintance selling eight-figure high-ticket offers via webinars.
