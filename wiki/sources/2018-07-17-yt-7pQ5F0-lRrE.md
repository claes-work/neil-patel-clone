# SEO Hack! How to Optimize for Google RankBrain (2018)

## Summary

A solo tactical breakdown of Google **RankBrain** and how to optimize for it. Neil explains
RankBrain is Google's machine-learning/AI component of the algorithm, which (he says) Google
found produces ~10% more accurate results than a human engineer, so Google keeps improving it
to maximize user satisfaction and keep people from switching to Bing/Yahoo. Because RankBrain
watches user behavior — click-through, dwell time, whether you click back to the SERP ("pogo-
sticking") — his four optimization tips are all UX/engagement plays. **(1) Fine-tune your
copy, especially title tags and meta descriptions**: use the searched keyword, action words
(how-to, effortless, easy, simple), and numbers/list formats (he analogizes to how-to/list
headlines on checkout-aisle magazines) to lift click-through. **(2) Increase time on site by
embedding audio and video** — he points to embedding his YouTube videos and podcast audio on
the neilpatel.com blog, which both cross-promotes and keeps visitors longer, improving user
metrics and lifting his search traffic month over month; no media of your own? record on your
iPhone or embed relevant YouTube videos. **(3) Improve user experience** — he says the ultimate
signal is whether the user finds what they want, not keyword density or backlink count;
hire designers via Dribbble, or use a free Crazy Egg trial to watch mouse recordings and fix
where users get stuck (its WYSIWYG editor and split tests help). **(4) Never stop** — keep
testing copy and UX every month; rankings climb slowly, even for authoritative sites (Forbes,
Business Insider), rather than jumping from page 10 to page 1.
