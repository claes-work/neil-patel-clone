# How I Stay Ahead of Google Algorithm Updates

## Summary
Tactical five-step playbook for tracking and adapting to Google algorithm updates.
Frames Google's ranking system as "over 200 components," not one lever, so a core
update can take weeks to fully roll out. Steps: (1) set up Google Alerts for "Google
algorithm" plus follow Google Search Liaison on Twitter, and use Search Console/GA to
spot traffic or conversion swings; (2) avoid blackhat tactics (keyword stuffing, hidden
tags/cloaking, bought/private-network backlinks, doorway pages) since gains don't last
and risk penalties or blacklisting — read Google Search Essentials instead; (3)
optimize user experience (site load in 1-2 seconds, working internal links, no 404s),
citing HubSpot that 88% of customers are less likely to return to a site with bad UX
and 90% stopped using a poorly-performing app; (4) keep content fresh — states he
personally stopped chasing every update and instead prioritizes "exceptional,
user-friendly content" plus regular content refreshes and consistent keyword research;
(5) use Search Console and SEMrush Sensor to diagnose which update hit a site and how.
Tool mentions: Google Alerts, Search Console, Google Analytics, DeepCrawl, BuzzSumo,
Found, Crazy Egg (heat maps), Moz/SEMrush-class "algorithm analytics tools." Closes
with an NP Digital plug.
