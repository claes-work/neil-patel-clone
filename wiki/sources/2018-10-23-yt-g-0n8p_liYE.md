# How to Recover Your (DYING) Facebook Traffic

## Summary

Solo tactical. Neil explains **Facebook's declining organic reach** — reach shrank over time as
the algorithm favored highly-engaged fans, and by 2018 **posts with external links perform worse**
than posts that keep people on Facebook. Four tactics to recover traffic despite the algorithm:
(1) **Put the link in the first comment, not the status update** — credited to **Brian Dean** —
so Facebook doesn't down-rank the post for linking off-platform; the status update teases "check
out the first comment." (2) **Build engagement before promoting** — a day or two before a
promotional link, post highly-resonant content (a quote/testimonial) to spike engagement, so when
you later post a link, your fan base is already "engaged" and Facebook shows the link to more people.
(He explicitly flags an Elon Musk "testimonial" as **made up for illustration** — see flag below.)
(3) **Boost your best posts** — not to give Facebook money per se, but to force-deliver content to
inactive fans; re-engaging them signals Facebook to show your **next** (unboosted) posts too.
(4) **Use Facebook Messenger** via **MobileMonkey** — subscribers who chat with you can be sent
new-post notifications; Neil claims **high open rates and 60%+ click-through** on messages to ~100
people, accepting unsubscribes as normal (remaining subscribers are die-hard fans). Closing ethos:
"it's social media… it's also about helping out the other person," not just promoting your business.

## Key claims

- As of 2018-10-23, **Facebook organic reach has steadily declined** and **link posts underperform**
  content that keeps users on-platform.
- As of 2018-10-23, the **first-comment link trick** (attributed to **Brian Dean**) avoids the
  link-post penalty — put the URL in the first comment, tease it in the status update.
- As of 2018-10-23, **priming engagement** with resonant content 1–2 days before a promo post lifts
  the reach of the later link post (Facebook reads fan engagement as a distribution signal).
- As of 2018-10-23, **boosting your best posts** re-engages inactive fans and improves reach of
  subsequent unboosted posts.
- As of 2018-10-23, **Facebook Messenger via MobileMonkey** yields high open rates and **60%+ CTR**
  to the website on a ~100-person send (self-reported); treat it as a helpful channel, not pure promo.
