# 6 Facebook Ads Tools and Strategies to Beat Your Competition (2019)

## Summary

Six Facebook-ads tactics as costs rise: (1) **spy on competitors' ads** via their Page's
**Info and Ads** tab (Facebook discloses every ad, campaign, landing page, and webinar) so
you copy proven approaches and tweak rather than reinvent; (2) **make your own ads harder to
spy on** by switching your Page to the **video template**, which changes the layout so
snoopers give up; (3) **BigSpy** (bigspy.com) — search by keyword to surface competitors'
ads you didn't know about; (4) **Facebook Audience Insights** — free demographic/interest/
timing data, especially valuable early when you lack a customer list for lookalikes; (5)
**Connectio** (connectio.io) — expands a seed interest into related targetable categories
ranked by popularity; (6) **remarket your own audience** — for visitors who reached a
checkout/lead page but didn't complete, run **video retargeting ads** explaining the
journey, and deliberately flip the **pitch type** (if the landing page is emotional, make
the retargeting ad logical, or vice versa) to get "two shots at the same person" — which he
calls his highest-converting campaign type.

## Key claims

- As of 2019-05-06, Neil said Facebook's **Info and Ads** tab on any Page reveals all a
  competitor's ads, campaigns, landing pages, and webinars — copy what works, then tweak.
- As of 2019-05-06, Neil said switching your Page to the **video template** makes it harder
  for competitors to spy on your ads (layout change deters snoopers).
- As of 2019-05-06, Neil recommended **BigSpy** for keyword-based ad discovery,
  **Audience Insights** for early demographic/interest targeting, and **Connectio** for
  expanding seed interests into related categories.
- As of 2019-05-06, Neil said to **remarket non-converting visitors** with **video ads**,
  and to use the **opposite pitch** (emotional vs. logical) from the landing page — his
  highest-converting campaign type.

## Notable verbatim quotes

> "Did you know Facebook tells you every single thing that your competitors are advertising on? Just go to their page, click on info and ads."

> "If your landing page has emotional appeal on why they should be buying your product, then your remarketing video should have the opposite pitch. ... this gives you two shots at the same person."
