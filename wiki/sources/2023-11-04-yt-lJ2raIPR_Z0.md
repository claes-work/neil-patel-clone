# Is Education a Good Revenue Stream?

## Summary
Neil argues education-based revenue (eBooks, courses, paid speaking, coaching) is a
weak, "shortsighted" business model compared to selling the underlying end result —
contrasting knowledge products (commoditized) with outcome products (higher
perceived value, e.g., supplement companies vs. fitness-education companies). He
recounts giving away his own eBook for free and monetizing via NP Digital services
instead, citing this as a driver of the agency's Inc. 21st-fastest-growing-company
ranking. ★ Bio detail: recorded while in Brazil for Web Summit, with meetings lined
up with Whirlpool, Nestle, Michelin, and a large Brazilian insurance company,
referencing NP Digital's dedicated Brazil and Latin America divisions.

## Key claims (dated, paraphrase unless noted)
- States flatly he "hate[s]" education-based revenue streams (eBooks, courses,
  paid webinars/certifications, paid conference speaking) as shortsighted and
  low-ceiling, despite having done all of them earlier in his career.
  (Neil, 2023-11-04) — agency-entrepreneurship.
- Framework: knowledge products are commoditized (anyone can teach the same
  knowledge), but people pay a premium for the *end result* — analogy: fitness
  supplement/equipment companies (Peloton, Tonal) out-earn pure fitness-education
  companies because they deliver outcomes, not just information.
  (Neil, 2023-11-04) — agency-entrepreneurship / content-marketing.
- ★ Biographical/business detail: gave away his book for free instead of charging,
  monetizing via NP Digital services instead — credits this model shift as part of
  what made NP Digital one of the "21st fastest growing companies" per Inc.
  magazine (self-reported ranking, no year given). (Neil, 2023-11-04) —
  agency-entrepreneurship.
- ★ States his average paid-speaking fee is roughly $50,000 (explicitly: doesn't
  charge $100K, doesn't charge $20-30K either), but says he increasingly avoids

  multi-million-dollar client contracts over speaking fees, since a single closed
  enterprise deal outweighs $50K + travel expenses. (Neil, 2023-11-04) —
  agency-entrepreneurship.
- ★ Bio/location marker: recorded while in Brazil for Web Summit, with meetings
  scheduled with Whirlpool, Nestle, Michelin, and Brazil's largest insurance
