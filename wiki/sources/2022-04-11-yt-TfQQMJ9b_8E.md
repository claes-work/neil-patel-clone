# How to Convert Your Leads Into Sales With These 5 CRM Features

## Summary
A Pipedrive-sponsored video (disclosed upfront; he states he'd have recommended
Pipedrive regardless, having used it "for so many years") framing sales process
and CRM discipline as the key driver behind NP Digital's growth. Self-reports the
agency runs close to 100 sales reps globally, with 48 reps in the "S&B division"
alone (hiring 12–13 more), and credits disciplined process for taking the
business "from nothing in 2018" to the #21 fastest-growing company in the US per
Inc. Magazine's 2021 ranking. Cites Nucleus Research's 2011 finding of an $8.71
return per $1 spent on CRM, arguing that figure has likely grown given how much
more mature CRM automation has become since. Walks through five CRM-selection
criteria — pipelines (visualizing deal stages), automations (reducing manual
sales-rep busywork), lead management (centralized inbox, web forms, live
chat/chatbot qualification), reporting (translating data into forecasts/goals),
and integration (Slack, Trello, Zapier, Gmail) — using Pipedrive's specific
feature set to illustrate each.
