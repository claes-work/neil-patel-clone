# Can You Rank a Website WITHOUT Writing Content?

## Summary
Argues content is not strictly required to rank, though it helps at scale. Cites
HubSpot: 82% of marketers reported actively using content marketing in 2021, up from
70% the prior year, framing content marketing as increasingly competitive but still
essential for major traffic. Contrasts today's competitive SEO landscape with ~10+
years ago when "mediocre" content could rank easily. Lists non-writing tactics to
improve rankings: testing titles/headlines to improve click-through rate in Google
Search Console (retells the Rand Fishkin "best grilled steak" click-pattern
experiment as an example of CTR's ranking effect, explicitly noting it isn't a
scalable tactic, just an illustration); consolidating cannibalizing content into one
authoritative page with 301 redirects (uses his own inability to rank for "SEO" until
he consolidated ~20 similar articles as a personal example); adding internal links
focused on relevance over exact-match anchor text; adding schema/structured markup
(review, product, offer, local-business); optimizing for mobile (responsive design,
AMP). For sourcing content without writing it himself, cites hiring writers via the
ProBlogger job board or guest contributors, and recounts Crazy Egg's strategy of a
small outsourced writing team plus infographics driving "over 200,000 visitors a
month."

## Key claims
- Cites HubSpot data: 82% of marketers actively used content marketing in 2021, up
  from 70% in 2020 (third-party-sourced stat, dated 2022-01-31) — content-marketing.
- States he personally couldn't rank for the keyword "SEO" until he consolidated
  ~20 near-duplicate articles into one page with 301 redirects from the rest
  (self-reported case study, dated 2022-01-31) — seo.
- Cites Crazy Egg (his earlier company, co-founded with Hiten Shah) generating "over
  200,000 visitors a month" via an outsourced guest-contributor writing team plus
  infographics (self-reported historical business figure, dated 2022-01-31,
  unaudited) — seo, content-marketing, agency-entrepreneurship.
