# How to Grow Your Business Using Video on LinkedIn

## Summary

Neil's LinkedIn-specific video tactics, built around the "one simple hack" that LinkedIn is
starved for video and rewards it. Core hack: just uploading a native video gets far more reach
than a text update — he claims a 4–5x difference in most cases. Additional tactics: (1) **Upload
an SRT file** (transcribe via Rev.com) because LinkedIn autoplays muted and viewers at work
follow along via captions. (2) **Use popular keywords** (found via Ubersuggest) in the video
description *and* in LinkedIn hashtags/tags — he uses tags like #digitalmarketing, #seo,
#googleanalytics to pull search traffic. (3) **Make the first line of the description short**
(one sentence) so LinkedIn truncates it with a "see more" link; clicks on "see more" register as
engagement and push the video more viral. (4) **Respond to every comment** and ask a follow-up
question — comments are the main factor LinkedIn's algorithm weighs, so encourage and answer them
fast to keep people returning. (5) **In the video itself, ask viewers to leave a comment** and
pose a question, which naturally drives virality.
