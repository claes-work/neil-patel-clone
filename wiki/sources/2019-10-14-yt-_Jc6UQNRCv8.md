# How to Build 41,142 Backlinks From One Simple Hack

## Summary
Neil argues that the fastest way to earn backlinks at scale — without endless manual outreach or producing tons of original content — is repackaging existing data-driven content as infographics. He cites his own Kissmetrics results as proof: over a two-year period, 47 infographics generated 2,512,596 visitors, 41,142 backlinks from 3,741 unique domains, plus 41,359 tweets and 20,859 likes (Kissmetrics was later redirected into NeilPatel.com). His step-by-step: use Ubersuggest's Content Ideas report to find data-heavy posts that already earn shares, traffic and links; rebuild that data as a visual infographic (Canva or Infogram), citing and linking the original source and adding your own value; keep it to five or six points that flow as a story; then pull the list of sites linking to the original post from the same report and email them offering your infographic to embed. Publishing it on your own site with a WordPress embed-code plugin (WP Embed Code Generator) lets even non-emailed sites copy the embed and link back. Neil closes by noting the tactic "still works today" because most people are too lazy to do it, and that his agency NP Digital still produces infographics for this reason.
