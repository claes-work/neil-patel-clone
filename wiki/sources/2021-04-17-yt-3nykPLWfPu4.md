# The Secret to Being Popular on YouTube

## Summary
Solo tactical video on YouTube growth, opening with his own channel analytics (879,000
views / 50,000 watch-hours in the trailing 28 days at recording time; states he does
not monetize YouTube; YouTube search is his top traffic source at 34.3%). Two-part
method: (1) baseline keyword research via Ubersuggest — target keywords with real
search volume, say them explicitly within the video's spoken content (not just
title/description) since he claims saying a keyword in-video improves ranking for it,
and keep videos 5–10 minutes; (2) the "secret" — aggressively promote every new video
within its first 24 hours via email blasts, browser push notifications (shows live
notification-performance stats, ~6,000 clicks for one back-link video email), and
social/Messenger-bot tools (ManyChat, MobileMonkey), because he states YouTube's
algorithm favors videos that get early engagement velocity, which then compounds into
sustained search ranking.
