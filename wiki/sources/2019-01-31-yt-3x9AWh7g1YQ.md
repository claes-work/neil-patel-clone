# 7 Social Media Hacks That'll Make Your Business Grow Faster

## Summary

A tactical list of seven engagement hacks for turning social presence into traffic and
sales. (1) **Respond to every comment** — Facebook/LinkedIn/Instagram value a comment
more than a like (comments are harder to earn), so replying and adding a question back
compounds engagement and reach. (2) **LinkedIn short-sentence hack** — write one or two
short opening sentences so readers must click "see more," which signals LinkedIn that
people want the content and boosts distribution. (3) **Don't only promote** — post
non-promotional value (quotes, pictures, stories) two-to-three times a week so reach
doesn't tank; higher page/profile authority then lifts your promotional posts too.
(4) **Leverage Instagram/Facebook Stories** — post many stories a day (10–13) with
swipe-ups (cites GaryVee doing this for podcasts/posts). (5) **Cross-promote across all
profiles** — tell each platform's audience to follow you on the others, because only a
fraction of fans ever see any single post. (6) **Use exit pop-ups** — desktop only (on
mobile wait ~30s); Neil says on his old blog quicksprout.com an exit pop-up asking people
to follow him on Facebook generated ~1,000 new fans/week, buildable free with Hello Bar.
(7) **Test video** — social platforms favor video and give it more reach.
