# My Secret Strategies For Global Brand Expansion

_Interview format; unnamed interviewer asks, Neil answers. All Neil-attributed content is
persona data._

## Summary
The companion to [[2018-03-17-yt-IXb_b9JbmkA]] — Neil details the **global expansion** in
progress. **Brazil** has done really well; the **Spanish/Latin America** market grew slower;
**Germany** is getting good traction; the US "crushes anything English-speaking." His model:
**don't repatriate the money — dump it all back into local growth** and hire locals. He claims
the Brazil business hit **$100,000/month in profit within 12 months**, and could reach **~$10M/
year within a few years**, with **no real competition** as the driver. He recounts speaking at
**RD Summit in Florianópolis, Brazil** to **~5,000 people** (mic/audio failed; 96%+ of Brazil
doesn't speak English, so audiences used headsets/translators and still wanted to do business).
Big theme: **hiring philosophy** — he'll **overpay, but makes people earn it first** (work
free or dirt-cheap for **30–90 days**; once they prove themselves he pays **well above market**
and hands them autonomy). He recruits **scrappy, creative, self-directed** people — explicitly
**not** the "Harvard graduate" or "perfect-SAT" person, but someone who **didn't grow up with
money** and had to fight and find creative ways to survive. He closes with **Richard Branson's**
advice (given to him at a shared conference): **a company is built on its people, not the idea**
— so find the right people, pay whatever it takes, and **take care of the people who got you
there** through downturns (recessions, crashes, a "crazy" competitor giving your product away
free) because they'll have your back.

## Key claims
As of 2018-03-24, Neil states:
- **Regional status**: Brazil strong; **Latin America / Spanish** slower; **Germany** gaining
  traction; the US dominates English-speaking.
- **Reinvestment model**: keep the money **in-country**, dump profit back into growth and local
  people rather than bringing it back to the US.
- **Brazil economics (self-reported)**: **$100K/month profit within 12 months**; potential
  **~$10M/year within a few years**, driven by **absence of competition**.
- Spoke at **RD Summit, Florianópolis** to **~5,000 people** despite audio failure and a
  language barrier (**96%+ of Brazil doesn't speak English**).
- **Hiring**: **overpay top performers, but make them prove it first** — free/cheap for
  **30–90 days**, then pay **well above market** with autonomy.
- Recruits **scrappy, creative, self-directed** people who **didn't grow up with money** — not
  Harvard grads or perfect-SAT scorers.
- **Richard Branson's advice** (shared conference): "**you can't build a company without the
  people — it's not the idea, it's the people and the team**."
- **Take care of the people who got you there**; they'll support you through recessions,
  crashes, and predatory competition — "don't be greedy… everyone on the team should be
  rewarded."
