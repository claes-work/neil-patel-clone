# How to Take Your Digital Marketing to The Next Level

## Summary

Five levers for competing as digital marketing gets more expensive. (1) **Leverage video**
— few do it well, and unlike Google SEO (which can take a year), **YouTube SEO can produce
results in under a week** if a video does well in its first 24 hours; upload natively to
each network (Facebook/LinkedIn penalize embedded/linked YouTube videos), and include
call-to-actions ("nothing wrong with self-promotion"). (2) **Create a funnel** with
upsells/downsells (SamCart, ClickFunnels) to raise revenue per visitor — essential because
ad costs rise yearly; he notes that even in the 2008 recession, with US house prices
falling 30–50%, Google still made more money from ads, showing digital marketing's
resilience. (3) **Guest post** — start on small sites, build a track record, and work up to
bigger sites that already hold your audience. (4) **Omnichannel approach** — single-tactic
businesses (like Facebook's original email-address-book invite growth) no longer work;
combine SEO, PPC, email, and growth hacking. (5) **Optimize for conversions** — Crazy Egg
(click maps + A/B tests), Hello Bar (email capture), Subscribers.com (push notifications)
to recapture the 98–99% who don't convert on first visit.
