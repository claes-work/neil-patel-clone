# How To Grow Your IG Followers Fast — Instagram Unlocked, Module 2, Lesson 2

## Summary
"Instagram Marketing Hacks" lesson, four hacks. (1) User engagement/community: the
algorithm bumps posts users interact with; Neil says he replies to every follower
comment he can and comments on competitor/industry-influencer profiles to boost his
own engagement, tracing the habit to his blog "over 13-ish years ago" and noting Gary
Vaynerchuk does the same; cites a claimed 3-5x higher long-run engagement from
comment-replying; also recommends a border around the profile image (says it increased
his own click-through), caption breakdowns for silent viewers, emoji use, and
all-hashtags.com for hashtag research. (2) Influencer marketing: cites "over 6 million
brands" using sponsored influencer posts; recommends niche micro-influencers over big
names; cites a 2018 stat (sourced to "status.com") that Instagram/Twitter influencer
engagement rates favored Instagram; lists influencer-vetting factors (follower
quality/engagement consistency, growth trend, publishing frequency, budget). (3)
Content-strategy hacks: repost/reuse top content across platforms; UGC; influencer
collabs; consistent posting (repeats the "at least once every two days" floor from the
prior lesson); newer formats to try — Stickers, Instagram challenges (a 30-day-photo-
challenge example), memes, AR filters (references Pokémon Go as a reference point for
AR). (4) Instagram Ads: says a business can start testing with $10-50; requires a
linked Facebook business page; points to a companion ad-setup resource at
`neilpatel.com/training` rather than covering ad mechanics in the video itself.
Closes with a "turning followers into customers" section: brand the profile as a
consistent-look "store" (cites Versace's logo-emblem consistency as an example),
tag products in posts, put the sales link in bio, use Stories swipe-up, run
follower-exclusive promotions, and keep replying to every comment.
