# My 7 Favorite Free SEO Tools to Get Page 1 Rankings on Google

## Summary
Solo tool round-up organized into three categories. **Keyword research**: (1) *FAQ Fox* —
built on his "think in problems, not keywords" framing (people search because they have an
unsolved problem: a new phone means dissatisfaction with the old one), it pulls the questions
people ask on a chosen site such as Reddit, Quora, Yahoo Answers or any industry site;
(2) *Ubersuggest Keyword Ideas* — he says the database recently expanded from simple seed
terms to over 1.2 million suggestions per keyword, with long-tails, comparisons, questions and
prepositions plus volume, paid/organic difficulty, ranking sites, backlink counts and social
shares. His favorite is the **Comparisons report**, because someone searching "Mailchimp versus
ConvertKit" has already decided to buy and is only choosing a vendor. **Link building**:
(3) *MozBar* — shows domain and page authority on any site you visit so you can tell which
link targets are worth pursuing (a high-authority relevant site beats "Joe the Plumber");
(4) *Ubersuggest Backlinks* — domain score, linking-URL authority, anchor text, daily link
gains and losses, nofollow vs. dofollow, first-seen and lost dates; he mainly uses it on
competitor URLs to find their most-linked pages, then creates similar content and pitches the
same linkers. **Technical SEO**: (5) *Panguin Tool* — Google makes roughly nine updates a day
(3,285 a year), and this tool overlays algorithm-update dates on your traffic so you can see
which update hit you instead of reading the whole SEO press; (6) *Structured Data Markup
Helper* — for rich snippets, star ratings, voice traffic and mobile; (7) *Ubersuggest SEO
Analyzer / Site Audit* — full crawl with load times, errors and recommendations ordered by
impact and ease, rescanned weekly with an email of new errors and fix tracking. Closes plugging
Neil Patel Digital.

## Key claims
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said he had been doing SEO for over sixteen years and that automating a big chunk of SEO work does not require expensive tools.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said most people think only in keywords instead of problems — a large portion of searches are people looking for solutions to a problem, not entertainment.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil recommended FAQ Fox for pulling the questions people ask on a specific site (Reddit, Quora, Yahoo Answers, or any industry site).
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said Ubersuggest had recently expanded from a simple seed-keyword tool to over 1.2 million suggestions per keyword, including long-tails, comparisons, questions and prepositions.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil named the Comparisons report his favorite Ubersuggest feature, because comparison searches ("Mailchimp versus ConvertKit") mean the searcher has already decided to purchase and is only choosing between solutions.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said Ubersuggest's keyword report also shows search volume, paid difficulty, organic difficulty, the ranking sites, their backlink counts and social shares.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said links from high-authority, relevant sites are worth far more than average links from low-quality sites, and recommended the MozBar to read domain and page authority while browsing so you know which targets to ignore.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil described Ubersuggest's Backlinks report as showing domain score, linking-URL authority, anchor text, link growth, daily new and lost links with exact dates, and nofollow vs. dofollow — enough to build a link "hitlist".
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said his main use of the Backlinks report is competitor URLs: find their most-linked popular pages, create similar content, then ask those same linkers to link to him.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said Google makes roughly nine updates a day — 3,285 in a year — and recommended the free Panguin Tool (a penguin/panda name mash-up) to map traffic drops or gains onto specific algorithm updates instead of reading articles across the web.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil repeated that over 50% of searches are now voice-based and recommended the Structured Data Markup Helper for rich snippets, star ratings, higher CTR and mobile optimization.
- As of 2019-12-16, Neil said Ubersuggest's Site Audit prioritizes fixes by both biggest traffic impact and easiest implementation, rescans every page weekly, emails new errors, and tracks fixes against traffic movement including during algorithm changes.
