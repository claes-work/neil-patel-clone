# 3 Email Marketing Hacks to Increase Your Click Through Rate and Boost Sales

## Summary
Neil addresses the situation where people open your emails but never click through to your site, and offers three hacks to lift email click-through rate. First, tell a story: stories are engaging and hook the reader (the same reason people watch soap operas or movies), so recipients read the whole email instead of skimming and are more likely to click. Second, include multiple links — the more links, the more clicks — but cap it at two to three, because throwing in 10-20 links risks landing in the spam folder; with two to three links there is a higher probability someone clicks at least one. Third, make the anchor text casual and benefit-driven: using a web page title as anchor text gets few clicks, while phrases like "follow these tips," "learn more here," or "follow these strategies" get more clicks because readers recognize the benefit. Following the three tips drives more clicks to your website, where visitors can convert into leads or sales.
