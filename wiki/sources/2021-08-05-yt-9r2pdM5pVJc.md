# 10 EASY SEO IDEAS That Will BLOW UP Your Traffic in 2023

*(Title says "2023"; content and internal references are dated 2021 — filed under
publish date per SUBJECT.md convention.)*

## Summary
A 10-item tactical SEO list, mostly reinforcing existing Ubersuggest-centric doctrine:
(1) combine near-duplicate pages via 301 redirect to the higher-authority URL rather
than letting them cannibalize each other; (2) delete underperforming pages, citing
**about.com** as a case study — they broke one mega-site with millions of thin pages
into smaller niche-focused sites and deleted large volumes of low-value content,
concentrating link authority on pages worth reading; (3) mine Google Ads copy for
title-tag ideas (appealing titles beat keyword-stuffed ones because higher CTR signals
relevance to Google); (4) build infographics from Ubersuggest's Content Ideas report
and pitch them to sites already linking to the source article; (5) turn top-performing
articles into YouTube videos (framed as "the second most popular search engine"),
keeping most videos around 10 minutes and avoiding very short (3-5 min) videos;
(6) internally link from strong pages to page-2/3 rankings to push them to page one;
(7) write "best X" list articles, but only if genuinely evaluative, not self-serving;
(8) implement AMP — reports it made little US difference but helped traffic in
translated regional markets like Brazil; (9) use structured markup to win SERP
snippets/star ratings for CTR; (10) use Ubersuggest's Backlink Opportunities report
(sites linking to 2-3+ named competitors but not you) to prioritize outreach targets.
