# 7 Actionable SEO Tips That Are Easy to Implement

## Summary
A tactical seven-tip SEO checklist framed around implementation speed rather than
strategy novelty. Tips: (1) run Ubersuggest's site-audit to find and prioritize SEO
errors by impact-vs-effort (fix easy/high-impact first); (2) add a free/cheap tool
(via CodeCanyon if a custom build isn't feasible) to your own site as a link magnet —
he cites neilpatel.com's Ubersuggest integration generating 24,000+ backlinks; (3) use
Google Search Console's year-over-year performance comparison to find declining pages
and rework them against current top-ranking competitors; (4) delete straight-up
irrelevant (not just outdated) old content and 301-redirect it, citing his own old
MySpace-era content as an example; (5) write on "Exploding Topics" (a tool by Brian
Dean) before they become competitive, but staying on-niche; (6) buy small, low-monetization
blogs in your niche that already rank well and merge their content into your site via
301 redirects; (7) merge near-duplicate pages on the same topic into one canonical page
to stop search engines splitting ranking signal between them (a lesson he says he
"learned the hard way").

## Key claims
- neilpatel.com's own Ubersuggest tool integration has generated 24,000+ backlinks (as
  of 2022-08-18, self-reported/tool-reported).
- Page-merging rationale: near-duplicate pages on one topic confuse search engines about
  which to rank; merge the lower-traffic page's content into the higher-traffic one and
  301-redirect (as of 2022-08-18, self-reported as learned "the hard way").
- Site-flipping tactic: acquire small, well-ranking, minimally-monetized niche blogs,
  merge content in, 301-redirect the old URL (as of 2022-08-18).
- Exploding Topics (Brian Dean's tool) recommended for early-trend content, with an
  explicit on-niche-relevance guardrail (as of 2022-08-18).
