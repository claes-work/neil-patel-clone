# How I Earn Passive Income Every Day with SEO - And You Can Too

## Summary
Two passive-income strategies. (1) **Tools**: build or buy small SEO/software tools
(Ubersuggest as the flagship example) and charge a monthly fee without heavily staffing
them — contrasted with the resourced, team-built version of Ubersuggest NP Digital
actually runs. Recommends **MicroAcquire** for buying small existing software/tools
cheaply, noting MicroAcquire's financing partnership with **Capchase**, which lets a
buyer acquire a tool with no money down (the acquisition is paid off out of the tool's
own passive income before ownership transfers) — Neil calls this his favorite
strategy. Also mentions **CodeCanyon** as a place to buy cheaper tool scripts to modify.
(2) **Courses**: cites his own discontinued **"Advanced Marketing Program"** — a
48-week, all-aspects-of-marketing course (SEO, PPC, CRO, etc.) that at peak generated
**~$600,000/month**, though he notes it wasn't truly passive (he ran ads and
continually revised/upsold it). Recommends platforms like Kajabi for a DIY course, or
listing on Udemy/Coursera at undercut pricing for a lower-effort few-thousand-to-$5,000/
month result. Frames both routes as an indirect SEO play — course marketplaces and
tool sites already rank well and can be link-building targets.
