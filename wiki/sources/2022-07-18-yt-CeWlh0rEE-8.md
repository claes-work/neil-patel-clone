# One Data Point That Will Change How You Do B2B Marketing

## Summary
Neil Patel ranks NP Digital's own top acquisition channels to make a case for B2B video marketing.
He states NP Digital has "700 plus employees globally" (2022-07-18) and that referrals are the
agency's #1 channel — via client word-of-mouth and via NP Digital employees who worked with a client
at a previous agency and get recruited back into the relationship. SEO is #2, driving "over 2
million visitors a month." The headline claim is #3: YouTube, which he says is under-used for B2B
despite being (per SimilarWeb) the second-most-used search engine in the world, and which drives NP
Digital "over 640,000 views a month, if not up to a million on a good month" — with the added benefit
that YouTube content can be repurposed across Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and TikTok.

## Key claims
- NP Digital has over 700 employees globally as of 2022-07-18 (self-reported; corpus previously
  recorded ~500–640-employee 2022 markers from earlier sources, consistent with continued 2022 growth
  — no contradiction, later date, higher count).
- NP Digital's #1 channel for new customers is referrals (client word-of-mouth, plus former-colleague
  recruitment via ex-employees at other agencies) (as of 2022-07-18, self-reported).
- NP Digital's #2 channel is SEO, driving "over 2 million visitors a month" (as of 2022-07-18,
  self-reported/unaudited).
- NP Digital's #3 channel for new *customers* (not just traffic) is YouTube, generating "over 640,000
  views a month, if not up to a million on a good month" (as of 2022-07-18, self-reported/unaudited).
- Cites SimilarWeb data that YouTube is the second-most-used search engine in the world, and argues
  YouTube is under-exploited for B2B and B2C marketing alike because it is comparatively
  uncompetitive (as of 2022-07-18).
- Recommends repurposing YouTube video content across Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, and
  TikTok to compound revenue from a single piece of content (as of 2022-07-18).

## Notable verbatim quotes

> It's not that competitive, there's not as many people trying to create content for YouTube, and I don't know why because it's an amazing channel.
