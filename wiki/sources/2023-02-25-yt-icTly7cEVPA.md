# My Ultimate (Quick) Traffic Hack

## Summary
Short tactical tip: append the current year to informational-page title tags (e.g.
"Beginner's Guide to SEO 2023") to signal freshness to Google and lift CTR, using SEO
plugins like All in One SEO or Yoast. Distinguishes evergreen-but-non-time-sensitive
topics (e.g. "how to tie a tie," "how to ride a bike") where a year suffix doesn't
help, from advanced/educational topics where searchers actively want the latest
version. Self-reports the tactic yields roughly 5–6% more traffic when done. Cautions
to remove stale in-body year references (e.g. leftover "2022" mentions once updating
to "2023") to avoid appearing deceptive. States he does this every year at year
boundary.

## Key claims
- Self-reports adding the current year to title tags yields roughly 5–6% more traffic
  (self-reported, dated 2023-02-25).
- States informational/educational content benefits from year-tagging more than
  timeless how-to content (paraphrase, dated 2023-02-25).
