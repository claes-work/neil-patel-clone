# What's are the best times to post on Instagram? 3 Tips For Maximizing Instagram Engagement!

## Summary
Neil Patel gives three tips for timing Instagram posts. First, sign up for Iconosquare, an Instagram analytics tool that visualizes which regions your followers come from, so you know which time zone to target. Second, post in the mornings of your followers' dominant time zone — he argues morning posts get more likes/comments than night posts because content posted at night goes stale by morning, and Instagram's algorithm then buries low-early-engagement posts in favor of fresh content. Third, actually understand and communicate with your audience rather than assuming: he tells a story about a model friend, Jessica, whose model followers sleep in, so she posts around 10–11am (right before they wake near noon) and gets more engagement than posting early. The lesson: time to the audience's real behavior, not just their geographic time zone.

## Key claims
