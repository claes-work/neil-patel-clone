# 7 Free Apps That'll Help You Write Content Faster

## Summary
A tools roundup for faster content writing, but the standout is a fully quantified
personal content-output breakdown he gives as context: ~12 YouTube videos/month, an
average of 5 Instagram posts/week (not counting Stories), an average of 5 TikTok
posts/week, one social (Facebook) post/day, 11 new blog posts as of recording (own
writing plus team translations into other languages — he notes he "really only"
speaks English), 5 LinkedIn posts/week, 2 Twitter posts/week, and one podcast episode
per day (~30/month) — totaling roughly 140 content pieces/month across platforms,
which he says would be "almost impossible" alone and requires a team for ideation,
structuring, and research so he can draft blog posts (typically Saturdays) "within a
few hours." Tool recommendations for people without a team: HyperWrite (AI paragraph/
sentence generation from a basic idea, with a Google Docs Chrome extension), Quillbot
(AI rewriting for tone/clarity), Frase.io (automated competitor-content analysis and
outline-building), Grammarly (grammar/error correction), Ubersuggest (his own product
— content-idea generation from keywords: trending pages, social traction, catchy
headlines, viral/link potential), Hemingway Editor (readability), and OneTab (browser
tab management for research).
