# 5 Free Resources to Learn Digital Marketing

## Summary
A resource-roundup video arguing you don't need a college degree or paid courses to learn digital
marketing. Neil lists five free resources: (1) his own Neil Patel Training Center
(neilpatel.com/training) — courses across SEO, all major social platforms, CRO, and email marketing,
with worksheets/cheat sheets/homework and direct Q&A access to his team; (2) HubSpot Academy — free
certifications spanning inbound marketing, social media marketing, general digital marketing, and
sales, positioned as résumé/LinkedIn-worthy credentials; (3) Backlinko (Brian Dean) — data-driven SEO
guides, citing a study of 3.6 billion articles (title-year inclusion and list/how-to formats
correlating with better performance) and a separate 208,000-webpage Core Web Vitals study (53% of
sites with a "good" LCP score, 46.23% "poor/needs improvement"; large layout shifts as the top cause
of poor CLS scores); (4) Search Engine Roundtable — aggregates algorithm-update data and community
before/after traffic reports to help identify patterns; (5) Mailchimp Marketing Library — broad
marketing education beyond just email, framed against the stat that 306 billion emails are sent/received
daily. Closes with the standard NP Digital pitch and a note that self-directed learning is fine too.

## Key claims
- Neil states Google makes "over eight algorithm changes per day on average" (as of 2022-06-18) — a
  figure close to, but not identical to, the "~9 algorithm changes per day" figure attributed to Neil
  elsewhere in the corpus (see [[2022-05-12-yt-PxUYlx4YZp8]]); flagged as a minor recurring-stat
  variance, not a hard contradiction.
- Backlinko (Brian Dean) cited as having analyzed 3.6 billion articles, finding that including the
  year in a title and using list-based or how-to formats correlates with better Google performance
  (as of 2022-06-18, third-party study, not Neil's own data).
- Backlinko also cited for a separate study of 208,000 webpages on Core Web Vitals: 53% of sites had a
  "good" Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) score, 46.23% had "poor/needs improvement"; large layout
  shifts were the top cause of poor Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS) scores (as of 2022-06-18,
  third-party study).
- Mailchimp Marketing Library cited alongside the stat that 306 billion emails are sent/received
  daily, as Neil's rationale for why email marketing remains a worthwhile, if "not sexy," skill (as
  of 2022-06-18).
- Neil Patel Training Center (neilpatel.com/training) described as covering SEO, all major social
  platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram), CRO, and email marketing, with worksheets, cheat
  sheets, homework assignments, and direct team Q&A (as of 2022-06-18).
