# Bulletproof Tactic to Dominate Google Search Rankings - How to Create Backlinks For SEO

## Summary
Neil explains **tier-two link building**: instead of getting more links to your own site, you build links to the pages that already link to you, raising *their* authority so the link they pass to you becomes more powerful. Workflow: use Ahrefs to list who links to you, sort by authority, find the pages linking to those authoritative sources' competitors, and email those webmasters suggesting your linking article (e.g. an Entrepreneur or Huffington Post piece) as an additional resource. He gives a reusable outreach email template and sets a 3–5 month expectation for the links to kick in.

## Key claims
As of 2017-07-19, Neil states:
- Rankings aren't just about how many links point to your site directly (as of 2017-07-19).
- **Tier-two links** = other websites linking to the sites that link to you; e.g. if entrepreneur.com links to neilpatel.com, people linking to that entrepreneur.com article help Neil's rankings (as of 2017-07-19).
- Mechanism: more links to the linking page tell Google that page/URL is important, raising its authority, which makes the link it passes to you more effective and boosts your rankings faster (as of 2017-07-19).
- Workflow: use Ahrefs.com to list all sites linking to you (sorted most-authoritative first); take the top ones, Google what they're about to find similar/competing pages, put those competing URLs in Ahrefs to see who links to them, then reach out to those webmasters (as of 2017-07-19).
- Outreach template: point a webmaster who links to one resource (e.g. a Huffington Post article on raising venture capital) to another article on the same topic (e.g. an Entrepreneur article) and ask them to link to it too if they think it's great (as of 2017-07-19).
- Timing: give tier-two links 3–5 months to kick in before rankings start rising (as of 2017-07-19).

## Notable verbatim quotes
> "Tier two links are other websites linking to the websites that are linking to you."

> "The more people that link to it, the higher authority and the more authority that page will receive. So then when that page, which is already linking to me, is there, that link will be more effective and it will help boost my rankings faster."

> "As you build these tier two links, give it three to five months for the links to kick in and then you'll notice that your rankings will start rising."
