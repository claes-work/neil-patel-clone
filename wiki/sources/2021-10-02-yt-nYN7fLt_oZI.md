## Summary
Names seven marketing skillsets he considers most valuable "in 2021 and the future":
big-data analytics (turning raw data into actionable decisions, not just slicing it);
business intelligence (tying KPIs to business growth, not vanity metrics); content
writing (dismisses AI/automation content tools of the era as "not amazing yet,"
framing creativity as priceless and recommending weekly writing practice to build the
skill); paid ads (the "easiest way to scale marketing," recommending Facebook's Ad
Library for competitive creative research); account management (working with agencies
as a valuable skill for in-house marketers at large companies); UX (defined narrowly as
a marketer's job to sequence copy/images so users get what they want fast — explicitly
not a design-team responsibility); and automation/AI/ML (framed as necessary to "get
more done with less people" under budget constraints).
