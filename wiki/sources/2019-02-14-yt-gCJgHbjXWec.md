# Get More Social Media Traffic Using These 7 Free Tools

## Summary

Seven free tools for squeezing more traffic out of social. (1) **Hootsuite** — schedule and
**re-share the same content ~6×/year** (roughly once every two months), which Neil says
yields 2–3× more traffic since most followers miss any single post. (2) **ManyChat** — a
Facebook Messenger chatbot whose open rates run above 60% and click-through above 30–50%,
beating email; visitors subscribe via Messenger and you push posts to them. (3) **BuzzSumo**
— find which topics are popular in your space and write/promote more of those. (4)
**SocialBlade** — beyond YouTube (Instagram, Twitter, etc.), track subscriber growth vs.
posting volume to find your optimal content cadence. (5) **Hello Bar** — collect emails so
that in a post's critical first hour you can email subscribers to like/share it and push it
toward virality. (6) **Subscribers** — same idea via browser push notifications. (7) **Zoho
Social** — team collaboration plus keyword research so you target the right keywords in
social titles/descriptions (many people search within Twitter and other platforms).
