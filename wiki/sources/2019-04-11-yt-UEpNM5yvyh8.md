# How to Spy on Your Competitors' SEO & Steal Their Traffic Through Competitor Analysis

## Summary

A step-by-step Ubersuggest walkthrough for competitor SEO analysis: (1) enter a competitor URL in
Ubersuggest (free) to see **traffic by region** (he notes even global businesses skew to a few
regions — e.g. Salesforce is strong in the US with **Japan as #2**); (2–3) open **Top Pages** to
see each page's social shares, backlinks, linking domains, ranking keywords, and CPC; (4) click
**View All** on keywords to find the **money keywords** — high traffic, high cost-per-click, low
SEO difficulty (high CPC signals converting traffic); repeat via the **Top Keywords** report and
the **Keyword Ideas** report (Google Suggest + AdWords related terms, with per-competitor share
data). Then don't just copy — apply **Brian Dean's Skyscraper Technique** to make content 10x
better so competitors won't dare copy you.

  cost-per-click, and low SEO difficulty** — high CPC signals the traffic converts, low difficulty

- As of 2019-04-11, Neil said to also mine the **Top Keywords** and **Keyword Ideas** reports

- As of 2019-04-11, Neil said not to merely copy competitors but to apply **Brian Dean's
  Skyscraper Technique** — build content "10 times better" (analogized to Dubai out-building
  everyone) so rivals won't dare copy you.
