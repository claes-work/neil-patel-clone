# Netflix's Genius Instagram Strategy

## Summary
Reacting to a viral tweet cataloging Netflix's large number of branded Instagram
accounts (split by country, genre, show), Neil frames "many branded accounts" as a
deliberate low-cost impression-multiplication strategy used by the biggest companies
(Netflix, Coke, Disney) and encourages smaller businesses to consider it too. Eric
adds a cross-promotion/"Instagram pod" angle: creators and businesses can grow fast by
having a network of accounts cross-promote each other via email/social, citing
accounts that jumped from a few thousand to ~200K followers this way. Neil notes his
own agency's clients (large multinational enterprises) run 20-40 Instagram accounts
each, largely as a byproduct of serial acquisitions rather than a designed strategy,
and adds that country-specific social profiles are also common among multinational
clients operating in many languages/markets (with sanctioned countries like Iran
excluded).
