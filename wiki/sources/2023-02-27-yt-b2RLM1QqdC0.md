# 7 Tools to Write Content Faster

## Summary
Tool-roundup video, seven tools he self-reports using for content production. (1)
AnswerThePublic — surfaces real-time search-query data from a seed keyword; uses his
own "digital marketing"/"ad agency" example. (2) Ubersuggest's AI writer — for
writing new content or refreshing existing pages; repeats the Wikipedia-freshness
rationale from other videos. (3) VistaCreate — templated social-graphic creation;
self-reports it lets his team skip hiring a dedicated designer ($4–6K/month) with no
noticeable engagement difference vs. custom design. (4) Grammarly — self-reports
having "poor grammar" and using it across email, email blasts, blog posts, and
LinkedIn posts; free. (5) Lumen5 — turns blog posts into videos; cites 10 million+
videos created on the platform across industries. (6) Portent's Content Idea
Generator — for blog-title/topic generation; self-reports using it for "seven or
eight years." (7) Hemingway App — grades writing-level; explicit stance against
using sophisticated vocabulary ("most people didn't go to Harvard"), aiming for
content a "kindergartner" can understand.

## Key claims
- Cites Lumen5 has been used to create 10 million+ videos (as cited, dated
  2023-02-27).
- Self-reports using VistaCreate templates in place of a dedicated designer,
  estimating $4,000–6,000/month in avoided headcount cost with no noticeable
  engagement difference (self-reported, dated 2023-02-27).
- Self-reports using Portent's Content Idea Generator continuously for "seven or eight
  years" (self-reported, dated 2023-02-27).
- States an explicit content-simplicity philosophy: write for a level a
  "kindergartner" can understand rather than to impress an educated audience
  (self-reported belief, dated 2023-02-27).

## Notable verbatim quotes
