# The Quickest Way to Create Popular Web Pages (And Get Tons of Traffic)

## Summary
Solo screen-share tutorial demoing Ubersuggest's **Top Pages by Traffic** report as a
competitor-page-cloning workflow. Method: enter a competitor's domain (demoed with
moz.com and wordstream.com against his own NP Digital), review their highest-traffic
pages by monthly traffic/authority, note which ones are non-blog assets (e.g., a "free
tools" roundup page, a "top 500 most popular websites" list) that can be replicated
without heavy original content, then click through to see every keyword each page ranks
for to reverse-engineer the on-page keyword set. Also demoed: switching the report's
region selector (India, Great Britain, etc.) to replicate the same competitor-mining
workflow for non-US markets. Frames this as a standing internal practice: "I give this
to my teams... we focus on creating webpages that are super popular on our competition
versus just creating whatever we want."
