# 11 USEFUL Digital Marketing Tools When You Have No Team

## Summary
A tool-recommendation roundup aimed at solo marketers/entrepreneurs without a team,
framed with a personal anecdote about his own early-career grind: manually copying
and pasting data into spreadsheets for hours per day before tools existed, and the
realization that paying people to do purely manual work "is a poor use of money."
Recommends: Madgicx (AI-driven paid-ad campaign optimization), AnswerThePublic
(search-intent/question discovery, one of his own owned products), Unsplash (free
stock images), Hunter (email-finding for outreach/link building, free tier up to 25
emails/month via Chrome extension), Google Data Studio (free unified analytics
dashboards for client reporting with live access), a cluster of AI-assisted SEO
content tools (Frase.io, MarketMuse, Clearscope, SEO Scout, Grammarly), and the
Ubersuggest Chrome Extension (his own product, in-context SEO data on Google search
and competitor sites).

## Key claims
- Self-reports that early in his career he spent up to five hours a day manually
  copying/pasting data into spreadsheets before adequate tools existed (self-reported

- States a general operating principle: don't pay people to perform purely manual

  dated 2022-03-19) — agency-entrepreneurship.
