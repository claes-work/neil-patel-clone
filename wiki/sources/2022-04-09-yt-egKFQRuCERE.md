# 8 Ways to Increase Your Website Traffic FAST and FOR FREE

## Summary
A list of eight consistency-dependent, free traffic tactics, framed against a claim
that his own site draws on 139 distinct traffic sources beyond Google (his stated
main source). The eight: (1) answer questions on Quora and link out to relevant
site articles, no spam/affiliate links; (2) build infographics in Canva from
competitors' most-linked data pages (found via Ubersuggest), credit the source,
then use Ubersuggest's backlinks report to find everyone who linked to the
original and pitch them the infographic; (3) participate in industry Facebook
groups, adding value before ever linking out; (4) use Instagram Stories'
swipe-up/link feature to promote posts, products, and podcasts; (5) multistream
live sessions across YouTube, LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook via Restream to
plug products/promotions; (6) publish multi-page PDFs as LinkedIn carousels to
build engagement and followers, then promote articles/products to that audience;
(7) engage on Twitter (comments on viral posts) despite it not being a large direct
traffic driver, citing its high-caliber B2B audience; (8) release a free tool on
one's own site, sourced/white-labeled from marketplaces like CodeCanyon, as a
compounding traffic asset.
