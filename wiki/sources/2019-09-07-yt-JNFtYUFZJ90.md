# How to Build Links Without Trying to Build Links (Get Links Organically)

## Summary

Neil's thesis: the best links are the ones you never asked for, earned by **giving away
something valuable for free** that people link to naturally. His flagship example is
**Ubersuggest** — a free version of Ahrefs/SEMrush/Moz that has organically earned tens of
thousands of links; he "disrupted the market" by giving away for free what competitors
charge for, and now gets links monthly without outreach. Tactics to replicate it on a
budget: (1) **give away a self-assessment/quiz tool** (LeadQuizzes) — quizzes attract
links; (2) **create infographics** from already-popular articles (via **Infogram** or
**Canva**) and tell everyone who linked to the original that a visual version exists — they
embed it and link back; (3) **build an Ubersuggest-style tool cheaply** using pre-made
scripts from **codecanyon.net** (or Googling scripts for any niche) for a small one-time
fee — this is literally how Neil started, buying a sub-$100 SEO script that became his
most-visited page; (4) **promote relentlessly** (Product Hunt, Twitter, Facebook,
Instagram, video, going live) so the tool gets users, word-of-mouth, and organic links.

## Key claims

- As of 2019-09-07, Neil said **promotion is mandatory** even for link-earning: push new
  features on Product Hunt and social to create noise, users, and organic links.
