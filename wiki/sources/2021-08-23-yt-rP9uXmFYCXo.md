# 7 SEO Secrets Every Successful Online Business Employs

## Summary
A seven-point SEO tactical rundown, all reinforcing doctrine already in the wiki
rather than introducing new frameworks. (1) **Topic clusters** — the Moz "Beginner's
Guide to SEO" example (already cited in the wiki) as thorough topical coverage beating
standalone-keyword targeting. (2) **Internal linking** — link every new article back
to at least three older related pieces. (3) **Outsmart competitors** via Ubersuggest's
Top Pages report (find high-traffic, low-backlink competitor pages) combined with
Brian Dean's **Skyscraper Technique**. (4) **Advanced/thorough guides** — the
Wikipedia continuously-updated-thoroughness example (repeat citation). (5) **Avoid
content cannibalization** via Google Search Console + 301 redirects, consolidating
internal links to the surviving URL. (6) **Boost brand queries** — repeat and
reinforce the "tell people to Google your brand" doctrine across ads/podcasts/
interviews/social bios. (7) **Target low-hanging-fruit keywords** via Ubersuggest's
"keywords I can rank for" filtered report.

## Key claims
- Reinforces the topic-cluster/thoroughness doctrine (Moz example) and the
  Skyscraper Technique (credited to Brian Dean) as complementary tactics — paraphrase,
  dated 2021-08 — seo. No new framework; consistent with prior sources.
- Reinforces the brand-query doctrine: instructing people to search a brand name
  across every channel (ads, interviews, social bios, giveaways) compounds long-run
  rankings — paraphrase, dated 2021-08 — seo.
- Describes the **Ubersuggest Backlinks Opportunity report** workflow in more detail:
  feed in several competitors' URLs plus your own to find sites linking to competitors
  but not to you (paraphrase, dated 2021-08) — seo.
