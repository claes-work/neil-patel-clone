# 8 Things That Are RUINING Your eCommerce Sales

## Summary
A tactical checklist video listing eight common eCommerce conversion mistakes and their fixes. Neil
Patel frames eCommerce as a domain where best practices are already well-tested, so store owners don't
need to guess. The eight mistakes, each with a cited stat or mechanism: (1) not optimizing for mobile —
cites Otterbox that 79% of smartphone users purchased something online in the first month of 2021, and
80% of shoppers use a phone in-store to check reviews/prices/locations; (2) low-quality product photos —
cites Shopify data that only 0.52% of consumers want a single photo vs. 33.16% wanting to browse
multiple photos; (3) not leveraging social proof/reviews — 93% of consumers say reviews influence
purchase decisions, and negative reviews should be used to improve the product rather than argued with;
(4) not staying in touch with customers/leads — pushes SMS marketing (98% open rates) and chat platforms
(WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger) alongside email as under-used retention channels; (5) failing to improve
average order value (AOV) — illustrates with a worked example (3 customers at $20 each costs more to
acquire than 1 customer at $60) and recommends upsells/downsells and automation to raise AOV, noting
rising Google Ads costs even through the 2008 recession and COVID; (6) bad product descriptions — sell
the transformational benefit, not just features; (7) poor on-site search — results should reflect
on-site behavior, plus autocomplete; (8) complex checkout — recommends adding PayPal (and Shopify's
ShopPay) as options, claiming eCommerce tests show roughly an 18% revenue increase when PayPal is
offered as a checkout option. Closes with the standard NP Digital agency pitch.

## Key claims
- 79% of smartphone users purchased something online at least once in the first month of 2021, per
  Otterbox; 80% of shoppers use a phone in-store to research products (as of 2022-06-23, citing external
  data).
- Only 0.52% of consumers prefer a single product photo vs. 33.16% preferring to browse multiple
  photos, per Shopify studies (as of 2022-06-23, citing external data).
- 93% of consumers say online reviews influence their purchasing decisions (as of 2022-06-23, citing
  external data); negative reviews should inform product improvement rather than be disputed.
- SMS marketing has ~98% open rates and is under-used relative to email in eCommerce; chat platforms
  (WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger) add personalization (as of 2022-06-23).
- Raising average order value is cheaper than acquiring more customers at a lower AOV — worked example:
  3 customers × $20 costs more to acquire than 1 customer × $60 (as of 2022-06-23).
- Google Ads costs have "continually gone up," including through the 2008 recession and COVID, reinforcing
  the case for AOV/automation over pure acquisition spend (as of 2022-06-23, self-reported observation).
- Offering PayPal as a checkout option is associated with roughly an 18% revenue increase, per Neil's own
  agency testing across eCommerce clients (as of 2022-06-23, self-reported/unaudited).
