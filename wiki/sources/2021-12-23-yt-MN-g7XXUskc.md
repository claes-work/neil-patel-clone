# A Minimalist's Approach to SEO: The Only 3 Things You Should do Every Week To Get Traffic

## Summary
Prioritizes a 3-part weekly SEO routine for time-constrained site owners, out of
Google's "200+" ranking factors: (1) update existing content — find near-miss keywords
via Google Search Console (high impressions, low clicks/rank), fold them into the page
and title/meta; delete genuinely underperforming pages (301-redirected to a related
article) even if they have backlinks, since links don't guarantee reader value or
ranking; consolidate multiple overlapping articles on the same topic into one
canonical page (301-redirect + internal-link cleanup) to stop sending Google
"conflicting signals" that cause rank flip-flopping; and refresh declining-traffic
articles by studying who now outranks them. (2) Competitive analysis via Ubersuggest's
"competing domains" report — surfaces keyword gaps (terms competitors rank for that you
don't) and competitors' top pages for new content ideas. (3) New content built around
content clusters/keyword groups the site has near-term potential to rank for (checked
via Ubersuggest's domain-filtered Keyword Ideas report), explicitly deprioritizing
high-difficulty terms (credit cards, online gambling, SEO) until the site has enough
authority, in favor of realistic near-term targets — same niche-authority-clustering
doctrine as prior videos, restated with the domain-filtered-keyword-ideas mechanic.

## Key claims
- Frames content consolidation (merging overlapping same-topic articles into one
  canonical page via 301 redirects + internal-link updates) as necessary to prevent
  "conflicting signals" that cause a site's own pages to flip-flop in rank against each
  other for the same keyword (self-reported SEO mechanic, dated 2021-12-23) — seo. More
  procedurally detailed than prior general consolidation mentions.
- States that backlinks to a page do not guarantee it deserves to rank or that readers
  value it — explicitly permits deleting content with existing links if it
  underperforms on engagement/traffic (self-reported opinion, dated 2021-12-23) — seo,
  content-marketing.
- Restates the domain-filtered Keyword Ideas Ubersuggest workflow (enter your own URL to
  filter keyword suggestions down to realistically-rankable terms) as the mechanism
  behind the niche-authority/content-cluster doctrine, explicitly naming high-difficulty
  terms (credit cards, online gambling) as ones to defer until a site has more authority
  (self-reported tactical framework, dated 2021-12-23) — seo.
