# How to Use Instagram Stories To Promote Your Business | 3 Instagram Marketing Tips!

## Summary
Neil explains how businesses can promote themselves using Instagram Stories, which he argues now reach more users than Snapchat. He walks through three tactics: building anticipation by teasing upcoming content and directing viewers to a website, storytelling so that individual story frames flow together and hold attention, and showcasing products, services, and client results visually. Neil frames these tactics as ways to grow followers and fans who are then more likely to buy when products or services are promoted.

## Key claims
As of 2017-10-08, Neil states:
- More people use Instagram Stories than Snapchat, making Instagram the more popular platform for stories (as of 2017-10-08).
- Businesses should build anticipation in stories by teasing upcoming footage or interviews and telling viewers to check the website where the content will be posted, which drives more traffic and repeat visitors (as of 2017-10-08).
- Stories should be told with a deliberate narrative where each frame flows into the next, rather than random pictures and videos, because coherent stories keep viewers watching longer (as of 2017-10-08).
- Sustained storytelling grows followers and fans, who are then more likely to buy when you promote your products or services (as of 2017-10-08).
- Businesses should use stories to visually showcase their products, services, and the results clients achieved, since the format is inherently visual (as of 2017-10-08).
