# The Biggest Opportunity in Paid Social Media Ads Since The Death of Organic Reach

## Summary
Argues personalized/segmented retargeting is the biggest underused opportunity in
paid social. Cites stats (We Are Social's Oct-2020 snapshot: 4.14B monthly social
users, 53% global penetration; Sprout Social: 57% of consumers follow brands to learn
about products; Influencer Hub: 91% of consumers visit a brand's site/app after
following it). Recommends segmenting ad creative by funnel stage (checkout vs.
landing vs. homepage visitors), by viewed product/page, and by "next step" messaging.
Gives concrete NP Digital examples: a humorous retargeting video for
neilpatel.com/ConsultingLeadGeneration non-converters (a "team of Neil Patels" ad,
quoted in full), and (in the info-product era, "we don't sell info products now")
remarketing case-study videos referencing a consulting.com/Sam Ovens style program.
Recommends warming cold traffic with content before pitching, then remarketing paid
customers with upsells, and combining personalization with humor (cites Squatty
Potty / Harmon Brothers-style ads) as his best-performing pattern this year.
