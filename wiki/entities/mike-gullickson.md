# Mike Gullickson

> ⚠️ **CONTEXT PAGE — NOT THE SUBJECT.** Mike Gullickson is documented here only as context for
> Neil Patel's story. Nothing on this page trains `persona/` files; **his statements must never be
> attributed to Neil Patel.**

Named by Neil (2022-01-03) as **NP Digital's CEO**. 🗣️ Prior roles cited, in order: operations
at **iCrossing**; **CEO of Covario** (acquired by iProspect/Dentsu); **president of iProspect** —
before joining NP Digital. 🗣️ ([[../sources/2022-01-03-yt-VS4ECrG_0uM]])
