# Marketing School (podcast)

Neil Patel's flagship audio property — a **daily, ~10-minute marketing podcast** co-hosted with
**[[eric-siu]]** (founder of Single Grain / Leveling Up). One of the longest-running daily
marketing shows; a core piece of Neil's audience-building engine.

## Facts [📰/🗣️]

- **Format:** daily, ~10 minutes, tactical marketing/SEO/growth tips drawn from the two hosts'
  own businesses. 📰 (podcasts.apple.com/.../id1138869817)
- **Launched ~2016-08-26** (Episode #1). 📰 (levelingup.com; Apple Podcasts).
- **Co-hosts:** Neil Patel ([[np-digital]]) and **[[eric-siu]]** (Single Grain / Leveling Up).
- **Scale:** reported **60M+ downloads**; consistently among top business podcasts. 📰/🗣️
- Distributed on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and a **Marketing School YouTube channel**
  (@marketingschoolpod). 📰

## Role in Neil's world

The podcast pairs Neil's SEO/agency perspective with Eric Siu's agency/growth perspective,
reinforcing both hosts' personal brands and funneling audiences to their respective businesses
([[np-digital]] / [[ubersuggest]] for Neil; Single Grain / Leveling Up for Siu).
