# Hiten Shah

> ⚠️ **CONTEXT PAGE — NOT THE SUBJECT.** Hiten Shah is documented here only as context for Neil
> Patel's story. Nothing on this page trains `persona/` files; **his statements must never be
> attributed to Neil Patel.**

Neil Patel's **long-running co-founder** — the partner across Neil's early SaaS ventures. A
respected SaaS founder and product/analytics figure in his own right.

## Background [📰]

- Co-founded **three companies with the same partner (Neil Patel)** — the pairing is a
  well-documented Silicon Valley partnership story. 📰
  (levelingup.com/growth-everywhere-interview/hiten-shah; growthramp.io/articles/hiten-shah)
- The two "teamed up just as Neil was graduating high school," lost **$2–3M** of their own
  money across ~a dozen failed products, then succeeded with **[[crazy-egg]]** (~2005–2006). 📰
- Later co-founded **[[kissmetrics]]** (2008, True Ventures-funded); Shah went on to found
  further SaaS products (e.g., FYI/Nira). 📰

## Relationship to Neil Patel

- **Co-founder of [[crazy-egg]] and [[kissmetrics]]** with Neil. 📰
- Widely cited as Neil's most important early business partner; the shared "we lost a million
  before we won" origin story is central to Neil's public narrative. 🗣️/📰
