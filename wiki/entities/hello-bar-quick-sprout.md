# Hello Bar & Quick Sprout

Two smaller Neil Patel web properties, combined here. **Quick Sprout** is his early personal
SEO/marketing brand; **Hello Bar** is a lead-capture widget acquired by the Crazy Egg team.

## Quick Sprout (~2007–) [🗣️/📰]

- Neil's **personal SEO/marketing blog and consulting brand**, launched **~2007** — the
  platform that first built his personal brand and funneled consulting clients. 🗣️/📰
  (grokipedia; aggregators quoting his about page).
- Later expanded into paid courses/community (e.g., "Quick Sprout University" / traffic
  courses) and remains a content property in his orbit alongside neilpatel.com. 🗣️
- Predecessor, in spirit, to the neilpatel.com blog + [[ubersuggest]] content funnel.

## Hello Bar (~2011/2012–) [🗣️/📰]

- A lightweight **website notification / call-to-action bar** for email capture and
  lead generation (a top-of-page CTA widget). 🗣️/📰
- **Acquired by Crazy Egg / Neil Patel ~2012.** 📰
  (yourtechstory.com; eaglestalent.com)
  > ⚠️ Created-vs-acquired nuance: some bios treat Hello Bar as a 2010–2012 creation, others
  > as a 2012 acquisition by [[crazy-egg]]. Best reading: **acquired ~2012**, then run as a
  > Neil Patel property. Medium confidence.
